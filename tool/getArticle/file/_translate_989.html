It couldn’t be easier to play Bingo for fun!

　　In classic Bingo at the ohmybingo website, you either make or receive a five by five （or larger for some games） grid which usually has a free space in the middle. That free space means you are already starting to win! The game can take a couple turns from here, but the object of the game is always to mark off five squares in a row （if that is the size of your grid） either vertically, horizontally, or diagonally. Go ahead and mark your free square, because you already got that one. The letters BINGO are printed at the top of the grid, and then numbers are filled in all but the free square. A caller then says a letter and a number. If you have that number within that column, you get to mark that square off. For example, B 47 would mean that somewhere in the column below the B on your Bingo card, you can mark off the number 47 if you have it. Because Bingo is so easy to play, bingo halls are a great place to socialize while playing.

　　Travel Bingo is a little different. When you take the kids on a long road trip, have them play Bingo for fun. They get a grid of five by five or larger, and instead of numbers their grids have different object that they might see on the road. These can be written in or they can be drawn for kids who can’t read yet. It’s a great way to keep the kids entertained and interacting.You can also play Bingo online on various websites, which allows you to play Bingo for fun where ever you are! Websites all over the web have different options and types of Bingo games for every level of player and for every type of Bingo game.

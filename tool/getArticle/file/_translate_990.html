澳大利亚赌场指南

作者: 博之道 来源: 未知 时间: 2011-10-24 阅读: 次 博彩译文

　　澳大利亚是一个为赌博而产生的国家，那里有许多世界级的赌场，在那里，你会感到宾至如归。

　　与亚洲相比，当谈到赌场，你会发现澳大利亚体现更多的是欧洲模式。现在各大城市都拥有一个赌场，但同时每个城市也只有一个赌场。有些人认为，这种缺乏竞争的做法并不是一件好事，但事实上，那里的赌场很大，安全性很好，普遍都具有良好的运行机制和豪华的场地，会照顾到每一个层次的玩家。绝大多数的赌场还提供了大量远离赌场的旅游景点。

　　以下是澳大利亚的一些著名赌场：

　　星城

　　星城是位于悉尼的赌场。恐怕在世界上的任何地方，你再也找不到一个像星城那样风景如画的赌场。赌场位于城市中部，你可以看到悉尼港和澳大利亚最具代表性的景象之一悉尼海港大桥这些美妙的景色。这就很容易让每个人都知道它处于什么位置。

　　它的所有设施，包括有一个大型的扑克室和一个新的体育酒吧，有来自世界各地票数很高的体育游戏。那里有一流的餐饮，展览馆和酒吧，是城市最好的五星级酒店之一。你从赌场出发，只需坐渡轮，公共汽车或火车，就可很快到达到著名的唐人街，达令港（Darling Harbour），环形码头（Circular Quay）这些世界上最好的城市海滩地区。

　　悉尼是一个世界级的赌场城市，而多年来，星城赌城也一直不断改善其服务。

　　堪培拉赌场

　　堪培拉是澳大利亚的首都，位于悉尼和墨尔本这澳大利亚最大的两个城市之间。堪培拉是一个小城市，人口约50万。这个城市不是旅游的圣地，但它的小赌场有着梦幻般的安静，这是一个干净而有安全感的城市。

　　堪培拉赌场很有欧洲的感觉，能够提供优质的服务。相信你最喜欢的游戏都可以在这里找到，它们也可以说是在全国内最古老的棋牌室。如果你抢到一台主机，并玩更大的赌注，你将会得到很好的服务。

　　堪培拉有一些很好的旅馆和餐馆，它们也是一个放松身心的好去处。如果您在游戏期间想了解一些文化的东西那里将会提供多种选择。这座城市的特色景点有国家战争纪念馆，澳大利亚的国会大厦，国家美术馆和博物馆。

　　阿德莱德赌场

　　阿德莱德处于澳大利亚州南海岸，是一座教会城市。它拥有美丽的花园，功能齐全，是澳洲最古老的赌场，也是这座城市的心脏之一。

　　中赌场建在老火车站，它会给你一个独特的感受。其特点是，有你所能想打的所有桌上游戏。它起于20世纪80年代，是一个历史悠久的扑克室。在21世纪，扑克室被关闭了几年，但它现在比以往任何时候都要发展得更大、更好。

　　阿德莱德的主赌场是一个很棒的地方。您可以发现这里建造成椭圆形的形状，有着被许多人认为是世界上最美丽的图案的蟋蟀地面。而从赌场到一些世界上最好的的酒厂仅一箭之遥，那里生产的葡萄酒，有奔富格兰奇和亨施克格雷斯山。在夜间和白天你都可以去酒厂看看，没有什么比这更享受的了。

　　Treasury赌场

　　Treasury赌场位于布里斯班，是昆士兰州省会城市的心脏。它在1995年开业，开始时是一间酒店，有着5间餐厅，七个酒吧和夜总会，现在成为澳大利亚最庞大的文物建筑群之一。

　　赌场分三个层次，有80张赌桌，超过1300台的赌博机。赌场有两个VIP区，提供更为宽松和时尚的氛围。

　　在该地区，有众多高端时尚商店和设计师的精品店，或者你也可以直接到出口购物中心参观。为什么不考虑一下坐着一个游船看看南岸的人造沙滩？

　　木星

　　位于昆士兰州黄金海岸的木星酒店和赌场，到世界著名的冲浪者天堂海滩只需一个很短的车程。昆士兰州的这个区域有风景如画的海岸线，那里有水晶般清澈的海水和30公里金色的白色沙滩。

　　木星提供了70多桌牌游戏和超过1300台赌博机。主要的游戏楼层都在一个楼层上，在楼上有一个贵宾室。扑克室最近经过重新装修，现在是澳大利亚最优质的扑克场地之一。

　　有7个餐厅，5个酒吧和超过600间客房和套房。现在计划花费350万澳元的财产将其打造成一个世界级的休闲和娱乐场所。

　　如果想到其它地方玩，您可以参观众多的主题公园，每天前往附近的山区，或者去海边晒晒太阳。

　　皇冠赌场

　　皇冠赌场是世界上最大的的赌场之一，坐落在墨尔本的心脏Yarra河银行附近。皇冠赌场很有欧洲的感觉，如果你喜欢精致的餐饮，咖啡馆和很浓的文化气息，你就来对地方了。

　　娱乐场博彩空间是很大的，可以很容易地找到空闲的赌桌。如果你想玩高的限制，那里有许多私人博彩区。如果你打算在这些地方玩牌的话，你需要确保你所说的话能够被听到。

　　皇冠是全国最大的扑克室之一。该地区最大的扑克锦标赛都在这里举行，每年一月，澳洲上百万家庭都会到那里参加。在那里有三个墨尔本最好的酒店，许多颇受好评的餐馆和所有城市的奇迹都会展现在你的面前。

　　A Guide to Australian Casinos

　　Australia is a country born on gambling and there are many world-class venues where you will feel right at home.

　　Compared to Asia, Australia has followed a more European model when it comes to casinos. Every large city now boasts a casino, but there is only one per city. Some argue this lack of competition is not a good thing, but the upshot is the casinos are large, safe, generally well-run and opulent venues that will look after every level of player. Most also offer plenty of attractions away from the tables.

　　Here is a showcase of the best of Australia's  casinos

　　Star City

　　Star City is Sydney's casino. You won't find a more picturesque setting for a casino anywhere in the world. Located in Pyrmont in the central city you can expect wonderful views of Sydney Harbour and one of Australia's most iconic images: the Sydney Harbour Bridge. It's easy to get to and everyone knows where it is.

　　It features all the games you have come to love along with a large poker room and a new sports bar that shows sport from all over the world in cinema quality seating. Great dining, shows and bars along with one of the city's finest five star hotels are just a few of its attractions. You are a short stroll from the famous areas of Chinatown, Darling Harbour, Circular Quay and The Rocks and a short ferry, bus or train ride to some of the best city beaches in the world.

　　Sydney is a world-class city with a world-class casino. Star City has consistently improved its services over the years.

　　Casino Canberra

　　Canberra is Australia's capital and lies between Sydney and Melbourne – Australia's two largest cities. It's a small city with a population of around half a million. The city is not on the popular tourist track but it is a quiet, clean and safe city with a fantastic little casino.

　　Casino Canberra is very European in its feel and offers a boutique service. All of your favorite games are here and they also have arguably the oldest card room in the country. If you want to grab a host and play larger stakes you will be well looked after.

　　Canberra has some great hotels and restaurants and it is always a relaxing place to visit. If you are looking for a bit of culture with your gaming there are plenty of options. The city features the National War Memorial, Australia's Parliament House and the National Art Gallery and Museum.

　　Adelaide Casino

　　Adelaide is on the coast of the state of South Australia, and is known as the city of churches. It has beautiful gardens and features one of Australia's oldest casinos right in the heart of the city.

　　The medium-sized casino was built in an old railway station, giving it a unique feel. It features all of the table games you could ever want to play. It also has a great poker room that originally opened in the 1980s. The poker room closed its doors for a few years in the 2000s but it's now back bigger and better than ever before.

　　Off the table there are a host of wonderful things to do in Adelaide. You must check out the famous Adelaide Oval, which is considered by many to be the most picturesque cricket ground in the world. You are a stone's throw from some of the world's best wineries, producing wines such as Penfolds Grange Hermitage and Henschke Hill of Grace. Gambling at night and visiting wineries by day – it doesn't get any better than that.

　　Treasury Casino

　　Treasury Casino is located in the heart of Brisbane, the capital city of the state of Queensland. It opened its doors in 1995 and houses a hotel, five restaurants, seven bars and a nightclub, all in one of Australia's grandest heritage buildings.

　　The three-level gaming emporium has 80 gaming tables and over 1,300 gaming machines. The casino has two VIP areas offering a more relaxed and stylish atmosphere.

　　There is plenty to do away from the tables. There are numerous high-end fashion shops and designer boutiques in the area or you can take a tour of the direct outlet shopping centres. Why not take a river cruise or visit the man-made beach at Southbank?

　　Jupiters

　　Jupiters hotel and casino is situated on Queensland's Gold Coast and is only a short drive from the world famous Surfers Paradise beach. This area of Queensland has over 30km of picturesque coastline offering crystal clear waters and golden white beaches.

　　Jupiters offers more than 70 table games and over 1,300 gaming machines. The main gaming floor is all on one level, and there is an upstairs VIP room. The poker room has recently been renovated and is now one of Australia's finest poker venues.

　　The complex has seven restaurants, five bars and over 600 guest rooms and suites. There are plans to spend AU$350 million on redevelopment transforming the property into a world-class leisure and entertainment destination.

　　Need a rest from the tables? You can choose to visit one of the many theme parks, take a day trip to the nearby mountains or maybe go to the beach and soak up the sun.

　　Crown Casino

　　Crown Casino is one of the largest casinos in the world and is situated on the banks of the Yarra river in the heart of Melbourne. Crown Casino （and Melbourne in general） has a very European feel and if you like fine dining, cafés and culture you have come to the right place.

　　The casino gaming floor is huge and it's easy to find a table. Many private gaming areas are available if you play high limits. Make sure you speak to a host as soon as you arrive if you intend to play in these areas.

　　The Crown poker room is the largest in the country and lays claim to being one of the largest in the world with over 50 permanent tables. It is also home of the region's biggest poker tournament, the Aussie Millions, held in January every year. It has three of Melbourne's best hotels on site, plenty of acclaimed restaurants and all of the wonders of the city are right at your doorstep.

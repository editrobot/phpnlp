常见的在线篮球投注类型

作者: 博之道 来源: 未知 时间: 2011-12-01 阅读: 次 博彩译文

　　对于一个新手来说，确定一个篮球投注方案是相当困难的，但如果你了解了正确的投注方案，你一定会取得成功。

　　单一号码下注，主要是指在每场比赛中下注，或者针对一个特定的匹配结果来下注。如果你认为弱旅赢就可以直接在此下注。当然，您也可以选择直接压钱的投注方式。这是简单、明了的投注形式，但也有较高的风险，因为你在受欢迎的比赛一方投注比较多，但此时，却又很难把握是否弱旅会赢得更多的利润。

　　另一种投注叫超额投注或简单地称为总注。当你进行打赌，目的就在于推测分数是高于还是低于你的预测数。在比赛结束的时候，就进行总比分投注。两队的分数将被合并，作为最后的数字。你可以打赌总分是在你设定的分数以上或者以下。而最后会根据你打赌的状况以及比赛的实际总分来判定。如果赌注是与实际上的总分一致，则为平局，投注者的赌注全部返还玩家。

　　在线篮球博彩下注的另一种形式是parlays，也被称为特别投注。主要是赌这场比赛会持续多少场比赛。

　　Common online basketball betting optionsMaking a basketball bet can be quite difficult for a newbie but if you understand the right betting options, you will definitely be successful.

　　With the Straight bet, you are betting mainly per game or for the results of a particular match. It is your call if you think the underdog will win or the Favorite. You can also opt to make a bet on the money line. It is simple, straightforward but there are higher risks for you since you have to wager a lot more for a favorite while it can be difficult to actually determine if the underdog is a worthy option for bigger profits.

　　Another kind of bet is the over-under or simply known as totals. The premise is that you are betting if the score is over or under a predicted number. You are wagering on the total score when the game ends. The scores of the two teams will be combined and that serves as the final number. That means if you bet over, you believe that the score is above the total. The reverse is for under where you bet that the score is under the total. If the bet is actually exactly as the total, then it is a tie and is considered a push and the money of the bettor will be given back.

　　Another form of wager for online basketball betting is the parlays & teasers. They are also known as exotic bets. In such a bet, you have to bet on several games.

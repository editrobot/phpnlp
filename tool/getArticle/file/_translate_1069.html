G2E展销会上ATRONIC与SPIELO姊妹公司游戏产品系列

作者: 博之道 来源: 未知 时间: 2011-10-26 阅读: 次 博彩译文

　　在2011年G2E展销会上的最大新闻将是ATRONIC和SPIELO这两个组织会合并在一起，只是差一个总的公司名称尚未公布。

　　这两个姊妹公司，由意大利游戏业巨头Lottomatica监督管理，在过去的几年，已经朝着游戏功能大集成方向迅速发展。2011年国际博彩博览会将标志着该组织作为一个完全集成的实体走在一起，现在它们拥有一个共享的出色的老虎机产品展销和与客户亲密接触的机会。

　　这次它们有着新的使命，ATRONIC和SPIELO公司将展示并测试一款新的游戏，为市场提供组合产品系列，今天的玩家马上就可以享受到，而未来的玩家则需排着队慢慢的等待。

　　一掷千金Join'N是此次在展会上的特色产品。这是一个链接社区的游戏，玩一掷千金Join'N游戏会提供一个基于时间的社区奖金给合格玩家。此外还有新的功能，一掷千金Join'N游戏设置有第六届卷轴“倍增器”，可以通过最大的赌注来激活豪伊对塞和三个基地游戏的神秘奖金。

　　3排金刚步进游戏会为玩家带来新的激情，目的是吸引玩家。其著名的电影怪物，是依靠新买进的一个多层次功能选项驱动。这个庞大的游戏设有ATRONIC奖金，此外还包括一个有限制的奖金，用于鼓励继续玩游戏的玩家。

　　ATRONIC和SPIELO最新的核心游戏组合系列有新的发展。在狂野的争吵中，通过“极端堆栈”卷轴的243种方式可以赢取分散奖金，可能还会获得大的胜利。

　　Goldify是一个新的可设定游戏，玩家可以根据自己的经验选择游戏类型 - 即便他们没有学习任何规则一样玩游戏。玩家通过触摸任何一个在屏幕上的符号，不管是什么，只要变成黄金，就算取得5X线游戏的胜利。

　　具有较高命中率的是独立游戏，可以通过增加赌注来获取获胜的机会。Tiki奖金的回升奖是通过2X，3X，5X的逐步乘法器来设置，而“新经典”基地游戏奖金的设置会让玩家获得更多的乐趣。

　　Tourna Master为运营商提供更多的灵活性，按下一个按钮开关就可以进入主游戏锦标赛。prodiGi游戏中，在第二个液晶屏幕中会显示一些玩家的名字，排名，和领先的总积分榜单。

　　译文

　　The biggest news expected from ATRONIC and SPIELO at G2E 2011 will be their new, unified corporate identity – a name, yet to be announced, that will encompass both organizations.

　　The sister companies, overseen by Italian gaming giant Lottomatica, have been moving toward functional integration for the past few years. However, G2E 2011 will mark the organization’s “coming out” as a fully integrated entity -- one that now possesses a shared, laser-like focus on exceptional content and customer intimacy.

　　Under their new mission and moniker, ATRONIC and SPIELO will be showing a combination of new games and tested, market-available products that today’s players can enjoy now, and that future players will be lining up to play.

　　Deal Or No Deal Join’N Play is among the market-ready products to be featured at the show. A fully-themed linked community game, Deal Or No Deal Join’N Play offers a time-based Community Bonus that’s guaranteed for qualified players. Rich with new features, Deal Or No Deal Join’N Play also includes a “multiplier” sixth reel, the max-bet-activated Howie Jackpot, and three base game Mystery Bonuses.

　　The new Passion 3-Reel King Kong stepper game is designed to attract players with its famous cinematic monster, a new buy-a-feature option and multi-level progressives. This gargantuan game is based on ATRONIC’s proven bonus-within-a-bonus concept, and includes a binding bonus that encourages extended play.

　　ATRONIC’s and SPIELO’s latest core games mix new developments with simplicity. In Wild Wild Wranglers, the “Extreme Stacks” stacked reels combined with 243 Ways to Win scatter prize can lead to big wins and bigger excitement.

　　Goldify is a new customizable game where players choose their experience and volatility-- and they don’t have to learn any rules. Players touch any one onscreen symbol, regardless of its value, and it turns to gold, awarding 5X the line wins.

　　A standalone progressive with high hit frequency, Cash Eruption targets progressive “shoppers” who can increase their chances of winning with bigger bets. A Pick-a-Prize Tiki bonus reveals frequent progressive multipliers from 2X, 3X, or 5X, while “new classic” base game bonuses add entertainment value.

　　TournaMaster gives operators the flexibility to switch from a main game to a tournament with the push of a button. Available on the prodiGi Vu cabinet, the second screen LCD shows individual player name, ranking, and total points, which are also displayed on a leaderboard.

你不可不知的百家乐策略

作者: 博之道 来源: 未知 时间: 2011-10-09 阅读: 次 博彩译文

　　玩真钱百家乐始终是一种赌博，但… …

　　当然，你的父母总是告诫你不要玩赌场游戏。“所有的都是要靠运气，你无法控制游戏，所以要远离它”总是相同的听得发腻的观点。那其实，你的父母并不完全正确。在任何赌场，在任何赌桌中的游戏都有运气的成分，不过，运气也往往只是起到推波助澜的作用，甚至增强的赔率和概率规则的一个基本的了解也会有助于你赢钱。也就是说，下一次你想要到外面走走，享受一些在法国的里维埃拉的异国情调，想象自己是詹姆斯·邦德，你也没有必要因做不到而感到遗憾。尤其是因为网络的发达，将允许你在家里做你的白日梦。只要确保你读了无敌百家乐战略，知识总是不负有心人的，你知道，“知识就是力量”。有时，陈词滥调恰好是正确的。

　　基本百家乐战略

　　规则依然就是规：学习的游戏规则。虽然在大多数的纸牌游戏中，牌面有较高的值，但在百家乐国王，例如，有一个0值。确保您对规则有所了解，在发挥前钻研制胜的游戏战略。这是一个非常基本的，但非常重要的概念。

　　一旦你理解了基本规则，是时候开始思考赔率和概率。例如，你可知道，庄家与玩家赢钱的概率吗？此外，还有赢得庄家的赌注，对玩家来说，或和局，都有不同的赔付率。投注和局的中奖赢钱是8:1，虽然它很少发生。往往是庄家会赢得赌注。决定在哪里下注时，请记住这一点。

　　请记住，没有万无一失的技巧能够在每一场每百家乐比赛中赢钱，跟踪的货币和财政是绝对必要的。掂量您的情况，决定花多少，你能负担得起和在什么时候应该停止投注。百家乐本质上是一种充满着运气的游戏，但请记住，当你赌博时，能够享受到乐趣那才是幸运的。

　　Playing Baccarat for Real Money is always a Gamble, but…

　　Your parents always warned you about playing casino games, of course. “It’s all about luck, you can’t control the game at all, stay away from it” were always the gist of the same old lecture. Well, your parents weren’t exactly correct. In any casino – in any table game – there is an element of luck. However, that luck can often be helped along, and maybe even augmented, with a basic understanding of the rules of odds and probability. That is, the next time you want to go out and imagine yourself James Bond in some exotic locale in the French Riviera, you’ll have no need to feel guilty – especially since your Internet connection will allow you to live your daydreams from home. Just make sure you read up on Baccarat strategy first – knowledge always pays off; you know, “knowledge is power” and all that. Sometimes that cliché just happens to be correct.

　　Basic Baccarat Strategy

　　Rule #1 is Rule #1: learn the rules of the game. Though in most card games, face cards have higher values, in Baccarat the king, for example, has a value of 0. Make sure that you understand how to play the game before delving into the strategy for winning it. This is a very basic, yet very important, concept.

　　Once you understand the basic rules, it is time to begin to think about odds and probabilities. For example, did you know that the Banker’s hand wins just slightly more often than the Player’s hand? Also, there are different payout rates for winning bets on the Banker’s hand, on the Player’s hand, or on a tie. A winning bet on a tied hand pays out 8:1, although it happens very seldom. There is often a commission for a winning bet on the Banker’s hand as well. Keep this in mind when deciding where to place your bets.

　　Keeping in mind that there is no surefire tip to win every Baccarat hand, keeping track of one’s money and finances is absolutely essential. Evaluate your situation, decide how much you can afford to spend and at what point to stop playing. Baccarat is essentially a game of chance, but keep in mind that what matters when you gamble is getting lucky and having fun.

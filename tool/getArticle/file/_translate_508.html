何时投注美式足球

作者: 博之道 来源: 未知 时间: 2011-09-29 阅读: 次 博彩译文

　　博彩可以是一个相当有趣的过程。如果您押注在一场比赛上，那么，比赛还未开始之前，你就需要等待。即使你得到了很好的赔率，你也需要等待，需要比在开始比赛之前等待更长的时间。因为打赌的时间很长，所以风险很大。

　　如果你在比赛开始的前一年就开始投注，你很有可能得到很好的赔率，但你需要坚持到比赛结束，如果你的投注是对的话。投注美式足球可以是有趣的、持续的、长期的赌注。当然，这是一个很大的风险，因为你不能在早期做出很好的预测。但是，如果你投注的那支球队赢得了比赛，你就可以获得大量的资金。这就是投注足球的魅力所在。

　　何时投注赌注取决于您是否要考虑和计算风险，或者只是猜测和希望，考虑和计算风险是很有意义的。您也可以从计算风险中找到更多的乐趣，因为你需要做很多准备工作。预测谁可能赢是投注的前提，但目前为止，预测也是处于在黑暗中的，因此猜测可能是错误的。尽管如此，提前做好猜测，依然会是一个帮助你赢钱的好方法，它可以能更好地增加您获胜的机会。

　　When to Place an American Football Bet

　　Betting can be quite an interesting process. If you bet a long time in advance of a match you can get much better odds than if you wait until just before it starts. This is because the risk is so much greater if you bet a long time away.

　　If you bet a year before the Super Bowl you are likely to get much better odds than if you wait until just before the final. It can be fun having a bet which lasts that long, thinking all season whether the team you predicted to win seem to be in a good enough shape to do so. Of course, it is a great risk to take because you cannot make a good prediction that far in advance. However, if that team did go on to win, you could make a lot of money from it. This is also the case for any game really, the closer to the actual start time, the lower the risk of the bet and the less money you are likely to win, should the team you bet on, win.

　　When to place the bet really depends on whether you want to take a calculated risk or just make a guess and hope that it is worthwhile. You may also find calculated risks more fun as you have to do a lot of work in predicting who might win, whereas betting so far in advance would just be a stab in the dark and therefore a guess. If you are no good at accessing the risk and working out who might win, then perhaps making a guess well in advance is a good way to go. You may end up winning more money that way. However, if you are good at predictions, once yo have all the facts and it is nearer to the actual game, then it could be better to increase your chance of winning but stand to win less money.

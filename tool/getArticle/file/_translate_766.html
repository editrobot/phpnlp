百家乐的基础知识

作者: 博之道 来源: 未知 时间: 2011-10-17 阅读: 次 博彩译文

　　百家乐是澳门的比赛，所有的游戏超过80％是百家乐。在这里我们希望对大家有帮助，所以即使你们中的大多数人都知道如何玩游戏，我们还是认为我们可能需要五分钟，因为你们这些人可能从来没有在玩之前看过非常基本的游戏世界和游戏杂志。

　　百家乐，和轮盘赌一样，已成为一个非常受欢迎的游戏，因为其非常简单。在百家乐中有三种投注方式：庄家，玩家和和局。一些赌场提供的另一边的赌注，但这些都是在游戏中最近的事态发展，并为他们的赌徒提供非常坏的赔率。像所有的游戏一样，赌场保留一个以上的玩家，统计学帮助他们取得了很长一段时间的赌场优势。在百家乐，玩家占赌场优势的1.24％，1.06％的属于庄家和和局的可能是（这些数字包括打赢庄家投注支付的5％）14.36％。这实际上意味着，如果你坚持，只是在庄家或玩家投注而不是投在和局上，你只需支付极少量的佣金。获奖归结为在某一天你是多么幸运。

　　其他重要的一点是关于百家乐的赌徒没有受过像他在21点或扑克游戏中的影响。该卡是按随机顺序处理。这实际上使得百家乐赌徒玩一场伟大的比赛，因为没有任何外界因素的影响，可以使胜负之间产生差异。在挤压百家乐的时候，使玩家最大的赌注或庄家获取开牌的荣誉。门外汉可能看起来这样像是决定了牌应该如何发挥。对于玩家来说，可以算是添加了更令人兴奋的游戏，他们选择的方式是在开放的牌卡上。毫无疑问，这是游戏的一部分，它使游戏有了更多的乐趣，但游戏的完整性始终是保持不变的，因为他们有时只增加了“战区”游戏。

　　在百家乐游戏中的幻数是九，九是最好的的数字，而零（或如果你喜欢什么，这是“百家乐”法语单词）是最坏的牌点。玩家和庄家各领取两张牌。他们每个人都增加了他们的总数。如果总数超过九点，后来干脆成了10点，并不断下降，直到总9个或更少。图片卡都是零值，或如果你喜欢10，像在21点游戏中。所以举例来说，一个国王和一个6会让你共有6或16点，它会自动为你忽略10。如果你得到一个9和7，你总共有16点，但是实际上成为6点。

　　百家乐增牌规则

　　百家乐是一个很简单的游戏，赌注是放在后才定局。规则是自动完成的。这意味着，在赌桌上的荷官的行为，可直接根据这些规则而不用咨询玩家。

　　任何百家乐牌点可以有最高的总数九点。一个两卡共九点被称为“自然”，稳赚不赔。一个8是第二个最好的牌点，也被称为自然。如果既当玩家和庄家有相同的牌点时，这是一个和局，既不是庄家获胜，也不玩家获胜。

　　无论是玩家还是庄家的手中将完成两个或三个牌卡。荷官把两个牌卡发到每一方（玩家和庄家）。下面的规则解释是否双方或获得第三张牌在各收到两张牌之后。

　　如果玩家或庄家共8或9点，他们都主张。有没有例外，此规则将覆盖所有其他规则。

　　如果玩家有6或7，玩家增牌。

　　如果玩家共5点或更少，玩家会自动点击（收到第三张牌）。

　　如果玩家的看台上，庄家共5个或更少的点击。

　　如果玩家得到的第三张牌，然后庄家拿第三张牌，按照以下规则：

　　庄家总额为0，1，2，庄家拿第三张牌。

　　庄家共3：如果玩家的第三张牌是1-2-3-4-5-6-7-9-0（除8），庄家增牌。

　　庄家共4：如果玩家的第三张牌是2-3-4-5-6-7，庄家增牌。

　　庄家共5：如果玩家的第三张牌是4-5-6-7，庄家增牌。

　　庄家共6：如果玩家的第三张牌是6或7，庄家增牌。

　　Baccarat: The Basics

　　Jan/Feb 2010

　　Baccarat is the game of Macau. Over 80% of all gaming action is on baccarat. Here at World Gaming magazine we like to help everyone, so even though most of you know how to play the game, we thought we might take five minutes to explain the very basics of the game to those of you who may never have played it before.

　　Baccarat, like roulette, has become a very popular game due to its simplicity. In baccarat there are three betting options: the banker, the player and the draw. Some casinos offer other side bets, but these are more recent developments in the game and offer the gambler very bad odds for their money. Like all games of chance, the casino retains an edge over the player that statistically helps them win over a long period of time. In baccarat, the house edge is 1.24% on the player, 1.06% on the banker and 14.36% on the tie （these figures include the 5% paid on winning banker bets）。 This actually means that if you stick to betting on just the player or the banker and forget about the tie, you are only paying a very small amount of commission. The winning and the losing comes down to how lucky you are on any given day.

　　The other important point about baccarat is that the gambler has no influence over the game like he does in blackjack or poker. The cards are dealt in a random order. This actually makes baccarat a great game to play for the gambler, because there are no outside influences that can make the difference between winning and losing. In squeeze baccarat, the person who makes the largest bet on the player or the bank is given the honour of opening the cards. To the uninitiated it may look like the person is making decisions on how the hand should be played. This is not the case; the player is simply allowed to add more excitement to the game by opening the cards in a manner that they choose. Make no mistake, this is part of the game that makes it so much fun; but the integrity of the game always stays the same, and the ‘drivers’ as they are sometimes called are only there to increase the ‘theatre’ of the game.

　　The magic number in the game of baccarat is nine – nine being the best, and zero （or if you prefer the French word for nothing, which is ‘baccarat’） being the worst. The player and the dealer are given two cards each. Each of them adds up their total. If the total is over nine, then simply drop off ten, and keep dropping off ten until the total is nine or less. Picture cards carry a value of zero, or if you prefer 10 – like in blackjack. So for example, a King and a 6 will give you a total of 6 or 16, which automatically becomes 6 as you ignore the 10. If you get a 9 and a 7, you have a total of 16 again, which becomes 6. An Ace always counts as 1, so a 7 and an Ace would total 8.

　　Baccarat Drawing Rules

　　Baccarat is a very simple game to play with no decisions made after a bet is placed. The rules are automatic. It means that the Dealer at the table acts according to these rules without consulting players.

　　The highest total any baccarat hand can have is nine. A two-card total of nine is called a ‘natural’ and cannot lose. An 8 is the second-best hand and is also called a natural. If both player and bank are dealt identical hands, it is a standoff （a tie） and neither bank nor player wins.

　　Both the player’s and the banker’s hands will finish with either two or three cards each. The dealer gives two cards each to each side （the player and the banker）。 The rules below explain whether the sides （player and banker） receive a third card or not. After receiving two cards each:

　　If either the player or the banker has a total of an 8 or a 9 they both stand. There are no exceptions and this rule overrides all other rules.

　　If the player has total of 6 or 7, the player stands.

　　If the player has total of 5 or less, the player automatically hits （receiving a third card）。

　　If the player stands, the banker hits on a total of 5 or less.

　　If the player gets the third card then the banker draws a third card according to the following rules:

　　Banker has total of 0, 1, 2: Banker always draws a third card

　　Banker has total of 3: Banker draws if Player’s Third Card is 1-2-3-4-5-6-7-9-0 （everything except an 8）

　　Banker has total of 4: Banker draws if Player’s Third Card is 2-3-4-5-6-7

　　Banker has total of 5: Banker draws if Player’s Third Card is 4-5-6-7

　　Banker has total of 6: Banker draws if Player’s Third Card is 6 or 7

　　Banker has total of 7: Banker always stands

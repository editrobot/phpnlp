全明星赛临近 MLB投注赔率发生变化

作者: 博之道 来源: 时间: 2012-06-30 阅读: 次 博彩译文

　　全明星赛是职业棒球大联盟本赛季的终极衡量时间，全明星赛给各个球队一个机会去消化前半个赛季的比赛，总结经验，查找不足，估量一下是不是能走上通往冠军的道路，同时也是买家在7月底投注截止期限前出手的时间。

　　在今年大多数球队将有一些小小的不同，那就是每一个联盟增加的外卡淘汰赛，很多球队可能会发现他们因为淘汰赛遇到只有几场比赛的情况，这也会改变球队在最后期限前交易的方式。

　　“我们已经看到还有四分之三的联盟还处在淘汰赛的争夺中”博彩分析师布拉德。道金斯说“在这种情况下，球队是不太可能解散队伍并开始为下个赛季组队，只有为数不多的有影响的球员可用会给买家创造一个机会”

　　前半个赛季在积分榜的前列也出现了一些惊喜，联盟中最大的惊喜可能是华盛顿国民队，这只自从他们搬到华盛顿就掉落到最后一名的球队，国民队保持着国联东区的领先地位，他们有一个投手并且分析家们相信国民队可以在这个赛季走的更远。

　　在西部，洛杉矶道奇队也给他们的球迷们带来了一些令人兴奋的事儿，道奇队在国联西区领先，领先于投注者最青睐的亚利桑那响尾蛇队和旧金山巨人队。道奇队已经成为给本赛季初不被看好的投注者带来欣喜感觉的球队中的一个。

　　在美国联盟，投注在德克萨斯游骑兵和洋基队是各自类别的最爱，游骑兵有棒球上最好的进攻，并且已经显示出其对在投手丘上的达比修有的帮助。

　　洋基队在过去的两个月中在东部排名中迅速提升到首位，红袜队也开始在自己的分区有所作为，以胜率超过50%的投注在周日攀升到赛季最高的四场比赛。

　　在美国联盟最热的投注对象是底特律老虎队，开赛后，老虎队一直在大部分的上半场拼搏着，只有最近老虎队才开始在克利夫兰印第安人队领先的充满惊喜的赛区出彩，经过几场比赛的良好发挥，周日公布的美联中区排名中，老虎队仅落后于克利夫兰印第安人队。

　　MLB Betting Odds Shiftling As All-Star Game Approaches

　　The All-Star game is the ultimate measuring stick time of the season for Major League Baseball teams. The All-Star game gives teams a chance to digest the first half of the season, and assess whether or not they should make a title run and be buyers at the trade deadline later in July.

　　This year will be a little different for most teams. With an additional wild card playoff team added to each league, more teams may find themselves in a situation where they are only a couple of games out of the playoffs, and that could alter the way teams do business at the deadline.

　　"Already, we see three-quarters of the league still in playoff contention," says Gaming Analyst Brad Dawkins. "With that being the case, teams are going to be less likely to dismantle their teams and start building for next season. What it is going to create is an opportunity for the buyers, with only a handful of impact players available."

　　The first half of the season has offered some surprises at the top of the standings. The biggest surprise in the league may be the Washington Nationals, a team that has been mired in last place since they moved to Washington. The Nationals hold the National League East lead, and they have a pitching staff that has analysts believing the Nationals could be around for the long haul this season.

　　Out West, the Los Angeles Dodgers have also provided some excitement for their fans. The Dodgers are leading the NL West, holding leads over the betting favorite Arizona Diamondbacks and San Francisco Giants. The Dodgers have been one of the teams that have provided underdog gamblers with a sense of joy early this season.

　　In the American League, betting favorites Texas and the Yankees are at the top of their respective divisions. The Rangers have the best offense in baseball, and have been helped on the mound by Yu Darvish.

　　The Yankees used a surge over the past two months to claim the top spot in the East. The Red Sox have also started to make their move in the division, climbing to a season high four games above .500 on Sunday.

　　The largest betting favorite in the American league coming into the season was the Detroit Tigers. After starting on fire, the Tigers have struggled for much of the first half. Only recently have they started to make up ground on the surprising division-leading Cleveland Indians. The Tigers entered play on Sunday just a couple of games behind Cleveland in the AL Central.

胡安・卡洛斯莫特森―赌桌上的斗牛士

作者: 博之道 来源: 未知 时间: 2011-10-13 阅读: 次 博彩译文

　　胡安·卡洛斯莫特森，也称为“斗牛士”，是拉丁美洲最成功的职业扑克代表。是所有获得最金额的赢家之一，卡洛斯莫特森不仅是赢得世界扑克系列赛主赛事最后的职业扑克选手，而且赢得一贯以来扑克的最大奖。

　　扑克名人卡洛斯莫特森及其职业

　　卡洛斯莫特森他在西班牙的时候，扑克游戏在这一个国家不仅不受欢迎，实际上是非法的。他第一次接触与游戏是在1997年，在一个私人俱乐部中，在那里，他从事一个调酒师工作，并从一开始就被证明是一个非常有天赋的玩家。

　　在他发现他的特别才能后，卡洛斯莫特森转移到美国发展，开始了作为职业玩家的生涯。他参加的第一个世界系列扑克比赛是在1999年，虽然发生了景况不佳的事件，但是仍然在现金游戏中获取了一些令人印象深刻的胜利。

　　次年，他显著提高了玩牌技能，最终在2000年世界系列扑克的主要赛事中胜出。他的献身精神和毅力终于在2001年带来了回报，当他再回到了世界扑克系列赛，在这次重要你的比赛中赢得1,500,000美元的奖金。

　　卡洛斯莫特森在2003年赢得了他的第二个WSOP手镯，并且赢得第34届世界扑克系列限制保持“EM事件”。在2006年，他再次表现出色，在三项赛事的决赛桌上，为自己赢得除去支付款项外的超过25万美元的奖金。

　　除了他在世界扑克系列赛的成功，卡洛斯莫特森也是公认的世界扑克巡回赛上最成功的玩家，曾获得两次世界扑克巡回赛的冠军。他也是唯一一个同时在世界扑克系列赛和世界扑克巡回赛胜利的玩家。

　　卡洛斯莫特森简介：玩牌风格

　　卡洛斯莫特森不紧不慢，超进取的玩法风格表现在他同时代的扑克巡回赛上。他，不是依赖于一个冒险的，有所松动的游戏，而是虚张声势，并放在一起翻牌手。

　　虽然莫滕森因他的冷漠态度而闻名，但他确实是玩家所的喜爱的，他的能力尽展在赌桌，他给别人心中树立了一个光辉的形象。

　　Carlos Mortensen

　　Juan Carlos Mortensen, also known as ‘the Matador’， is Latin America’s most successful representative on the professional poker circuit. One of the biggest money winners of all time, Carlos Mortensen was not only the last professional poker player to win the main event at the World Series of Poker, but has continued winning consistently since taking poker’s biggest prize.

　　Carlos Mortensen Poker Celebrity & Career

　　Carlos Mortensen picked up his poker game in Spain, a country where poker is not only unpopular, but virtually illegal. He first came in contact with the game in 1997 at a private club where he worked as a bartender, and from the start proved to be an exceptionally talented player.

　　Having found his true calling Carlos Mortensen moved to the United States to develop his career as a professional player. He played in his first World Series of Poker event in 1999, faring poorly at the event, but managing to pick up some impressive wins in cash games.

　　He was back the following year, putting in a markedly improved performance to finish at the final table of the 2000 World Series of Poker main event. His dedication and persistence finally brought rewards in 2001 when he returned to the World Series of Poker, this time winning the main event to pocket the $1,500,000 prize.

　　Carlos Mortensen claimed his second WSOP bracelet in 2003, winning the Limit Hold ‘em event at the 34th Annual World Series of Poker. In 2006 he performed well again, making the final table in three events and earning himself a tidy pay-check of over $250,000.

　　Apart from his success at the World Series of Poker, Carlos Mortensen is also recognised as the most successful player on the World Poker Tour, having won the main event at the World Poker Tour on two occasions. He is also the only player ever to have won the main events at both the World Series of Poker and World Poker Tour.

　　Carlos Mortensen Profile: Playing Style

　　Carlos Mortensen does not go for the tight, hyper-aggressive playing style of many of his contemporaries on the poker tour. He instead relies on an adventurous, somewhat loose game that allows him to bluff and put together good hands on the flop.

　　Whilst Mortensen is known for his cool demeanour, he is loved by fans for his ability to put together complex chip-stack sculptures on the table as he plays.

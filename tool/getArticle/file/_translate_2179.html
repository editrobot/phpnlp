Bwin第三季度收入增长显著

作者: 博之道 来源: 未知 时间: 2011-11-28 阅读: 次 博彩译文

　　Bwin最近发布了一个官方数据，由于在意大利推出新的二十一点游戏，其第三季度收入总计有显着增加。Bwin公司拥有网上赌场俱乐部，宾果和扑克等赌场游戏品牌。该公司并宣布，第三季度净营业收入同比增长百分之三。

　　该公司表示，收入的增加主要是由于新游戏，如在意大利推出的21点和轮盘赌。早在7月，意大利政府开辟了这样的游戏市场，这有助于该公司在第三季度增加他们的收入。

　　财务总监马丁Weigold针对该公司的净收入增加显著提出解释：“我们预计我们业务增长会存在一定弹性，因为我们今天所处的时代环境还是金融危机时期。已经有一些市场受到金融危机的冲击，也许我们这个行业也不能幸免。但我们在希腊的业务增长情况比较例外。然而，尽管我们在希腊的业务比上一季度有所增长，但如果与去同季情况比较，其实已经下降了两个百分点。”

　　Bwin平均每日收入比上一季度的增加8％，这是一个显著的增加速度。该公司目前正在积极寻找德国市场，如果他们能够获得德国的经营许可证，将会更大幅度增加他们公司的纯收入。德国在几个月前通过了一部赌博法案，这部赌博法案向全世界的赌徒表示出一种友好的态度，因此Bwin公司希望能尽快进入德国网络赌场市场。

　　译文：

　　Bwin Party Digital recently released their third quarter revenues and the totals have increased significantly thanks to new games such as blackjack being launched in Italy. The company owns such brands as Bwin, Party Casino, Foxy Bingo and Party Poker, and announced that net revenues grew three percent in the third quarter.

　　The company stated that the increase in revenues was due to the new games such as blackjack and roulette which were launched in Italy. Back in July, the Italian government opened up the market for such games and this helped the company increase their revenues in the third quarter.

　　Martin Weigold the Chief Financial Officer for the company commented on the increase in net revenues by stating: “We expected our business to be resilient but not immune to the recession, and I think that’s where we are today. There are certain markets that have been particularly hit by the financial…for us Greece is that one that stands out. However, even Greece shows quarter-on-quarter growth, but if you looked at the year-on-year movement, we have seen a double-digit decline.”

　　Bwin saw an increase in the average daily revenues by eight percent from the last quarter which is a significant increase. The company is now looking to move into the German states if they are able to secure a license. Laws were passed just a few months ago in Germany, which are gambler friendly, so the company is hoping to get the ball rolling and move into the market soon.

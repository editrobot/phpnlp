堪萨斯检察官希望立法机构改变针对慈善扑克的法律

作者: 博之道 来源: 时间: 2012-08-17 阅读: 次 博彩译文

　　堪萨斯州正在开始一项监管赌场博彩的实质性尝试，这将改变当地一些慈善机构和执法部门官员的观点，一些慈善机构将因为违反州法律组织慈善扑克赛事而受到审查。

　　但是并不是每一个克萨斯人都为法律禁止慈善机构以此种途径募集资金而感到高兴，这些人当中就包括芬尼县的检察官约翰。惠勒，他是负责起诉类似犯罪的。

　　惠勒在接受花园城电讯报采访时说“现在有团队或是组织为很多人提供一些需要的东西以及为慈善事业募集资金，我们却把它称之为犯罪，这在我看来是矛盾的，相反，我希望立法机关能看看这些年来他们所作出的贡献”

　　惠勒对待博彩的态度和目前时期很多州的态度是有相似之处的，博彩业税收已经成为州议会弥补财政预算缺口的一个普遍途径，堪萨斯在几年前就是这些州中的一个了，并且是其中第一个对博彩采取监管的州。

　　而慈善机构组织的慈善博彩在很大程度上对博彩监管的发展造成了影响，立法者和博彩官员们希望所有的赌金在赌场内受监控的流通，一些州已经开始着手打击非法的慈善扑克比赛。

　　慈善扑克赛事甚至没有受到传统扑克赛事的影响，在堪萨斯以及全美各地，慈善扑克赛事成为募集慈善资金的普遍方法。机车手获得赞助，出发开始各种各样的寻宝游戏，在各个目标区找到卡片，在行程的终点，他们聚集在集合地点，谁收集到最佳扑克手卡片的就是赢家。

　　慈善扑克赛事在堪萨斯是非法的，在其它几个州慈善赌博也被认为是违法法律规定的，但惠勒在堪萨斯有大量的支持者来改变这一法律，议员们表示他们很快就会考虑这个问题。

　　Kansas Prosecutor Hoping Legislature Changes Poker Charity Law

　　The state of Kansas is starting to get their first real taste of regulated casino gambling, and that has changed the perspective of some local charities and law enforcement officials. Charities have come under scrutiny for breaking Kansas law by organizing charity poker events.

　　Not everyone in Kansas is happy that the law prohibits charities from raising valuable money for their cause. Among those that are not pleased is Finney County prosecutor John Wheeler, who is responsible for prosecuting these crimes when assigned.

　　"We have a group or organization that sponsors something that's fun for a lot of people and raises money for a charity, and we call it a crime," said Wheeler, to the Garden City Telegram. "And it just seems to me to be contradictory, contrary, and I wish the Legislature would take a look at it after all these years."

　　Wheeler's approach towards gambling is similar to the one taken in many states these days. Casinos have become a popular avenue to pursue for state legislature's that are facing budget shortcomings. Kansas was one of those states several years ago, becoming one of the first in the current group of states that are regulating casinos.

　　The charities have largely been the groups affected negatively by the creation of regulated casinos. Lawmakers and gaming officials have wanted all gaming money to go through the casinos, and many states have started to crack down on illegal charity poker games.

　　It has not even been traditional poker events that have suffered. In Kansas, and around the US, poker runs have become popular for raising money. Motorcycle riders gain sponsors, and then set out on a scavenger hunt of sorts, picking up cards at various locations in the event area. At the end of the run, the bikers meet in one centralized location, and the player with the best poker hand from the cards they collected, is the winner.

　　This event is illegal in Kansas and several other states where charity gambling is considered against the law. Wheeler has plenty of support in Kansas to have the current laws changed, and lawmakers have stated that they may soon look into the issue.

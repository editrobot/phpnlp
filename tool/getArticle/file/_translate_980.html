博彩中赔率的重要性

作者: 博之道 来源: 未知 时间: 2011-10-22 阅读: 次 博彩译文

　　博彩赔率才是赌博的本质。他们就像你想购买商品的价格。那么，你会有兴趣在寻找销售店的时候货比三家，以获得一些折扣吗？当然，你会这样做。这就是为什么赔率对于投注者来说它的意义是重大的，因为赔率会影响赌博的结果。

　　赔率可以增加您的获奖或让你只会遭受正常性的损失。它们的匹配概率能够估计你将要支付多少。作为一个规则好一个团队，在比赛中有更高的可能性获胜。这就是为什么一些投注者喜欢下小赌注在衰狗队。就是以防他们输了，投注者也就是失去一点点，但他们应该赢得的呢，其实敢于冒险的赌徒能够赢得更多。

　　寻找最好赔率的网站时，要看评论。要小心，不要到要付钱的网站，那些称为完美的投注者的网站其实只是诱饵。同时避免那些需要给予或接受这样的事情的网站。赔率，你会发现没有最好的。钱花去了，而任何赔率仅仅是书面上的。你的这些钱用起来是有风险的，所以要非常的小心。

　　对于任何一种运动，你都会看到有独立的赔率。所以以防万一你同时对足球和赛马感兴趣就要清楚两者的赔率是不同的。作为规则，你将看到以下几种：

　　橄榄球赔率和大学橄榄球规则；

　　NBA的赔率；

　　NHL的几率；

　　棒球赔率等等。

　　你会发现成千上万的赔率即便是说最佳的足球赔率。但要确保目前所有的赔率都能够为您的成功有所帮助。事实上，你是否会赢得比赛也依赖于其他的一些因素：

　　在领先的情况下，能够赢得一场比赛；

　　有团队合作精神；

　　天气条件会对足球比赛，高尔夫球比赛或赛马有所影响。

　　而赔率则是让您的赌注回收和获胜的关键。

　　Betting Odds Importance

　　Betting Odds are of essence. They are like prices for the goods you want to buy. So are you interested in finding stores with sales or in shopping around to get some discount? Surely you are. That's why betting odds are incredibly significant for bettors, because they influence gambling outcomes.

　　Betting odds can increase your winning or normalize your loss. They are a probability of a match or an estimate of how much you will be paid. As a rule the better a team plays, the higher are the odds that it will win when in a match with an underdog team. That's why some bettors like making small wagers on underdog teams. In case they lose, bettors lose little, but should they win, risky gamblers win big.

　　When looking for a site with the best betting odds, turn to reviews and listings. But be careful not to take the bait of the sites that pay money to be called perfect sites for bettors. Actually avoid the sites that both give and accept such things. The odds you will find there are no best. Money is paid, any betting odds are written. And it's you who risk money, so watch out.

　　For any kind of sport you will see separate betting odds. So in case you are interested in football or horse betting odds offered they will be absolutely different. As a rule you will see the following categories:

　　NFL betting odds and college football lines;

　　NBA odds;

　　NHL odds;

　　baseball odds, etc.

　　You will find tons of information on betting odds as well as best football odds. But make sure that all the odds shown are current as your winning depends on them. The fact whether you will win relies also on some other things:

　　absence of leading players that can strike the result of a match;

　　team spirit;

　　and even weather conditions for football matches, golf competitions or horse races.

　　Betting odds are the key to getting your wager back and receiving a winning.

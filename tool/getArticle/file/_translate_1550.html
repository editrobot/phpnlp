大西洋城加大裁员幅度

作者: 博之道 来源: 未知 时间: 2011-11-10 阅读: 次 博彩译文

　　在九月，大西洋城的赌场被迫削减将近1400个就业岗位，与去年裁员相比多了300多名。

　　在9月，赌场历来削减的旅游业就业岗位在劳动节后有所放缓。与传统赌场不同，网上赌场使用较少的劳动力就可以完成相应的工作。

　　根据新泽西州赌场管制委员会公布的数据可以看出，博彩业共减少总劳动人口35917，这同时也反映出博彩收入进入持续疲软的状态，所有11个赌场酒店也都削减他们的员工数量。

　　经济不景气以及来自周边国家赌场的竞争，使得大西洋城的博彩经营者增添了额外的压力，他们不得不大量削减人员以降低成本，在夏季旅游高峰季节结束后，业务减慢，就会进行季节性定期的裁员活动。

　　布赖恩J.泰瑞尔是进行酒店及旅游业管理研究的，他在新泽西州的理查德斯托克顿学院担任副教授。他说：“该行业的季节性决定就业的不稳定性。业务量下降，那么需要的员工就会减少。”

　　总体而言，今年的9月有1389个职位被淘汰。而在2009年9月是1015名，在2008年9月是裁员1,067名。今年九月，Borgata酒店赌场及水疗中心，这个博彩行业的最大雇主，也裁员272 人。赌场管制委员会的数据显示，在Borgata工作的员工还有6685人。

　　Atlantic City Job Cuts On Rise: Casino News

　　In September, Atlantic City casinos were forced to cut almost 1,400 jobs and increase of more than 300 job cuts last year.

　　In September, Casinos traditionally eliminate jobs in the post-Labor Day slowdown in tourism. Unlike in traditional casinos, online casino makes use of fewer workforces to get the job done.

　　Reducing the industry’s total work force to 35,917, and reflecting continued weakness in gaming revenue, all 11 casino hotels cut their payrolls for the month, according to figures released by the New Jersey Casino Control Commission.

　　While the sluggish economy and competition from casinos in surrounding states have put additional pressure on Atlantic City gaming operators to cut even deeper to reduce costs, the seasonal jobs routinely cut after business slows down at the end of the peak summer tourism season.

　　Brian J. Tyrrell, associate professor of hospitality and tourism management studies at the Richard Stockton College of New Jersey said, “The seasonality of the industry dictates employment. You have fewer employees with less business”。

　　Overall, in this September 1,389 jobs were eliminated. In September 2009 and 1,015 in September 2008, there were 1,067 job cuts. This September, Borgata Hotel Casino & Spa, the industry’s largest employer, led the pack with 272 job cuts. Casino Control Commission figures show that Borgata’s work force now stands at 6,685.

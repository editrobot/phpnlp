百家乐策略曝光

作者: 博之道 来源: 未知 时间: 2011-10-17 阅读: 次 博彩译文

　　许多人声称有过玩百家乐的秘密活动。有些人赢，也有一些人输掉游戏，这是游戏的性质。然而，最重要的是确保你自己是一边在玩游戏一边在享受游戏。

　　两家在玩游戏时，要考虑百家乐战略。首先有许多人声称有完善的长期游戏战略来赢得游戏。二是，有许多赌博专家却反驳这些说法，并说，没有什么战略能够有助于你战胜百家乐。无论哪种方式，但无可否认，百家乐是一个令人兴奋和愉快的的游戏，对事情的态度众说纷纭，但是可以肯定的是，成千上万的玩家赢得了大量的奖金。

　　赌博是要承担风险并且充满希望的，本场比赛的运气就在你身边当你需要它的时候它就会出现。人们赌博，是因为他们喜欢它作为一种消遣，是作为一种合法的娱乐形式。有些人喜欢打高尔夫球，但是有些人又喜欢玩百家乐。这些选择权在于你自己。关于百家乐的最好的事情是，它是一个循环往复的最好的赌博。与大多数其他形式赌博相比它的赔率非常低。

　　百家乐在投注时只有三种选择。经常，百家乐是很容易确定的。 中国新年2010年2月，在《历特刊》的庄家打赌的赌场优势显示只有1.06％，这包括您赢得赌注后要支付的5％佣金。从玩家来说，这样的赌场优势是不太好的，而不到一半的轮盘赌场优势则为1.24％。押注平局的赔率，是14.36％，这无疑是比较好的。这一切都意味着什么呢？在和局上投注是比在庄家或玩家下注来说一个坏的赌注。如果对下注没有什么特别的感觉，那么从数学的角度来说，建议你在庄家这方下赌注。不过，玩家和庄家之间的差异，是非常小的。

　　有良好的资金管理是赢钱的最好的方式，以确保您能够进行更久的游戏。我们都知道，我们应该只赌什么，因为我们输不起。但有一些方法，我们可以增加我们获胜的机会。尝试并设置好你打算玩牌时给定的限制。对一些人来说，可能是100万港元，500港元。显然，有些人会有比别人更高的赌注。如果达不到你的限制，可以休息一会再继续，休息了几个小时回来，你永远不知道你的运气可能会因此改变。

　　永远不要低估运气。游戏是随机的，从数学角度看每手牌领到的几率都是相等的，但是，这并不意味着你不能有令人难以置信的打牌方式，序列和趋势。有些人是幸运的，有些则没有那么幸运。如果你觉得有信心在某一天契合你的感受，如果你赢了，你会感觉好极了。如果你觉得别人有着连胜的可能，你就可能为此而失去胜利的机会。其他有关幸运好事，像你投注的奖金，从心理上这些奖金是可以实现的，这是一个伟大的时间优势，利用良好的打牌方式，按您的投注，离赢钱也就不远了。

　　Baccarat Strategies Exposed

　　Many people claim to have worked out the secret to playing baccarat. Some people win and some people lose, that is the nature of the game. The most important thing however is to make sure you enjoy yourself while playing and play whichever way works for you.

　　There are two sides to the story when considering baccarat strategy. The first is that there are many people who claim to have perfected long term winning strategies for the game. The second is that there are many gambling experts who refute these claims and say that there is no strategy that will help you beat baccarat. Either way there is no denying that baccarat is an exciting and enjoyable game to play and that thousands of players win big dollars when things go their way.

　　Gambling is about taking risks and hoping that the luck of the game is on your side when you need it. People gamble because they enjoy it as a pastime and that is certainly a legitimate form of entertainment. Some people like to play golf, others like to play baccarat – the choice is up to you. The best thing about baccarat is that it is one of the best gambles going around – the house percentage is very low compared to most other forms of gambling.

　　Baccarat handAs there are only three options when playing regular baccarat it is easy to determine the best bet. The house edge on a banker bet 2010年春节特刊 | Chinese New Year 2010 2 is only 1.06 percent and this includes the 5 percent commission you pay on winning bets. The house edge on the player is 1.24 percent which is not quite as good but is still less than half the house edge on roulette. The edge on the tie bet is 14.36 percent which is certainly nowhere near as good. What this all means is that betting on the tie is a bad bet compared to the player or the banker. If you have no feeling on what the next hand will be then the math suggests you should bet on the banker even though the difference between the player and the banker is very small.

　　Good money management is the best way to ensure you stay in the game for longer. We all know that we should only gamble what we can afford to lose but there are some ways that we can increase our chances of winning. Try and set a limit as to how much you intend to play with on any given session. For some it might be HK$500 for others it might be HK$1 million. Obviously some people will play higher stakes than others. If you reach your limit for any one session take a break and come back in a few hours after a rest; you never know your luck might change.

　　Don’t ever underestimate luck. It is true the game is random and that the math is the same every hand, but that doesn’t mean that you can’t have incredible runs, sequences and trends. Some people are lucky and others are not. If you feel confident on any given day go with your feelings, you’ll feel great if you win. If you feel someone else is on a hot streak that you want to jump on then there is nothing lost by following their lead. The other good thing about a lucky run is that you are betting with winnings – psychologically those winnings are still “the house’s money” which is a great time to take advantage of a good run and press your bets.

网上扑克之星如何作弊成功

作者: 博之道 来源: 未知 时间: 2011-12-16 阅读: 次 博彩译文

　　在任何一台计算机上设置的游戏，无论是魔兽世界还是Pac - Man，总会有一些作弊手段和黑客可以帮助玩家进一步预知游戏的结果。这样的逻辑思维同样适用于在线扑克游戏，因为游戏本身就是计算机通过一系列算法，运用子程序和软件来模拟比赛实况。

　　也就是说，问题就在于是否存在在线扑克作弊手段，或者说这种手段是否具有可行性。在线扑克室都是使用一个软件程序来生成扑克牌，以前的一些数据资料标明，如果有人知道到底是用什么代码来编写游戏程序，那么这些扑克室就很容易被操纵。

　　一些真正的在线扑克骗子会声称有能力预测基于RNG的卡（随机数生成器）的结果，这在现实中不一定能够凑效，因为现在的扑克室已经采取措施，增加其程序的复杂性，防止这样的情况发生。

　　真正的在线扑克作弊手段不依赖于RNG来确定和操纵结果，他们可以使用算法和与软件相关的子程序，来使得在在线扑克游戏中取胜。操纵算法是被证实的唯一一种在在线扑克作弊能够产生作用的手段。

　　就像任何其他计算机生成的游戏一样，在线扑克也是很容易被破解，被黑客攻击的，或者被一个能够很清楚知道是通过什么代码来决定游戏成功的玩家操纵。此代码在在线扑克游戏中起着很大的作用，可以决定任何一个玩家是否能够在游戏中取得胜利的关键。

　　How Online Poker Stars Cheats Succeed

　　In any computer generated game, whether it is World of Warcraft or Pac-Man, there are always cheats and hacks that can help a player further advance into the game. This same logic applies to online poker simply because the game itself is computer generated through a series of algorithms, subroutines and software that simulates a live game.

　　With that said, the question of whether online poker cheats exist or are even possible becomes a viable question. Since the online poker rooms use a software program to generate cards it is a well-documented and proven fact that these poker rooms can be easily manipulated if one knows exactly what the code of the programs are doing.

　　Some online poker cheats will purport to have the ability to predict the outcome of the cards based on the RNG （Random Number Generator）， This, in reality is not entirely possible, simply because of the complexity and security implemented by the poker rooms to prevent this very action.

　　The real online poker cheats do not rely on the RNG to determine and manipulate the outcome, rather they utilize the algorithms and subroutines associated with the software in order to force wins in the online poker games. The manipulation of the algorithms is the only proven online poker cheats that work.

　　The bottom line is that, just like any other computer generated game; online poker can easily be cracked, hacked, or manipulated by a player who knows exactly what to look for in the code. This code is the determining factor in the outcome of the online poker game, which makes it possible for anyone to win using specific online poker cheats.

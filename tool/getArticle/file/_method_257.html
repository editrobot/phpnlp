扑克玩家联盟新闻

作者: 博之道 来源: 未知 时间: 2011-09-08 阅读: 次 博彩技巧

　　扑克玩家联盟是世界上最杰出的扑克组织之一，它与58位国会候选人，在美利坚合众国，它被认为是最具强有力恪守承诺，在网上扑克合法化问题上给予最优先权的组织。

　　在即将到来的其中选举期间，扑克玩家联盟将支持这些候选人。选举将于第二个11月份举行，期间，众议院以及三分之一的参议员人员将面临着改选。扑克玩家联盟有43人是民主党人，剩下的是共和党人；二分之一的这些共和党人是现任的。根据扑克联盟新闻的陈述，推选出来的，了解调整以及使网上扑克合法化价值所在的在职行政官的人数呈上升趋势，由于扑克玩家联盟专用人员数的影响。

　　联盟相信，本年度选举以及那些赞成网上扑克并准备拥护他们权利的提倡候选人的重要性。扑克狂热者将不得不在确保候选人以他们投票赞成的方式当选中，扮演重要角色。

　　尽管巴尼弗兰网上赌博规定，消费者保护以及试试法案找到进入美国众议院的方法，但它在这年的选举中仍未能得到全票支持。党派分析家们感觉到，法案并没有能取得多大的进步，这意味着使网上扑克合法化这一问题退回了起点。专家期待一个更高的共和政体建立在这年里，然而，这将受赌博法案改革不可能被确立的影响。

　　Poker Players’ Alliance (PPA) News

　　The Poker Players’ Alliance (PPA), one of the most prominent poker organisations in the world, has listed 58 congressional candidates, who it believes to have a strong commitment to giving top priority to the issue of regularising online poker in the United States of America.

　　The PPA will endorse these candidates during the mid-term elections, which are just around the corner. The elections will be held on the 2nd of November, during which the House of Representative seats and a third of the Senate seats will face re-election.

　　Forty-three of the candidates in the PPA list are Democrats and the rest are Republicans; two of these Republicans are incumbents. According to a PPA press statement, the number of elected officials who understand the value of regulating and legalising online poker is on the rise as a result of the efforts of the dedicated members of PPA.

　　The alliance believes in the importance of the current year’s election and advocates electing candidates who sympathise with online poker players and are ready to support their rights. Poker enthusiasts will have to play a huge role in ensuring that the right candidates are elected by voting for them.

　　Although Barney Frank’s Internet Gambling Regulation, Consumer Protection, and Enforcement Act found its way into the House of Representatives, it failed to get a full vote this year. Political analysts feel that the bill might not make much progress, which means that the issue of legalising online poker is back to square one. Experts expect a higher Republican estimation this year; however, the effects that this will have on gaming legislation reform cannot be ascertained.

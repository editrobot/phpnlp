幽默的扑克名家斯科蒂・阮

作者: 博之道 来源: 未知 时间: 2011-10-18 阅读: 次 博彩译文

　　越南出生的斯科蒂·阮是职业扑克玩家中最活跃的玩家之一，在奖金收入上也是最成功的之一。他著名的是他的幽默感和在游戏中的不太认真的态度，更不用说他的流行称呼“扑克婴儿”。

　　扑克名人斯科蒂阮的职业

　　斯科蒂·阮开始打扑克是在他的家人已迁往加州的几年后。他在1995年首次打扑克巡回赛。等待三年，阮赢得他的第一场比赛，声称获世界系列扑克奥马哈高低手镯和150,000美元的奖金，他的作风就是那样的豪放。

　　次年，斯科蒂·阮甚至超出自己的期望，赢得第29届世界扑克系列的主要事件，这是在历史事件的第一个百万美元的奖金。

　　那场比赛的最后一手牌使斯科蒂·阮著名。荷官处理一个完整的牌，阮对他的对手凯文麦克布赖德说“你应该把它调动过去”麦克布莱德，这是持有一个完整的牌，忽略这一意见，因而迅速失去了一百万美元，到斯科蒂·阮则强满堂红。

　　斯科蒂·阮声称从这著名的胜利以来，已经成为世界扑克系列赛的主要参与者。他有四个以他的名字命名的世界系列扑克手镯，这是他在2000年主要事件系列最新赢得的。

　　其他方面的成功，包括赢得了世界扑克巡回赛，在那里他获得$ 969,000，获取Goldstrike世界扑克公开赛第一主赛事的胜利。斯科蒂·阮还在2007年世界扑克系列主赛事中赢得第11位，得奖50万美元。

　　迄今为止，在他印象深刻的职业生涯中，斯科蒂阮已赢得超过150倍的钱。这个数字包括30次比赛奖金的获得。迄今为止，斯科蒂·阮在他的职业扑克生涯中赢得了$ 7,000,000。

　　斯科蒂阮简介：打牌风格

　　斯科蒂阮是最容易辨认的玩家，他梳着背毛，穿着最昂贵的服装和戴着金链的职业扑克玩家。他也是一个明显的丰富多彩的个性，被称为赌桌上的动作，直到他赢得比赛时经常发表评论。他很喜欢他的幽默感，但是这种战术往往是非常令人不安的，这对对手来说非常有效。

　　Scotty Nguyen

　　Vietnam born Scotty Nguyen is one of the most active players on the professional poker circuit, as well as one of the most financially successful. He is renowned for his sense of humour and for not taking himself or the game too seriously, not to mention his bad beat catchphrase ‘that’s poker baby’。

　　Scotty Nguyen Poker Celebrity & Career

　　Scotty Nguyen started playing poker several years after his family had relocated to California. He hit the poker tour for the first time in 1995. Nguyen had to wait three years before winning his first tournament, doing so in style by claiming the World Series of Poker Omaha Hi/Lo bracelet and $150,000 in prize money.

　　The following year, Scotty Nguyen exceeded even his own expectations by winning the main event at the 29th Annual World Series of Poker, claiming the first million dollar prize in the history of the event.

　　The final hand of that tournament made Scotty Nguyen famous. The dealer dealt a full house, and Nguyen turned to his opponent Kevin McBride saying ‘You call it’s gonna be all over baby.’ McBride, holding a full house, ignored this advice, called, and promptly lost out on a million dollars to Scotty Nguyen’s stronger full house.

　　Since claiming this famous win, Scotty Nguyen has been a major player in the World Series of Poker. He has four World Series of Poker bracelets to his name, with his win in 2000 his only main event win in the series to date.

　　Other notable wins have included taking a main event victory on the World Poker Tour, where he earned $969,000 by finishing first in the Goldstrike World Poker Open. Scotty Nguyen also claimed half a million dollars by finishing in 11th place in the main event of the 2007 World Series of Poker.

　　To date Scotty Nguyen has finished in the money over 150 times during his impressive career. This figure includes 30 first places finishes. To date Scotty Nguyen has won over $7,000,000 in his professional poker career.

　　Scotty Nguyen Profile: Playing Style

　　Scotty Nguyen is one of the most easily recognisable players in professional poker with his slicked back hair, expensive clothing and gold chains. He is also a distinctly colourful personality, who is known to comment on the action at the table up until the moment he wins. While he is well liked for his sense of humour, this tactic is often very effective in unsettling his opponents.

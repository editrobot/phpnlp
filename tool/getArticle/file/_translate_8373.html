开业以来牛津赌场收入相比其他赌场较高

作者: 艳艳 来源: 时间: 2012-08-31 阅读: 次 博彩译文

　　波特兰--缅因州最新的赌场，在它运作以来的前两个月收入近10000000美元，而自从新赌场开业，国家其他地方的赌场收入逐渐的降低。

　　六月五日牛津赌场在缅因州西部的牛津镇开业，赌徒们的净收入损失数额----在六月和七月一共达到了9800000美元。根据缅因州赌博控制板它支付了近4100000美元的税金。

　　这些数字是强大的，赌场桌上游戏的收入几乎是预计的两倍，东北博彩项目在大学massachusetts-dartmouth的克莱德巴罗这样说道。七月份的时候，赌场平均每日每桌上游戏盈利达到2471美元。

　　“我们原本预计他们每台每天开始在大约1300美元，然后逐渐加大到1700美元，”文章出自博 之道罗说。”这些数字个大西洋城的是有得一比的。”

　　牛津赌场计划在十月份的时候增加10种桌上游戏，还有数以百计的老虎机，发言人史葛·斯密说。这个赌场现在有529个插槽和12种桌上游戏，包括二十一点，双骰子，轮盘赌。

　　“我们有了一个很好的开始，” 斯密说。

　　班戈的好莱坞赌场的净收入，距离牛津大约120英里，七月共计5500000美元，相比2011年同一个月的5900000美元有所下降。好莱坞赌场共有923台老虎机和16种桌上游戏。

　　一些导致减少的因素可以归因于牛津开设赌场，总经理约翰奥斯本说。对比2011年来说，这个七月的周末更少，而周末是一周中最繁忙的天数，。

　　对于牛津赌场有很多的好奇心，文章出自博 之道但确定其长期影响的班戈设施速度太快了点，他说。

　　“我们相信长期客户仍然会频繁的选择他们的赌场，我们希望是在班戈的好莱坞赌场，”他说。

　　如果好莱坞赌场对牛津开幕没有任何影响，那将会是很神奇的事情，巴罗说。

　　PORTLAND -- Maine's newest casino had revenues of nearly $10 million in its first two months of operation, while revenues at the state's other casino in Bangor have fallen since the new casino opened.

　　Oxford Casino opened June 5 in the western Maine town of Oxford and had net revenues -- the amount lost by gamblers -- of $9.8 million in June and July combined. It paid nearly $4.1 million in taxes, according to the Maine Gambling Control Board.

　　Those numbers are strong, and revenues from the casino's table games are nearly double what was projected, said Clyde Barrow of the Northeast Gaming Research Project at the University of Massachusetts-Dartmouth. In July, the casino was winning an average of $2,471 per table game per day.

　　"We had originally projected them to start out at about $1,300 per table per day and gradually ratchet up to $1,700," Barrow said. "Those numbers are comparable to Atlantic City."Oxford Casino is planning to add 10 more table games and hundreds more slot machines in October, said spokesman Scott Smith. It now has 529 slots and 12 table games including blackjack, craps and roulette.

　　"We're off to a very a good start," Smith said.

　　Net revenues at Bangor's Hollywood Casino, about 120 miles from Oxford, bozhidao.com totaled $5.5 million in July, down from $5.9 million the same month in 2011. Hollywood Casino has 923 slot machines and 16 table games.

　　Some of the fall-off can be attributed to the opening of Oxford Casino, said General Manager John Osborne. This July also had fewer weekend days, the busiest days of the week, than 2011.

　　There's a lot of curiosity about Oxford Casino,bozhidao.com but it's too soon to determine its long-term effect on the Bangor facility, he said.

　　It would be surprising if Hollywood Casino didn't feel any effect of the Oxford opening, Barrow said.

网上赌博行业诞生两个新的合作关系

作者: 博之道 来源: 未知 时间: 2011-11-24 阅读: 次 博彩译文

　　导读：立博与Microgaming形成新的合作伙伴关系。与此同时，Tower Gaming 也与Cake Network 和 EverMatrix双方达成合作协议。

　　今天，网上赌博行业两个新消息值得关注，这一领域的公司之间诞生新的合作关系。首先是立博和Microgaming宣布达成合作协议。立博，英国著名的在线赌博公司，与西班牙Microgaming网站形成新的合作伙伴关系。

　　在西班牙，随着网上赌博的法律越来越完善，立博抓住机会成为西班牙第一家网上赌博公司。它活跃在西班牙的互联网上，并充分调节西班牙的在线赌博业。西班牙新的网上赌博法律是从1月1日起实行，立博将作为西班牙第一个完整的在线赌博网站，包括现金赌场游戏，在线扑克室以及博彩线上游戏等等。

　　对于这个项目，立博选择了与Microgaming合作。Microgaming是在线赌博软件开发行业最好的公司之一。 Microgaming已经与世界上主要的在线赌博公司广泛合作，这次与并立博的合作，会明显加强Microgaming公司的市场价值。

　　关于网上赌博行业的另一条新闻是TowerGaming.com宣布与the Cake Network 和 EverMatrix合作。TowerGaming公司提供专业的网上赌场游戏，在线扑克室以及网上体育博彩等。 EveryMatrix和TowerGaming的合作已经不是第一次了。

　　TowerGaming的在线体育博彩平台就是属于EveryMatrix开发的OddsMatrix平台。现在他们已经延长了合作协议，新的协议还包括GamMatrix平台，游戏管理平台以及新的付款处理平台。不久，一个拥有TowerGaming帐户玩家将可以在TowerGaming.com体验20个新游戏。

　　与此同时，TowerGaming已经脱离Ongame扑克网络。TowerGaming是Ongame扑克网络长达6年的成员，如今转而加入欧元区的Cake Network。TowerGaming对此作出解释，新的网络能为他们的玩家提供更好的服务。现在，TowerGaming用户能同时享受扑克世界扑克巡回赛，以及在欧洲和北美黄金时间的举办的扑克比赛盛事。

　　译文：

　　Two New Online Gambling Partnerships

　　Ladbrokes partners up with Microgaming

　　Meanwhile, Tower Gaming sides with the Cake Network and EverMatrix

　　Two new online gambling news for today, announcing new partnerships between the companies that are active in the field. The first is the one between Ladbrokes and Microgaming. Ladbrokes, the well known British online gambling corporation, has partnered up with Microgaming for its Spanish and Danish websites, which the company si about to open up.

　　With the online gambling laws becoming closer to a reality in Spain, Ladbrokes seized the opportunity to become one of the first online gambling companies that is active in the new, fully regulated Spanish online gambling industry. The new laws are set to be active starting January 1st, and that’s when Ladbrokes is set to open its newest site, that will act as a complete online gambling site, with cash casino games, an online poker room, and online sports betting sections.

　　For this project, Ladbrokes have chosen to collaborate with Microgaming, one of the best companies in the gambling software development business. Microgaming has already collaborated with most of the world’s major online gambling companies, and the addition of Ladbrokes to their portfolio will only strengthen their market value.

　　Another piece of news from the world of online gambling comes from Tower Gaming, a company that provides online casino games, an online poker room and an online sports betting section on their website, TowerGaming.com. They have announced two new agreements with the Euro Cake Network and EveryMatrix. EveryMatrix and TowerGaming aren’t pairing up for the first time – Tower Gaming’s online sports betting platform was made a reality courtesy of EveryMatrix’s OddsMatrix platform. They have now extended their agreement to include the GamMatrix platform, which includes game management and a new payment processing platform. Soon, players that have an account with TowerGaming will gain access to over twenty new games.

　　At the same time, Tower Gaming have left the Ongame Poker Network, where they had been members for six years, to join the Euro Cake Network. The reason offered by Tower Gaming is that the new network offers better services for their players. Now, the users have access to such poker events as the World Poker Tour, the Aussie Millions, or the European and North American Primetime liquidity.

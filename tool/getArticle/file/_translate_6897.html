史诺柯米市玩家赢得1045270美元

作者: 博之道 来源: 时间: 2012-06-22 阅读: 次 博彩译文

　　史诺柯米市，华盛顿州-（新闻稿）-火箭博彩系统高兴地宣布他们的黄金系列二级博彩颁发了2012年第四个百万美元级奖金，这也是该项目历史上金额排名第二的奖金。

　　2012年 6月11日周一，一名幸运的游戏者在玩火箭金杯步进赌博时获得了黄金系列1045270美元的顶级大奖，这一幸运大奖出现在据西雅图30分钟路程的华盛顿州史诺柯米市史诺柯米赌场，

　　这标志着史诺柯米市第二次出现黄金系列大奖，也是黄金系列2009年引进后的第18次顶级大奖，09年至今，黄金系列一应总计发出超过8亿美元的奖金。

　　黄金系列目前包括9个视频项目和10个步进项目，并且经常出台新的赌博项目，为玩家在显示器前进行的独特而且有趣的大范围中奖项目，黄金系列已经成为这个行业最受欢迎的二级博彩项目。

　　Snoqualmie player wins $1,045,270

　　SNOQUALMIE, Washington -- (PRESS RELEASE) -- Rocket Gaming Systems is pleased to announce that its Gold Series line of Class II games has awarded its fourth million dollar prize of 2012 and the second highest amount in its history.

　　On Monday, June 11, 2012 a lucky player was awarded a Gold Series top progressive prize of $1,045,270 while playing Rocket’s Gold Cup stepper game. The win occurred at the Snoqualmie Casino in Snoqualmie, Washington, 30 minutes from Seattle. This marks the second time the Gold Series has hit at Snoqualmie Casino and the 18th Gold Series top progressive prize since the game was introduced in 2009. In all, the Gold Series has awarded over $800 million in prizes.

　　The Gold Series currently consists of nine video titles and 10 stepper titles, with new games being released frequently. Providing players with unique and entertaining themes along with a wide range of bonus round displays, the Gold Series has become one of the most popular Class II product lines in the industry.

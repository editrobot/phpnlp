扑克女孩魄力―把钱拿来！

作者: 博之道 来源: 未知 时间: 2011-10-20 阅读: 次 博彩译文

　　当涉及到职业扑克，很少有人会完全不知道存在一些极有才华的的女扑克职业选手。向您展示在2008年我们结合现场比赛的奖金超过$八百九十万七顶级女扑克玩家。

　　1。安妮公爵

　　现场比赛的奖金总额超过$ 3,600,000

　　安妮公爵（1965年9月13日出生，）持有的“钱”的大部分，在世界妇女扑克系列的记录。2004年，她赢得了世界系列扑克锦标赛冠军，获得2,000,000元，在任何单一事件的扑克玩家中，这在当时是支付给一名女性的最高的奖金。她收到了大量的宣传活动的邀请，辅导本阿弗莱克，他因此赢得了2004年加利福尼亚州扑克冠军赛。杜克大学拥有一个在奥马哈高低WSOP手镯，她认为：现在妇女不玩扑克，其实扑克是男性和女性可以完全平等竞争的为数不多的体育之一。

　　2。珍妮·哈曼

　　现场比赛的奖金总额超过$ 2,200,000

　　珍妮·哈曼（11月29日出生，1964年）是唯一的女性获得两个公开活动的WSOP手镯。她8岁开始与她父亲玩扑克游戏。在获得了生物学学位后，她开始在赌场玩现金游戏，曾经试图开始自己的生意，自己两次破产后放弃。在一次赌桌上，赢得一些钱后，她的玩法趋于稳定，并开始参加比赛的竞争，因此在2002年赢得她在2000年的第一个WSOP手镯，之后又赢得第二个。

　　3。 辛迪·紫罗兰又名“小甜蜜”。

　　现场比赛的奖金总额超过$ 1,200,000

　　辛迪·紫罗兰（生于1959年8月19日）从年轻时玩扑克牌。当她到达法定年龄，她开始玩赌场扑克牌，她的第一个重大胜利来到时，于1984年在Golden Nugget的七张牌梭哈比赛，她赢得了74,000元。当时，这是一名女子锦标赛扑克赢得最多的钱。在2004年，紫罗兰实现她的梦想，在七张牌梭哈高低分裂比赛中，她赢得WSOP手镯。此后，她出现在2005年世界扑克系列三个决赛桌。

　　4。 LIZ辽又名“扑克DIVA”

　　现场比赛的奖金总额超过690 000美元

　　LIZ辽（8月2日出生，1974年）出生于越南，与她的家人移民到美国时，她还是一个婴儿。辽在13岁开始打扑克，2005年，她第一次的现场扑克锦标赛在WSOP NLHE事件获得第五名的成绩。利兹已经成功地赢得2006年和2007年洛杉矶扑克经典比赛。从她第一次在2005年开始，她把她的比赛奖金的20％捐赠给慈善机构。而她的绰号“扑克DIVA”是源于她时尚的风格。

　　5。埃里卡勋伯格又名“21点贝贝”

　　现场比赛的奖金总额超过$ 630,000

　　埃里卡·勋伯格（1978年3月25日出生，）是原来职业是模特。最初她的热情被21点吸引。后来她参加了著名的麻省理工学院21点的培训，接着参加21点的世界系列竞争。勋伯格终于放弃了这个错误选择，并于2005年开始玩扑克。 2007年，她在WSOP无限额德州扑克比赛中，排第三位，在高赌注扑克选手大卫Benyamine之后。

　　6。珍妮·佛提莉又名“Unabombshell”

　　现场比赛的奖金总额超过$ 450,000

　　珍妮·佛提莉（生于1958年9月16日，）也是最出名的一个演员。她实际上是一个在赌博场上的一个喜剧角色，到2003年，让它渡过第一次她在扑克的兴趣，她在比赛开始玩球。在2005年，她赢得了在女人街无限额德州扑克事件后不久，在洛杉矶赌场赢得第三次世界扑克巡回赛女士邀请赛的WSOP手镯。蒂莉通常命名为最佳的名人扑克玩家，目前与职业扑克选手菲尔Laak又名Unabomber是恋人关系，这是她的扑克昵称“Unabombshell”的出处。

　　7。莎侬·伊丽莎白

　　现场比赛的奖金总额超过137 000美元

　　莎侬·伊丽莎白（生于1973年9月7日，）最出名的是她作为一个演员的工作，也许特别是她在美国派电影的影响。即便如此，她的第二个职业生涯是扑克，被称为领先的名人扑克选手之一。她的成功在很大程度上是由于她的朋友，她向职业扑克选手安东尼奥的Esfandiari和菲尔Laak学习。伊丽莎白已经第三次获奖，特别在2007年，NBC的全国对决扑克锦标赛，赢得了2006年妮基希尔顿新年的夏娃扑克锦标赛。

　　Poker Girl Power - Show me the money!

　　When it comes to professional poker few people will be completely unaware of the presence of some extremely talented female poker pro's. To show you just how much of a dent these ladies are making on the pro circuit we take a look at 7 of the top female poker players with combined live tournament winnings which in 2008 exceed $8,907,000 .

　　1. Annie Duke

　　Total live tournament winnings exceed $3,600,000

　　Annie Duke （born September 13, 1965） holds the record for the most 'in the money' finishes at the World Series of Poker by women. In 2004 she won $2,000,000 in the World Series of Poker Tournament of Champions, which at the time was the highest winnings paid to a female poker player in any single event. She received a lot of publicity for tutoring Ben Affleck who consequently won the 2004 California State Poker Championship. Duke holds one WSOP bracelet in Omaha HiLo and now refuses to play in women's only poker tournaments saying that poker is one of the few sports where men and women can compete on a totally equal footing.

　　2. Jennifer Harman AKA "Jen"

　　Total live tournament winnings exceed $2,200,000

　　Jennifer Harman （born November 29, 1964） is the only woman to hold two bracelets in WSOP Open Events. She started playing poker at the young age of 8 at her father’s poker games. After having earned a degree in biology she started playing cash games at casinos and ended up bankrupting herself twice, once trying to start her own business and once at the tables. After loaning some money she eventually managed to find stability in her play and started competing in tournaments consequently winning her first WSOP bracelet in 2000 and her second in 2002.

　　3. Cyndy Violette AKA "Sweetest"

　　Total live tournament winnings exceed $1,200,000

　　Cindy Violette （born 19 August 1959） got into poker by playing from a young age through family members. She started playing casino poker when she reached the legal age and her first major win came when she won $74,000 at a seven-card stud tournament at the Golden Nugget in 1984. At the time this was the most money ever won by a woman in tournament poker. Violette fulfilled her dream when she went on to win a WSOP bracelet in 2004 in the seven-card stud HiLo split tournament. Consequently she managed to make three final tables at the 2005 World Series of Poker.

　　4. Liz Lieu AKA "Poker Diva"

　　Total live tournament winnings exceed $690,000

　　Liz Lieu （born August 2, 1974） was born in Vietnam and immigrated into the US with her family when she was still a baby. Lieu first started playing poker at age 13 and played her first live poker tournament in 2005 earning a 5th place finish in the WSOP NLHE event. Liz has managed to win both the 2006 and 2007 L.A. Poker Classic and has been donating 20% of her tournament winnings to charity ever since she first started in 2005. Her love for fashion and style is where her nickname ‘Poker Diva’ originates from.

　　5. Erica Schoenberg AKA "Blackjack Babe"

　　Total live tournament winnings exceed $630,000

　　Erica Schoenberg （born March 25, 1978） is a former professional model.  Initially blackjack was her passion and she received training from a member of the renowned MIT Blackjack Team and went on to compete in the World Series of Blackjack. Schoenberg finally caught the bug and started playing poker in 2005. In 2007 she came third in the WSOP No-Limit Hold'em and is currently engaged to high stakes poker player David Benyamine.

　　6. Jennifer Tilly AKA "The Unabombshell"

　　Total live tournament winnings exceed $450,000

　　Jennifer Tilly （born September 16, 1958） is perhaps best known for her accomplishments as an actress. It was in fact a role in the gambling comedy Let it Ride which first got her interested in poker and by 2003 she started playing in tournaments. In 2005 she won a WSOP bracelet in the Ladies' No-Limit Hold'em event and soon after won the third World Poker Tour Ladies Invitational Tournament at Bicycle Casino in LA. Tilly is often named the best celebrity poker player and is currently dating professional poker player Phil Laak AKA The Unabomber which is where her poker nickname ‘The Unabombshell’ originates from.

　　7. Shannon Elizabeth

　　Total live tournament winnings exceed  $137,000

　　Shannon Elizabeth （born September 7, 1973） is best known for her work as an actress and perhaps specifically her role in the American Pie movies. Even so she now describes poker as her second career and has been called one of the leading celebrity poker players. Much of her success is due to learning from her friends, professional poker players Antonio Esfandiari and Phil Laak. Elizabeth since has come third in the 2007 Special, NBC National Heads-Up Poker Championship and won the 2006 Nicky Hilton New Year's Eve Poker Tournament.

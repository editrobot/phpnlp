Facebook允许在线扑克广告

作者: 博之道 来源: 未知 时间: 2011-11-14 阅读: 次 博彩译文

　　社会媒体巨头逐渐开始接受在线扑克广告，其广告将针对所有在线游戏网站。尽管在线扑克是当今流传最广的娱乐活动之一，但它的地位在世界各地的许多国家仍然是不确定的，因为它是在一类依靠运气的机会游戏。在线扑克一直处于一个法律灰色地带，这阻碍了许多扑克玩家追求自己喜欢的游戏，主要是担心可能会出现某些法律后果。美国是一个很好的例子，最近该国对在线扑克合的法化问题进行激烈的争论。随着美国全速扑克的关闭，许多在线扑克高手不得不重新寻找新的在线扑克网站。

　　而最新的扑克新闻为在线扑克带来了一些负面影响。谁更好地恢复在线扑克在互联网上的地位？也许只有今天最大的媒体巨头：FACEBOOK。

　　多年来，Facebook不支持任何在线扑克网站的任何广告，直到最近，它对自己的广告系统作出了一定的调整。Facebook目前已经允许在线赌博公司在该网站上投放广告。Facebook的这一措施，让我们见证了某些大型社会媒体网站开始逐渐接纳在线扑克网站的广告。这一个新的变化将为在线扑克网站提供了更多的自由空间。

　　Facebook to allow online poker ads

　　The social media giant now accepts poker ads.The ads will be targeted and available for all online gaming sites

　　Although online poker is one of today’s most widely spread sports, its status is still uncertain many countries all over the world, as it’s put in the same category as games that are based solely on luck. This gray area that online poker has found itself in has prevented many poker players to pursue their favorite game, mainly due to possible legal repercussions that might arise. The United States are a perfect example, as recently the country has found itself in the middle of heated arguments for and against online poker being legalized. Many online poker pros have found themselves looking for a new country to call home, and many online poker rooms, from the smallest to even the largest of them all, have been forced to shut down in the US.

　　But things are looking up. The latest poker news are set to bring back some of the glory that online poker has lost over the summer, between the numerous scandals surrounding the sport. And who better to improve on online poker’s resume than one of the largest media giants on the internet today: Facebook.

　　Throughout the years, Facebook has made little changes to their advertisement system, but one of them remained in effect until recently: it did not support any ads from any online poker sites. However, this week everything changed, and Facebook is now allowing online gambling companies to advertise through the site. This includes online poker sites, and we’re about to witness the first of these ads available on the social media site.This new policy does not give unlimited freedom to online poker sites.

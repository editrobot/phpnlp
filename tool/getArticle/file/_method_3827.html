七喜百家乐游戏规则（中英对译）

　　6. 在这些七喜百家乐规则里:

　　1.七喜百家乐定义：

　　1.1.1 “手牌”是指在这轮游戏中已处理“庄家”或“闲家”的牌.

　　1.1.2 “初步处理”是指只有前两张“闲家”的牌和前两张“庄家”的牌已经处理了。“闲家”的手上第一张牌固定面值是 7.

　　1.1.3 “天牌”是指在初局时“闲家”或是“庄家”手上牌总点数是8或9.

　　1.1.4 “总值”是指手上的纸牌牌的总数值

　　1.1.5 “一轮游戏”是指游戏阶段包括游戏开始和荷官公布结果，游戏结算

　　1.1.6 “和局”意思是“闲家”和“庄家”手上的牌在这局游戏结束时的点数相同。

　　1.1.7 “超级7“是指所有完整的一手牌中至少有2张7组成的，包括托管到“闲家”手上的7。

　　2.七喜百家乐游戏模式

　　2.1 7喜百家乐游戏应有四到八副牌，每副有52张不含王牌，牌背面是同颜色和设计的牌。

　　2.2 游戏布局如下表所示：

　　2.2.1 庄家下注区域

　　2.2.2 闲家下注区域

　　2.2.3 和局下注区域

　　2.2.4 超级7 下注区域

　　3.七喜百家乐游戏规则

　　3.1 当荷官宣布“请下注”时就代表游戏开始

　　3.2 当荷官宣布“不能进行下注”时意味着这局下注结束

　　3.3 牌的数值统计应当如下：

　　3.3.1 任何2到9的牌（含）点数值就是牌面的数值，A 的数值就是它的点数1

　　3.3.2 任何10，J,Q,K的点数都是0

　　3.4 手上的牌的总数值统计如下：

　　3.4.1 手上牌面相加的总数如果是在0到9之间，就是这手牌的点数

　　3.4.2 手上牌面相加的点数如果是在10或10以上，那么这手牌得点数是以个位为准

　　3.5 “庄家”“闲家”“和”“至尊7”赌注是独立下注的，可以组合或单独下注

　　3.6 一般会发两手牌，一个是发给“闲家”，另一个是发给“庄家”

　　3.7 “闲家”的第一张牌固定面值是7

　　3.8 从牌靴所发的三张牌应如下：

　　3.8.1 所发的第一张和第三张牌代表“庄家”手上的第一和第二张牌

　　3.8.2 所发的第二张牌代表“闲家”手上的第二张牌

　　3.9 在前四张牌知道后，随从每个下注区的总点数值的声明，应要求荷官所发的第三张牌应当依据下面的规则：

　　抽牌规则

　　3.9.1 如果“闲家”或是“庄家”手上的牌在发完初始的四张牌后加起来的点数值为8或9，就是所称的“天牌”，那么此时就无需再发牌

　　3.9.2 当“闲家”手上有：

　　3.9.3 当“闲家”停牌时，“庄家”的牌面总点数和是0-5时抽牌，6或7时停牌

　　3.9.4 当“庄家”有：

　　0-1-2 当“闲家”第三张牌点数值为0-9时，“庄家”抽牌

　　3 当“闲家”第三张牌点数值为0-9，除8以外时，“庄家”抽牌

　　4 当“闲家”第三张牌点数值为2-7时，“庄家”抽牌

　　5 当“闲家”第三张牌点数值为4-7时，“庄家”抽牌

　　6 当“闲家”第三张牌点数值为6或7时,“庄家”抽牌

　　“庄家”在其余点数时都为停牌

　　3.10 一轮游戏中的赢者就是手上总点数值最高者

　　3.10.1 如果“闲家”和“庄家”手上的牌总点数值相同时，这局就为“和”。在下“和”或是所有“和”的赌注赢得情况下，“闲家”和“庄家”的初始赌注是不输不赢的

　　七喜百家乐规则与条款

　　5.1 如果在桌面出现纸牌错误的行为：

　　5.1.1 此牌局视为无效；

　　5.1.2 此牌局的所有结果将暂停 .

　　5.2 在初局时，如果有一张多余的牌发到“闲家”手上时 ，也不能重洗牌，那么此牌局视为无效

　　5.3 在初局时，如果有两张以上的牌发到“庄家”手上时，也不能重洗牌， 那么此牌局视为无效

　　5.4 在初局时，如果从牌靴中同时有两张或以上的牌被抽出（暴露），并且纸牌没有按照牌序来发，那么此牌局视为无效

　　5.5 如果为了完成此局而出现无效的牌，那么此牌局视为无效

　　5.7 当荷官宣布“不能进行下注”时，那么此时就不能进行下注或增加下注额度，并且所下的投注或是一定程度上增加的投注额都将被拒绝或 不被兑现

　　5.7 当荷官宣布“不能进行下注”时，那么此时赌注是不可收回的，所收回的赌注将会依据游戏结果进行结算

　　7 UP BACCARAT

　　(MBS)

　　Games Rules

　　26 August 2011

　　1. Definitions

　　2. Mode of Play

　　3. How to Play

　　4. Settlement

　　5. Irregularities

　　6. In these rules:

　　1. Definitions

　　1.1.1. "Hand" means the cards that have been dealt to the “Banker” or “Player” in a round of play.

　　1.1.2. “Initial deal” means only the first two cards to the “Player” and the first two cards to the “Banker” have been dealt. The first card of the “Player” hand will always have a value of 7.

　　1.1.3. "Natural" means a point total of eight or nine for either the “Player” hand or the “Banker” hand in the initial deal.

　　1.1.4. "Point total" means the total value of cards in a hand.

　　1.1.5. "Round of play" means the period of play commencing with the commencement of play and concluding when the Dealer announces a result and, if applicable, collects losing wagers and pays winning wagers.

　　1.1.6. "Tie" means that both the “Player” hand and the “Banker” hand have t he same point total at the end of a round of play.

　　1.1.7. “Super 7’s” is any completed hand that contains at least 2 cards of 7s, inclusive of the mandatory 7 card dealt to the “Player” hand.

　　2. Mode of Play

　　2.1.The game of 7 Up Baccarat shall be played with four to eight decks of cards, with each deck having 52 cards without jokers and with backs of the same colour and design.

　　2.2. The layout for this game is as shown in Diagram “A”, Diagram “B” and Diagram “C” with:

　　2.2.1. Betting areas for the placement of “Banker” wagers;

　　2.2.2. Betting areas for the placement of “Player” wagers;

　　2.2.3. Betting areas for the placement of “Tie” wagers; and

　　2.2.4. Betting areas for the placement of “Super 7’s” wagers.

　　3. How to Play

　　3.1. Commencement of play shall begin when the Dealer announces “Place your bets, please”.

　　3.2. Closing of bets shall take effect when the Dealer announces “No more bets”.

　　3.3. The point value of cards shall be as follows:

　　3.3.1. Any card from Two to Nine inclusive shall have as its point value its face value and an Ace shall have as its point value a value of one; and

　　3.3.2. Any Ten, Jack, Queen or King shall have a point value of zero.

　　3.4. The point total of a hand shall be as follows:

　　3.4.1. Where the total value of the cards in the hand is a number between zero and nine, the point total shall be that number; or

　　3.4.2.Where the total value of the cards in the hand is the number ten or a higher number, the point total shall be the right digit of that number only.

　　3.5. “Banker”, “Player”, “Tie” and “Super 7’s” wagers are independent wagers and may be played in combination or individually.

　　3.6. There shall be two hands dealt, one of which shall be designated as the “Player” hand and the other as the “Banker” hand.

　　3.7. The first card of the “Player” hand shall always have a value of 7.

　　3.8. Three cards shall then be dealt from the shoe with:

　　3.8.1.The first and third cards dealt representing the first and second cards of the “Banker” hand; and

　　3.8.2.The second card dealt representing the second card of the “Player” hand.

　　3.9. Following the announcement of the point total of each hand after the initial four cards are known , the Dealer shall deal a third card if so required pursuant to the Table of Play below:

　　Table of Play

　　3.9.1. If the point total of the “Player” hand or the “Banker” hand after the initial four cards are known is 8 or 9, which is called a "Natural", no more cards shall be dealt to either hand.

　　3.9.2. When the “Player” has:

　　0-1-2-3-4-5 “Player” draws a card

　　6-7 “Player” stands

　　8-9 Natural, no cards are drawn to either hand

　　3.9.3.When the “Player” stands, the “Banker” draws on 0-5 and stands on 6 or 7.

　　3.9.4.When the “Banker” has:

　　0-1-2 “Banker” draws when the “Player” third card has a point value of 0-9.

　　3 “Banker” draws when the “Player” third card has a point value of 0-9, except for 8.

　　4 “Banker” draws when the “Player” third card has a point value of 2-7.

　　5 “Banker” draws when the “Player” third card has a point value of 4-7.

　　6 “Banker” draws when the “Player” third card has a point value of 6 or 7.

　　“Banker” stands at all other times.

　　3.10. The winner of the round of play is the hand with the highest point total.

　　3.10.1.If the “Player” and “Banker” hands have an identical point total, the round of play is a “Tie”.The initial wagers on “Player” and “Banker” do not win or lose in the case of a “Tie” and all “Tie” wagers win.

　　4. Settlement

　　4.1.Settlement of wagers shall be as follows:

　　Winning Bet Pays

　　“Banker” with point total of 7 9 to 5

　　“Banker” with any other point total 1 to 1

　　“Player” with point total of 7 1 to 2

　　“Player” with any other point total 1 to 1

　　“Tie” with point total of 7 9 to 1

　　“Tie” with any other point total 7 to 1

　　“Super 7’s”

　　Any two 7s 2 to 1

　　Any three 7s 7 to 1

　　Any four 7s 17 to 1

　　Any five 7s 77 to 1

　　Any six 7s 777 to 1

　　5. Irregularities

　　5.1.If a foreign card is found in the course of a game:

　　5.1.1.The current round of play shall be void; and

　　5.1.2.All results of the previous games will stand.

　　5.2.When more than one card is dealt to the “Player” hand on the initial deal, and the hand cannot be reconstructed, the round of play shall be void.

　　5.3.When more than two cards are dealt to the “Banker” hand on the initial deal, and the hand cannot be reconstructed, the round of play shall be void.

　　5.4. In the event that two or more cards are simultaneously drawn from the shoe or device and it is not possible to ascertain the order of the cards drawn during the initial deal, the round of play shall be void.

　　5.5. If there are insufficient cards in the shoe to complete a round of play, the round of play shall be void.

　　5.6. After the Dealer has announced “No more bets”, no wager may be placed or increased and any wager so placed or to the extent increased will be refused and/or will not be honored.

　　5.7. After the Dealer has announced “No more bets”, no wager may be withdrawn and any wager withdrawn shall be won or lost based on the game results.

匪徒的抢劫目标：赌徒

作者: 来源: 博之道 时间: 2011-12-09 阅读: 次 博彩译文

　　当人们谈论赌场安全问题时，他们通常所讲的是投注策略，资金管理，玩游戏的最佳赔率，以及其他类似的事情。虽然这些对于一个赌徒来说很重要，但当你晚上去赌博时，还有另个安全问题需要警惕：赌徒容易成为抢劫对象。

　　今天上午芝加哥警方表示，两名持枪男子殴打一名62岁的中国移民，而这位中国公民当晚在印第安纳州赌场赢得了超过10,000美元的赌金。持枪男子殴打他，并抢走了他的赌金。

　　受害人的儿子陈伟霆表示，他相信劫匪是从赌场尾随他父亲回家的。 他说：“在晚上，唐人街经常发生这样的事情。”陈先生还说，匪徒的猎物通常是中国移民。他的父亲被打伤，但没有严重受伤。

　　成龙说，这种犯罪类型在唐人街是常见的。在赌场内是安全的，因为赌场的每一个角落，包括每一寸地板，都被赌场摄像机覆盖。一旦你出了赌场，特别是你独自走在大街上时，你需要好好照顾自己。许多城市在赌场外都安排有警力，以阻止这种犯罪，但警察不会跟着你回家。出于这个原因，你需要做好预防措施。如果可能，不要单独从赌场回家。你还需要特别当心周围的环境，如果发现可疑行为要立即报警。如果你步行或乘坐公共交通回家，也需要格外小心，必要时可以在出门前准备一些防范工具，如胡椒喷雾就是一个不错的主意。当你离开赌场时，身上最好不要携带过多的现金。匪徒们通常会假定你在赌场内赢得了充足的金钱。正因为如此，你将匪徒们的抢劫目标。这意味着你必须要谨慎。

　　译文

　　When people talk about casino safety, they usually talk about betting strategy, money management, playing the games with the best odds, avoiding side bets and other such things. Those are important, but there is another type of casino safety that you should keep in mind when you go out for a night of gambling.

　　This morning, Chicago police responded to the alleged assault of a 62-year-old Chinese immigrant who had won more than $10,000 at an Indiana casino. Two armed men assaulted him and stole his money, making off with cash and casino chips. The victim’s son, William Chan, said that he believes the robbers followed his father home from the casino. “Stuff like this seems to be happening more than enough times in Chinatown,” he says. Chan says that many robbers prey on Chinese immigrants who visit the casinos to rob them of their winnings. His father was wounded but not seriously injured.

　　Chan says that type of crime is common in Chinatown, but it happens elsewhere as well. The inside of a casino is pretty safe because of the high level of security, including staff on the floor and cameras covering every inch of the casino. Once you get outside the casino, though, you’re alone on the street and need to take care of yourself. Many cities have a police presence outside the casinos to discourage that kind of crime. The police won’t follow you all the way home, though. For that reason, you need to take your own precautions. If possible, don’t go home from the casino alone. Be aware of your surroundings and try to notice suspicious behavior. Be extra careful if you walk home or take public transit. Carrying something for defense, such as pepper spray, is also a good idea. The bottom line is that when you leave the casino, you are associated with money. Thieves will assume that you either won money or have enough money that you could afford to lose it at the casino. Because of that, you will be a target, which means you have to be cautious.

赌场评论：Montbleu度假村赌场和塔霍湖温泉

作者: 博之道 来源: 未知 时间: 2011-10-27 阅读: 次 博彩译文

　　大约半年前，我有幸访问了Montbleu度假村赌场及霍湖温泉水疗中心，它们处在美丽的内华达山脉下。我们喜欢那里美丽的风光，它们有一流的住宿，可口的饭菜，以及舒适的水疗温泉和令人兴奋的赌场娱乐。

　　该度假村拥有超过430间最高标准的豪华的客房，以及美丽的湖泊和山脉美景。如果你想把您的商务会议移动到您的套房，那么该度假村可以给您提供足够宽敞的的客房，还有大屏幕电视，酒吧和餐饮等功能。

　　Montbleu的温泉水疗中心是旅游的最佳目的地，让您以适合的方式享受桑拿，漩涡浴缸和蒸汽浴室等健身运动。每天我至少费一个小时在温泉放松和调理自己的身体。

　　Montbleu赌场的管理是相当特别的，它能满足所有类型的玩家，包括那些狂赌者。该赌场的提供的游戏包括三卡扑克，德州扑克，迷你百家乐，累积奖金牌九等等。二十一点的牌桌总是很繁忙的，虽然我在二十一点的牌桌上没赢得到一轮的游戏。

　　Montbleu的赌场还提供一些超级娱乐游戏，而且你会发现它的游戏主题十分丰富。

　　此外，该度假村赌场最近还改建了40个新的大屏幕电视和以及一个全天开放的体育馆和图书室。

　　如果你想寻找一个最佳的度假村赌场，我肯定会推荐这个度假村赌场及水疗中心。

　　Casino Review: Montbleu Resort Casino and Spa on Lake Tahoe

　　Around six months ago, I was lucky enough to visit Montbleu Resort Casino and Spa which is set against the backdrop of the beautiful Sierra Nevada Mountains.  I liked the fact that we managed to pack beautiful scenery, great accommodation, delicious meals, pampering spa sessions  and exciting casino entertainment all into four days.

　　The resort boasts over 430 rooms which are of the highest standards of luxury, with exceptional lake and mountain views.  If you want to move your business conference into your suite, you’ll be able to thanks to the big living area, big screen TV, wet bar and dining features.

　　Montbleu’s Onsen Spa is the ultimate pampering destination, allowing you to fit in hours of fitness and relief through exercise devices as well as saunas, whirlpool baths and steam rooms.  I put aside a whole day to spend relaxing in the spa, and managed to fit in at least an hour a day in other treatments throughout my stay.

　　The casino at Montbleu is quite exceptional and manages to cater to all types of players – from those looking for budget entertainment to high rollers. Table games on offer include Three Card Poker, Texas Hold ‘em, Let it Ride, Mini Baccarat and Jackpot Pai Gow Poker.  I did pretty well at the blackjack table and the craps table was always busy, although I didn’t manage to get round to the game.

　　The slot machines at Montbleu’s casino also offer super entertainment, and you’ll find a wide range of different denominations and themes. I tried my luck on Wheel of Fortune, Mega Bucks and Deuces Wild video poker but there were endless others to choose from, all boasting the last word in ‘ticket in, ticket out’ technology.  With the opportunity to wager anything from a nickel to $25 a spin, everyone is catered for here as well.

　　Also, check out The Zone,  a recently remodeled Race and Sports Book Room with 40 new large-screen TVs and all day sports action.

　　I would definitely recommend this resort casino and spa to anybody in the Nevada region of Lake Tahoe.

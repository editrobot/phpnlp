百家乐游戏可以提高拉斯维加斯的收入

作者: 博之道 来源: 未知 时间: 2011-11-08 阅读: 次 博彩译文

　　在经济困难时期，大多数赌场赌博游戏已经进入到低迷时期。百家乐游戏作为一种赌博游戏，却持续上升。12月，在拉斯维加斯赌场的百家乐下注就已经达到13亿。而所有的百家乐赌博游戏，使得赌场赢得了155.7万美元。2009年的收入增长了26.5％，超过970万美元。

　　百家乐游戏是一种高风险的游戏，并在附近地带的17个赌场有提供。百家乐游戏在中国富人当中十分受欢迎。

　　百家乐游戏是从其他的赌场赌博游戏当中分出来的吗？百家乐游戏确实从其他的赌博游戏当中获得一些业务，但这并不是赌博业务下降的唯一原因。百家乐游戏给斯维加斯的博彩业带来了很多新的业务。

　　像Aria这样的新赌场，就因为百家乐游戏而带来了很大的利益。如果10万美元的单手赌注在小赌场发挥，当一个幸运的玩家命中，赌场就已经很难支付。而数百万美元的支出更是小赌场无法支付的。

　　没有百家乐游戏，在拉斯维加斯的博彩业将会大幅度下降。在所有赌博游戏当中，除了百家乐游戏，都下降到2003年的水平。百家乐游戏有助于提高博彩收入的一半以上。百家乐能够帮助博彩业走出经济低迷的状态。

　　新的赌场一般将大的事件运用到百家乐赌场上。像Aria这样的赌场百家乐桌上游戏的展开取得非常成功。中国新年对于桌上游戏来说也是一个大的事件。

　　Baccarat Increases Vegas Revenues

　　In tough economic times most casino gambling has been down. Baccarat is a form of gambling that is on the rise. 1.3 billion was wagered in Las Vegas in December on baccarat. Casinos won 155.7 million dollars on baccarat gambling. Revenue for 2009 was up 26.5 % going over 970 million dollars.

　　Baccarat is a high stakes game that is offered at 17 casinos near the strip. The game is popular with the wealthy Chinese population.

　　Is the draw taking away from other forms of gambling? Baccarat is probably taking some business from other forms of gambling but this is not the only reason the gambling business is down. Baccarat is bringing a lot of new business to the gaming industry in Las Vegas.

　　New casinos like Aria have brought a big interest to the game. With single hand wagers in the 100,000 dollar range little casinos cannot afford to take the hit of a lucky player. Million dollar payouts are possibilities small casinos cannot pay.

　　Without baccarat the gaming industry in Las Vegas would have fallen drastically. Over all gambling, other then baccarat has declined to levels of 2003. Baccarat has single handedly helped improve gaming numbers taking in over half of the gaming revenue. Baccarat is helping the state’s depressed revenue out as well.

　　Big days for the high stake game have been new casino play. Places like Aria have had a very successful opening at the baccarat tables. The Chinese New Year is also a big event at the tables.

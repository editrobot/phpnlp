引领潮流的地方：拉斯维加斯

作者: 博之道 来源: 未知 时间: 2011-10-27 阅读: 次 博彩译文

　　拉斯维加斯始终是一个引领潮流的城市，它有很多与众不同的酒吧，餐馆和商店。拉斯维加斯是一个度假天堂，你可以在那里度过一个美好的假期。下面是笔者给您的一些提示。

　　在哪里停留？

　　当您访问拉斯维加斯，丽都酒店是一个特别值得您停留的地方。该酒店是拉斯维加斯城一家著名的酒店，因为它有一个特别酷的一流的酒吧和餐馆，酒吧里还有一个令人难以置信的两个万串珠晶体制成d 吊灯。在酒吧里，您可以尽情尝试许多美味的鸡尾酒。

　　去哪里聚会的？

　　拉斯维加斯的晚上是热闹非凡的。那里有最棒的夜总会，有最时尚的舞蹈地点。拉斯维加斯是一座著名的不夜城，在那里没有安静的夜晚，只有欢乐的夜晚。

　　去哪里赌博？

　　在拉斯维加斯，有许多著名的超级赌场，有无数访客慕名而来。我最喜欢的赌场是贝拉吉欧赌场，在那里我可以体验到非凡的刺激感。从你走进这个赌场的那一刻起，你就会被一种“任何事情都可能发生”的感觉击中。富丽堂皇，雄伟，壮丽和无懈可击的服务会让你想永远停留在那。贝拉吉欧，是一个引领拉斯维加斯潮流的地方，那里每天都聚集了数以千计的赌徒。

　　凯撒宫是另一个引领拉斯维加斯城市潮流的地方，它波光粼粼的喷泉和令人惊叹的建筑会让你明白，凯撒宫为什么是众多玩家喜爱的赌场。它对所有玩家开放，在那里，你会发现自己与许多顶级的赌场玩家擦肩而过。

　　拉斯维加斯还有很多值得参观的地方，包括米高梅大酒店，曼德勒海湾度假村赌场和卢克索酒店赌场。所有这些都是顶级的度假胜地。

　　Trendsetting Places to Go in Las Vegas

　　Have you ever wondered where the real trendsetters head to when they hit Las Vegas? They are plenty of bars, restaurants and stores in Vegas, but only a few are considered THE places to see and be seen.

　　Where to stay?

　　The Cosmopolitan hotel is a particularly hip place to stay when you visit Las Vegas. The hotel is well sought after because it has a particularly cool collection of top bars and restaurants under one room, including the Chandelier Bar with an incredible two million beaded crystals as its piece de resistance.  Try out cleverly put together cocktail drinks spread across three levels of bar space for a trendsetting experience.

　　Where to party?

　　If you’ve come to Las Vegas to party the night away, then it’s time to boogie your way down to Tao nightclub at the Venetian, which continues to top the bill when it comes to trendy dance locations. This Asian themed club is situated over several levels, boasts stunning Zen features and is hot spot for the beautiful crowd.

　　Where to gamble?

　　There are many super casinos in Las Vegas, but there are a number that really hit the nail on the head when knowing exactly what their visitors are looking for in a gaming experience. My favorite has always been the Bellagio, which gives a new meaning to over-indulgence. From the minute you walk on to the casino floor at this resort, you are hit with a feeling of ‘anything-can-happen’。  The opulence, grandeur, magnificence and impeccable service will make you want to stay forever.  The Bellagio is truly a gem and the trendsetters are here by their hundreds.

　　Caesars Palace is another location where trendsetters head, and its massive Roman columns, sparkling fountains and stunning architecture show you why. Caesars is a favorite among players because it is accessible to all and you’ll find budget oriented players rubbing shoulders with top high rollers.

　　There are many other locations worth visiting in Las Vegas, including the MGM Grand, the Mandalay Bay Resort and Casino and the Luxor Hotel and Casino.  All these  are top resorts that continue to attract jetsetting.

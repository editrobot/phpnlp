宾夕法尼亚11月份老虎机税收保持稳定

作者: 艳艳 来源: 时间: 2012-12-06 阅读: 次 博彩译文

　　宾夕法尼亚赌博控制委员会今天在其网站报告，在宾夕法尼亚赌场，通过发挥老虎机，十一月的收入总额相比一年前产生的略有增长量，据报道，在经营11个赌场的老虎机游戏，十一月的总收入是192512491美元，比2011年11月份的总收入191852271美元，增加了0.34%。2011开满一个月后的赌场比起今年2012年只有10个赌场的11月份，收入低了1.89%。

　　该委员会还报告说，十一月，联邦从老虎机游戏中产生的大量的税收有103691872美元。在宾夕法尼亚，在十一月2012，11家赌场平均数操作老虎机的数量是26356台，2011年11月份是26534台。

　　董事会将在2012年十二月十五日后公布十一月游戏的税收收入。

　　译文：

　　The Pennsylvania Gaming Control Board reported today on its website that total revenue generated through the play of slot machines at Pennsylvania casinos during November rose slightly compared to the amount generated a year ago.

　　According to the report, this November's gross revenue at the 11 operating casinos from slot machine play was $192,512,491 compared to $191,852,271 of gross revenue in November 2011, an increase of 0.34%. In a comparison of just the 10 casinos operating in November 2012 that were also open for the full month of November 2011, revenue was 1.89% lower this year.

　　The Board also reported that the amount of tax revenue generated for the Commonwealth from slot machine play during this November was $103,691,872.

　　The average number of operating slot machines throughout Pennsylvania at the 11 casinos was 26,356 in November 2012 compared to 26,534 at the casinos in November 2011.

　　The Board will release table game revenue for November 2012 after December 15th of this month.

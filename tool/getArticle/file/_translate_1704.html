888计划申请内华达州的网上赌场执照

作者: 博之道 来源: 未知 时间: 2011-11-14 阅读: 次 博彩译文

　　虽然有一些公司已经受到在线扑克丑闻的影响，但在别人看来这是一次难得的发展机遇。当然，这两个事件可能是因果关系，不幸运的是Full Tilt Poker，在黑色星期五事件中导致临时关闭。它是世界上最大的在线扑克室之一，提供好玩有趣的真钱扑克游戏。随着它的关闭，其他在线扑克网站似乎在一夜之间茁壮成长，Full Tilt Poker的成千上万的老玩家急需找到一个安身之所。

　　在Full Tilt Poker多灾多难的时间里，888作为最知名的网上赌博业务品牌之一，希望扩大其业务。目前，888有一个著名的网上赌场，那里提供许多网上赌场游戏，包括一个流行的在线扑克室，在线赌博网站以及在线体育博彩网站。888活跃在网上赌博市场不低于十四年来，自从它在1997年推出以后。目前888现在已经是在网上赌博业的顶级品牌。即使你到网上赌博，你可能已经看到他们在不断地为许多体育赛事提供赞助。在很多体育赛事现场，你都可以看到它作为赞助商的标志。

　　据彭博社报道，888已经获得了美国内华达州扑克许可听证会，有望获得内达华州的网上赌场执照。888已经被认为是世界上第二个Casears。Casears是世界上最大的赌场公司，是拉斯维加斯最知名的品牌之一。尽管目前888还没有获得美国内达华州的许可，但听证会会谈已经进入了另一个较高的会议阶段。根据888副主席表示，他们计划在美国实行新的在线扑克网站发展计划，如果一切顺利，他们将视美国为主要的目标市场。

　　888 set their eyes on a Nevada license

　　While some companies have been affected by the most recent online poker scandals, others seem to be thriving. Of course, the two events might be connected, as Full Tilt Poker was, until the unfortunate incident that led to its temporary shutdown, one of the largest online poker rooms in the whole world, and during the time that real money poker games were unavailable on Full Tilt, the other sites seemed to thrive and their numbers of players practically exploded.

　　In this troubled time, one of the most recognizable names in the online gambling business, 888, is looking to expand thier business. Currently, 888 is one of those sites that feature every single type of online gambling section there is. They have a famous online casino, which is filled with tons of casino games, a popular online poker room, an online bingo site and an online sports betting site. They have been on the market for no less than fourteen years, having launched all they way back in 1997, and they are now among the top brands in the online gambling industry. Even if you’re not that into online gambling, you might have already seen their logo in one of the many sporting events that they constantly sponsor.

　　According to Bloomberg, 888 is in talks to pair up with Caesars Entertainment and obtain a poker license in the U.S. state of Nevada. They are supposedly already in the advanced stages of talks with Casears （the world’s largest casino company and one of the most recognizable names in Las Vegas）， so the deal is not far from becoming a reality. According to the the Deputy Chairman from 888, a mid-autumn release of their new American online poker site is planned, and if all goes well they will be one of the few large online poker sties that are active in the United States.

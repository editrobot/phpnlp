福克斯波赌场会谈

作者: 来源: 博之道 时间: 2011-12-12 阅读: 次 博彩译文

　　美国足球联盟的新英格兰爱国者队的老板鲍勃卡夫，有了一支新的足球队，但他还设想在马萨诸塞州建设一个娱乐中心，并计划把它变成马萨诸塞州的一大亮点。在那里人们不仅可以观看足球比赛，还可以享受包括时髦的酒吧和夜总会，电影院，酒店，零售商店等更多的乐趣。现在，卡夫希望在福克斯波罗建设一个赌场。

　　卡夫和赌场运营商史蒂夫永利赌场计划一起合作投资该项目，但这项计划目前遇到了障碍。根据该城市条例，建筑物的高度限制为300英尺，而赌场的建筑物高度要更高。目前福克斯波罗规划委员会已批准修在该区域建设赌场。

　　上周星期四晚上，数以百计的福克斯波居民出席规划委员会会议以讨论此事。有些居民支持在当地建设赌场的想法，因为这能创造就业机会。居民丹尼尔弗林告诉波士顿先驱报记者，福克斯波镇深陷债务之中，赌场也许能帮助福克斯波避开财政灾难。然而，与会的大多数居民反对赌场的建设。他们担心的不仅是交通问题，还有犯罪增多问题。居民珍妮说：”我在拉斯维加斯和密西西比州等一些赌场密集地区见过一些人间悲剧，我不想在福克斯波罗看见那样的事情发生。”

　　最后规划委员会拒绝了卡夫的投资建议。但这并不一定意味着福克斯波将来没有赌场，只是要在该镇建设赌场可能会面临更大的困境。

　　Foxborough Casino Meets

　　When Bob Kraft, owner of the NFL’s New England Patriots, had a new stadium built for the football team, he envisioned it as an entertainment center that would be a major attraction in Massachusetts. Aside from the football games, which operate only during the season, all year people can go to the stadium and enjoy the fun at Patriot Place, which includes trendy bars and clubs, a movie theater, hotel, retail stores and more. Now Kraft wants to bring a casino to Foxboro as well.

　　Kraft and casino operator Steve Wynn have plans to bring a casino to the stadium, but those plans have hit a roadblock. The city ordinance has a 300-foot height limit for buildings and a casino would be taller than that. The Foxboro planning board would have to approve revising the zoning regulations to allow the casino.

　　Thursday night, hundreds of Foxboro residents attended the planning board’s meeting to discuss the matter. Some of the residents support the idea of a local casino and the jobs it would create. Resident Daniel Flynn told the Boston Herald that the town is deep in debt and a casino could “stave off a fiscal calamity.” Most of the residents in attendance, however, spoke out in opposition to a casino. Opponents worried about traffic problems and increased crime. “I’ve traveled to places like Las Vegas and Mississippi, and I’ve seen human tragedies,” said resident Jeanne Lightbody. “I don’t want to look at that in Foxboro.”

　　The planning board rejected the proposal to remove the high restriction. What that doesn’t necessarily mean there will be no casino, it will make bringing a casino to the town considerably more difficult.

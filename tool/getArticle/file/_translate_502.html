The FA Cup also represents a fantastic opportunity for fans of sports betting as it pits teams from different divisions against each other and even when teams from the same division, with massively differing fortunes, face each other there is no sure outcome –  the FA Cup is thought of as a great leveller.

　　When betting on the winner of the tournament, betting is usually focused on a small group of teams, with Portsmouth’s victory in the 2011 final being the only real shock win since 1995.

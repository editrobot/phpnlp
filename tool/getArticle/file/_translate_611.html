真钱百家乐，网上游戏的完美选择

作者: 博之道 来源: 未知 时间: 2011-10-09 阅读: 次 博彩译文

　　百家乐意味着经典

　　百家乐有这样一个豪华和充满想象的游戏声誉做了比实际上更多的努力。百家乐刚开始是出现在电影或照片的描绘上，现场通常设有一个TUX、饮料和一个总是用gowned和宝石装饰的女人。现实稍微有些不同，赌场做一切都是用来排除掉他们的光环，百家乐可能永远都不会动摇其特殊的地位。百家乐的历史是笼罩在朦胧中的，这只会增加其神秘性。百家乐是在15世纪的意大利发明的，当然，经过多年来的不断演变已经改变了。 “百家乐”（“T”是无声的）这个词是来源于意大利词“baccara”这意味着零，并指所有的牌面数的和从几十到零值。

　　真钱百家乐流行

　　在欧洲，百家乐游戏仍然是最流行的赌场游戏之一。在美国，它掩盖了赌场巨头21点，插槽和扑克。然而，在美国和世界各地，无论是在什么时候，真钱百家乐是越来越多欢迎的，主要是因为网上赌博的到来。

　　百家乐的变化

　　有三种常见的百家乐变种（和许多鲜为人知的版本）：十一点（有时也被称为法国百家乐），百家乐BANQUE（又名双抽穗）和Punto的银行（也称为北美百家乐）。 Punto银行可能是最流行的一种百家乐，它是在古巴开发类似美国百家乐赌场的游戏。 Punto银行可以在不同大小的赌桌上进行。迷你PUNTO银行百家乐有7名玩家，MIDI Punto银行有9名玩家，和Punto大银行则是14玩家。

　　网上赌博百家乐永远改变了原来的面貌

　　尽管百家乐原来传说是一种富人游戏，随着网上赌博的到来，大约是在10年前百家乐流传到群众中。现在，可以从1美元的低点开始的赌注，来自各行各业的普通人都可以尽情在真钱百家乐中发挥。百家乐是最好的赌场游戏的赔率和易用性方面何以的游戏之一。游戏的目的很简单：玩家押注，把赌注压在赌桌上，在庄家或玩家中的一方。接近9的就是胜利者。百家乐有一个非常低的赌场优势。如果玩家下注，赌场优势约1.36％；如果你对庄家打赌，是一个更好的为1.17％。 （有一个小的“税”，以弥补这一差异。）

　　百家乐是网上赌博的完美选择

　　尽管百家乐也很流行，但是赌场百家乐也起到了促进的作用。大多数百家乐领域都是离散的封锁（想象一下红色天鹅绒绳索，你会得到这样的图片），荷官都穿着tuxedoes而玩家们都是随时准备厮杀的状态。不过在现实中，百家乐只是一个充满着机遇的游戏。如果你会一个角子机游戏你就会玩百家乐。事实上，它是如此简单易学，已成功过渡到互联网。数以百万计的人从来没有玩过百家乐，或一些赌徒也没有玩真钱百家乐，它已在过去十年重生，这一事件被称为网上赌博爆炸。

　　Baccarat will Always Mean Classy

　　Baccarat's reputation for being a posh and fancy game has more to do with presentation than fact. When baccarat is depicted in movies or photographs, the scene usually features a guy outfitted with a tux and a drink and a woman who is always gowned and bejeweled. Reality is a tad different, yet casinos do everything they can to nurture the aura of exclusivity and Baccarat will probably never shake its special status. Baccarat's history is shrouded in obscurity, which only adds to its mystique. Baccarat was invented in Italy in the 15th century; it has, of course, changed over the years and is constantly evolving. The word "baccarat" （the "t" is silent） is derived from the Italian word 'baccara," meaning zero, and refers to the zero value given to all of the face cards and tens.

　　Real Money Baccarat Gains Popularity

　　In Europe, the game of baccarat remains one of the most popular casino games. In the U.S. it is overshadowed by the casino giants - blackjack, slots, and poker. Nonetheless, in the States and the world over, real money Baccarat is gaining in popularity all the time, due mostly to the advent of online gambling.

　　Baccarat Variations

　　There are three common variants of baccarat （and many lesser-known versions）： Chemin de Fer （sometimes known French baccarat or railway）， Baccarat Banque （a.k.a. deux tableau） and Punto Banco （also known as North American baccarat）。 Punto Banco is probably the most popular form of baccarat; it was developed in Cuba and is similar to American baccarat in that the casino always banks the game. Punto Banco can be played at different sized tables - mini Punto Banco baccarat has 7 players; midi Punto Banco has 9 players; and large Punto Banco has 14 players.

　　Online Gambling has Changed the Face of Baccarat Forever

　　Despite Baccarat's reputation as a game for the wealthy, baccarat came down to the masses about 10 years ago, along with the advent of online gambling. Now, with wagers starting as low as $1, regular folks from all walks of life can enjoy playing baccarat for real money. Baccarat is one of the best casino games available in terms of odds and ease. The object of the game is simple: players bet on either of two hands dealt on the table, the player or the banker. The hand that comes closer to 9 wins. Baccarat has a very low house edge. If you bet on the player, the house edge is about 1.36%; if you bet on the banker the edge is an even better 1.17%. （There is a small "tax" to compensate for that difference.）

　　Baccarat is Perfect for Online Gambling

　　Despite online baccarat's populist approach, casino baccarat still fosters the air of glamour. Most baccarat areas are discretely sealed off （imagine red velvet ropes and you'll get the picture）， the dealers are dressed in tuxedoes and the players are dressed to kill. In reality, though, baccarat is just a game of luck - a glorified slot game, if you will. The fact that it is so easy to learn has made its transition to the Internet a huge success. Millions of gamblers who would never have played baccarat otherwise, are playing real money baccarat; it has had a major rebirth in the last ten years, thanks to the explosion known as online gambling.

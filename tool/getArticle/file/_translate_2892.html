关于皇家赌场的评论

作者: 来源: 互联网 时间: 2011-12-14 阅读: 次 博彩译文

　　简介

　　“皇家赌场”是位于拉斯维加斯大道上黄金地段的一个非常小的赌场。赌场为游客而建设在这样的一个黄金地带位置绝对是一个符合经济计划的做法。这一个地方的运行并不是非常的顺利，那里聚集了那些可能在其他地带有着较高的最低投注赌场碰壁的红筹投注玩家。

　　地点

　　皇家赌场处在威尼斯和Harrah之间的一小块地方。马路对面直接是海市蜃楼。距离凯撒宫，金银岛，永利这几个赌场都非常的近。

　　赌博

　　根据《2009年的美国赌场指南》里面对皇家赌场的描述，皇家赌场拥有17,500平方英尺的赌场空间。通过对比的方式，我们可以惊讶的发现156023的米高梅赌场比它高近9倍之多。据内华达州的博彩管制局介绍，皇家赌场共有14张赌桌，还有445台老虎机。而米高梅大酒店则拥有178张赌桌和2470台老虎机。在皇家赌场并不会提供现场扑克。

　　红筹玩家在“皇家赌场”都感到非常的受欢迎。在那里，我经常通过扫描表看到有许多的玩家在打赌，看起来像是一片红色的海洋。你在任何地方都很难看到绿色筹码，黑色的更少。如果你是一个$ 5 - $ 10的玩家，你应该可以在皇家赌场找到一个赌桌，而不需要像跳蚤那样走来走去。

　　在“皇家赌场”的庄家，非常友好，并乐于助人。他们中占很大一部分的庄家都已经牙齿缺失，但他们往往是更好的庄家。在“皇家赌场”，低限注的赌博游戏似乎更加吸引那些没有多少游戏战略的年轻玩家。一些荷官，尤其是一个名为迈克尔的，当玩家坐在赌桌上感到非常困惑时遇到他，是非常有益的。即便你无所要求，他们也会提供有益的建议。在那里，所有的荷官都是如此。

　　在“皇家赌场”，鸡尾酒对玩家来说是相当不错的服务。那里的女士们也许并不年轻，但是她们的速度都是非常快速的，并且态度很友好。但是，我并不会为了一杯鸡尾酒而选择一个破烂的玻璃杯，这就是我的生活态度。因为，那所谓的鸡尾酒，品尝一下，感觉一半是醋。如果你是一个21点玩家，也可以到“皇家赌场”玩。那里有14个赌桌，有6个就是用来玩21点游戏的。而大多数的赌场通常只是有一个21点的赌桌。

　　Casino Royale Review

　　Introduction

　　The Casino Royale is very small casino located in a prime location on the Las Vegas Strip. This is definitely a place for tourists who are on the economy plan yet insist on a prime Strip location. The place is rather run down yet packed with red-chip betting players who are likely rebuffed by higher minimum bets at other Strip casinos.

　　Location

　　The Casino Royale occupies a small parcel between the Venetian and Harrah's. Directly across the street is the Mirage. Caesars Palace, Treasure Island, and the Wynn are all within easy walking distance.

　　Gambling

　　According to the 2009 American Casino Guide , the Casino Royale has 17,500 square feet of casino space. By way of comparison, the MGM has almost 9 times as much at 156,023. According to the Nevada Gaming Control Board , the Casino Royale has 14 table games, which is also what I counted, and 445 slots. By way of comparison, the MGM Grand has 178 table games and 2,470 slots. There is no live poker.

　　Red chip players are made to feel very welcome at the Casino Royale. While there, I regularly scanned the tables to see how much players were betting, and it was a sea of red. Not a green chip anywhere, much less black. If you are a $5-$10 player, you should have no trouble finding a table at the Casino Royale, and you won't be made to feel like a flea.

　　The dealers at the Casino Royale run the gamut from brick walls to being very friendly and helpful. A disproportionate number of them had missing teeth, but they tended to be the better dealers. The low limits at the Casino Royale seem to attract younger players, many of who are clueless about proper strategy. Some of the dealers, especially one named Michael, were very helpful when such players just sat there looking perplexed. They would offer helpful advice, without having to be asked. That is how all dealers should be.

　　Cocktail service was pretty good at the Casino Royale. The ladies were perhaps not as young and pretty as at the big Strip properties, but they were just as quick and friendly as anywhere. However, I did order a glass of red wine, which was the worst glass of wine I've had in my life. It tasted like half port, half vinegar. If you are a Blackjack Switch player, then you'll be in Heaven at the Casino Royale. Of the 14 table games, six of them were Blackjack Switch. Most big casinos will have, at most, one such table.

大西洋城狂欢赌城重新开张，流行天后碧昂丝演唱助阵

作者: 博之道 来源: 时间: 2012-03-22 阅读: 次 博彩译文

　　大西洋城的官员一直在等待一个时机振兴该城的博彩业。但是在过去的几年里大西洋城的博彩魅力渐失，因为赌徒们有其他赌博目的地可以选择，如马里兰州或宾夕法尼亚州。

　　大西洋城狂欢赌城经过前段时间的休整，即将于四月重新开门营业，狂欢赌城为此做了充分准备，邀请了美国著名流行天后级歌手碧昂丝·吉赛尔·诺斯出场助阵开幕式。万千赌徒的目光又将再次聚焦在大西洋城。

　　碧昂丝将于5月25日至27日在狂欢赌城举办盛大的演唱会，以吸引音乐爱好者的进入狂欢赌场度假村。

　　狂欢赌场可以为游客们提供1100个酒店房间，以及提供当地人的一些餐饮服务和娱乐服务。大西洋城狂欢赌场的重新营业，为新泽西州的居民提供了超过5000个就业岗位，这使许多失业居民重新燃起了就业希望。

　　预计今年新泽西州的博彩市场将迎来一次大冲击。许多分析家的预测，宾夕法尼亚州的博彩收入将在2012年度赶超新泽西州。大西洋城的赌场高层管理者，希望借狂欢赌场的重新开张， 扭转新泽西州博彩市场当前面临的不利形势。

　　自经济衰退以来，美国各州的许多议员纷纷倾向于发展博彩业来恢复经济。大西洋城和拉斯维加斯将不得不及时采取措施，以应对数亿美元博彩收入流入美国其他州地区。

　　Atlantic City officials have been waiting for a buzz that could help revive the city's gaming industry. Over the past several years, the city has lost its clout among gamblers, with many choosing more convenient locations such as Maryland or Pennsylvania.

　　Enter the new Revel casino. The multi-billion dollar gaming and entertainment destination is being looked at as the savior for the AC gaming industry, and Revel has assured that at least during the weekend of its grand opening, all eyes and ears will be again on Atlantic City.

　　Technically, Revel will open its doors for the first time in the beginning of April, but it will be Memorial Day weekend when all the grand opening festivities will take place. Beyonce, one of the hottest musical acts in the world, will help bring customers in during the opening when she plays concerts on May 25th-27th. The concerts will serve as a way to introduce music fans to the new casino resort.

　　Revel will offer visitors 1,100 hotel rooms, with several dining and entertainment options for vacationers and locals alike. The casino is also responsible for adding over 5,000 employees to the New Jersey workforce. That figure is one that many New Jersey residents are glad to see.

　　New Jersey is expected to lose their designation as the top gaming market on the East Coast by the end of this year. Pennsylvania, by many analysts' predictions, will surpass New Jersey in terms of overall gaming revenue by the end of 2012. Casino executives in Atlantic City are hopeful that Revel helps buck the trend of gamblers traveling to Pennsylvania to enjoy casinos.

　　Since the economic recession, many lawmakers around the US have turned to casino gambling to help revive their economy's. Atlantic City and Las Vegas have had to deal with the brunt of that expansion, losing hundreds of millions of dollars in gaming revenue to other states.

百家乐与掷骰子游戏

作者: 博之道 来源: 未知 时间: 2011-10-08 阅读: 次 博彩译文

　　掷骰子游戏是最流行的离线赌场游戏之一。然而，很少有人知道有一场伟大的比赛，能够像百家乐一样令人兴奋的。虽然打法有很大的不同，但都需要聪明的人， 看谁想要得到乐趣，从而不怕风险，希望在赌博中中大奖或者获得一些现金。

　　掷骰子游戏基础

　　在掷骰子时，游戏主要是围绕着一对骰子。玩家尝试推出第一名册上一个幸运的7。如果11个不上来，无论是推出什么都成为一点。这是一场比赛，看到的点是否会是7从而赢得比赛。然而，这一次七是一个失败的数字。掷骰子是一个有趣的一方面是没有涉及更多的玩家。

　　为什么百家乐可以更精彩

　　在双骰子，你不知道是什么号码，可能会希望出现的骰子让你兴奋。在真钱百家乐游戏中，你必须弄清楚这两方打牌谁有最大的机会使牌面值接近或等于9。若是算数更好的，你将有更多的边缘。当然，使得游戏无法预测的是，所有的“十”卡和“面子”卡都不计算在内。此外，任何数额超过十的列数都只算单位数。换句话说，如果你的牌点添加到十七，你实际上的只是7。由于七是接近九，你有一个夺冠的好牌。

　　玩家投注

　　在这两场比赛中，你不必把赌注放在主要的玩家上。在掷骰子游戏中，不要管玩家是谁。你决定你想要的投注。你甚至可以打赌玩家失去而“庄家”赢得。在百家乐游戏中，你选择一方来下注。你可以打赌庄家或玩家这一方。这是唯一你可以在赌场积极发挥的游戏。

　　九是常见的骰子，在百家乐游戏中则很少见

　　从科学的角度分析，当您掷出两个骰子，九会出现的是最常见的数字。如果你在玩百家乐游戏中出现的是九，你会有很好的机会取胜。但在百家乐游戏中，这可能不太常见，但如果是这样他们几乎都是赢家。如果双方都有九个时，此时的9才不赢。然后，它被认为是和局，所有的赌注都返回。总体而言，7跟9一样是幸运数字。事实上，在这些比赛中，获得前九到七可能会有赢得上千元和失去五百元的差异。

　　Baccarat and Craps

　　Craps is one of the most popular casino games around, both on and offline. Yet few people know there is a great game that is just as exciting called Baccarat. While the style of play is very different, both require clever people who want to have fun and are not afraid to risk some cash in hopes of hitting a big cash jackpot.

　　Craps Basics

　　In craps, the game revolves around a pair of dice. The shooter tries to roll a lucky seven on the first roll. If seven or eleven doesn't come up, whatever number is rolled becomes the point. It is then a race to see whether the point will come up and everyone wins or a seven will be rolled. This time seven is a losing number. An interesting aspects of craps is that there are no playing cards involved.

　　Why Baccarat Can Be Even More Exciting

　　In Craps you have the excitement of not knowing what number may come up on the dice. In real money Baccarat, you must figure out which of two hands of playing cards has the greatest chance of equaling the number nine. The better you are at math, the more of an edge you will have. Of course, what makes the game impossible to predict is that all "ten" cards and "face" cards are not counted. Additionally, any amount over ten is changed to reflect the number in the ones column. In other words, if your hand adds up to seventeen, you really have a seven. Since seven is close to nine, you have a good shot at winning.

　　Bet on the shooter, Bet on the Hand

　　In both games, you do not have to be the main player to place wagers. In craps, it doesn't matter who the shooter is. You decide what you want to bet on. You can even bet on the shooter to lose and the "House" to win. In Baccarat, you have the choice of which hand to bet on. You can bet with the "Player's" hand or on the "Bank's" hand. These are two of the only games where you can actively play with the casino.

　　Nine is Common in Craps, Less So in Baccarat

　　Mathematically, when you roll two dice, nine is one of the most common numbers that can come up. If you roll nine as your "come out" roll, you are in good position to win. In Baccarat, nines may be less common, but they are almost always a winner. The only times a nine does not win is if both hands have a nine. Then it is considered a push and all bets are returned. The point is, overall, nine is just as lucky as number seven. In fact in these games, getting a nine before a seven may be what makes the difference between winning a thousand dollars and losing five hundred.

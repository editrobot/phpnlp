美国推出“5卡现金”全新彩票游戏

作者: 博之道 来源: 时间: 2012-02-10 阅读: 次 博彩译文

　　近日，美国肯塔基州彩票机构放出消息，该州将于2月12日推出全新彩票游戏“5卡现金”，该款彩票游戏方式和规则与传统乐透型彩票有着极大的不同——应用带有图案符号的纸牌作为开奖“数字”，并且与即开型彩票打了个擦边球。“‘5卡现金’与传统的类似‘匹克3’和‘强力球’彩票不一样，以前的彩票都是需要玩家选择数字，等待一次开奖结果，而一张‘5卡现金’包含5张印有图案符号的纸牌，玩家只需看开奖，核对开奖的纸牌是否与手中彩票的纸牌相匹配。”肯塔基州彩票机构营销部门副总裁史蒂夫·卡西比尔介绍道。

　　据了解，“5卡现金”彩票包含的内容并不是传统乐透型彩票的“数字”，而是带有图案符号的“纸牌”。该款彩票须由新型终端机出票，票价格为每张2美元。“5卡现金”彩票包含5张印有图案符号的纸牌，周一至周六每天开奖，每天开出一张中奖纸牌，每周共计开出6张中奖纸牌。购买该款彩票的玩家有两次获奖机会：彩票的5张纸牌中出现两个对子或者同花顺时，玩家赢得即时奖金5000美元；彩票的5张纸牌与开奖结果的6张中奖纸牌相比较，匹配2张至5张均可获得奖金，奖金金额范围是5美元至10万美元。

　　Recently, the Kentucky Lottery agency news release, the state will launch a new lottery game 5 card cash on February 12, methods and rules of this type of lottery games with traditional Lotto has a great difference - Application with pictorial symbols of the cards as lottery numbers ", and called walking a fine line and instant lottery. '5 Card cash 'similar' Pick 3 'and the' Powerball 'lottery not the same as the previous lottery players choose the number, wait for one lottery results, a '5 card cash' contains five printed pictorial symbol cards, players only need to look at the lottery, check lottery cards to match the cards with the hands of the lottery. "Kentucky lottery marketing vice president Steve Casey Bill said.

　　It is understood that the "5 card cash lottery contains content not in the traditional Lotto" numbers ", but" playing cards "with pictorial symbols. Which the lottery by the new terminal ticket, the ticket price of $ 2 each. 5 card cash lottery includes 5 cards printed pictorial symbols, Monday to Saturday daily lottery, daily out of a winning cards, and a week out of a total of six winning cards. Purchasing this lottery players have two award-winning opportunities for two pair or a straight flush: five cards in lottery, the player to win instant prize of $ 5,000; the lottery five cards and lottery results 6 winning cards compared matches 2-5 will be eligible for a bonus, the bonus amounts range is 5-100000 U.S. dollars.

网上赌博资金管理策略

作者: 博之道 来源: 未知 时间: 2011-09-24 阅读: 次 博彩译文

　　管理网上赌博的资金，最重要的一点是你要远离胜利者，平衡，是理财的艺术。简单的真理是，艺术地赢得金钱又避免失去太多。

　　撇开运气或偶尔的大胜利，成功的网上赌博很大程度上就是赢在“平衡”这一点上。

　　保持清醒的头脑

　　避免两分钟不合理的投注损失一个小时精心积累的奖金。我们都知道这些情况，你已经玩了一会儿，赢得了一点钱，足够玩一晚上所获得的价值。然后你决定赌一点直觉上。几乎在眨眼的时间，你失去了你的所有。所以，请保持你的冷静和你的理性。

　　玩你能支付的钱

　　事先决定总金额，您可以提交您在线赌博一个月的金额，不影响您的预算。它叫做娱乐津贴。不要把它与其他货币（您的赌场帐户显然是一个完美的地方），并使用它作为您的极限。如果它运行前的一个月结束，那么你的博彩娱乐为一个月。无论你做什么，不要被诱惑，如果事情变得黑暗，你就要承受损失更多钱的风险。

　　设置一个损失极限

　　如果你有麻烦，你会失去比你想试图弥补的损失多更多。设置一个特定的损失点，决定一个最高金额，当你到达时，请记得退出。明天还会是全新的一天。

　　当你累时请不要玩

　　一部分资金管理是确保你保持警惕，避免本来可以避免的错误。所以如果你开始觉得无聊，想添加一些额外的兴奋而增加你的赌注，这将可能导致你的资金损失。如果你感觉到疲倦了，就停下来吧。厌倦和疲劳是资金的杀手。

　　玩的几率

　　条纹又放在一边，你很有可能会失去。你赢了，所以减少下一个赌你赢。如果你保持你的赌注不变的话，你可能会失去更多，因为你预期的连续赢两次的几率很小。赌注减半后再下赌注，你可能会失去，但并不多。记住，资金管理，尽量减少你的损失。

　　不要追赶

　　你的一个最大的错误就是立即设法弥补损失。你决定下赌注，希望赢回你的钱，但不意味着赌博神同意，更有可能的是，你会失去更多。挽回损失的途径是通过耐心和精明的游戏，而不是一个幸运的降临。

　　这些建议将有助于你更成功地管理你的资金。最后一件事——虚拟性质的在线赌博意味着它很容易忽视这样一个事实：每次点击代表真正的金钱，屏幕上显示的数字代表着你正在玩的现金。

　　Successful Online Gambling Money Management

　　Money management in online gambling

　　One of the most important areas to master if you're going to walk away a winner, on balance, is the art of money management. The simple truism is that the art of winning money gambling online is about not losing too much.

　　Setting aside the lucky streaks or the occasional big win, successful online gambling is largely about winning a bit here, losing a bit there, and coming out ahead at the end of the session. Poker players call this 'grinding it out'.

　　Keep your head

　　Avoid blowing an hour's worth of carefully accumulated winnings in 2 minutes of irrational betting. We all know these scenarios - you've been playing for a while and have won a little money - enough to make the evening worthwhile. Then you decide to bet a little more on a hunch, or get an unlucky card at the blackjack table and try to make up the loss on the very next hand. And it doesn't pay off. Almost in the blink of eye, you've lost what you painstakingly gained, and a winning session has become a losing one. Keep your cool and stay rational.

　　Only play with money you can afford to lose

　　Decide beforehand on the total amount of money that you can currently commit to your online gambling in a given month - effectively, money that you can afford to lose and not feel an impact in your budget. Call it an entertainment allowance. Keep it separated from your other money (your casino account is obviously a perfect place), and use it as your limit. If it runs out before the month ends, then that's your gambling entertainment for the month. Whatever you do, don't be tempted to add to it, because that's when things become murky, and you start to risk more money than you should.

　　Set a loss limit

　　If you have trouble stopping a session when you're losing, and you tend to lose more than you wanted to in trying to make up for your losses, set a specific loss point. Decide on a maximum amount you're prepared to lose in a session and when you reach that, shut down the casino. Tomorrow is another day.

　　Don't play when you're tired

　　Part of money management is making sure that you're always alert and that you avoid making otherwise avoidable mistakes because you're bored or tired. So if you start feeling bored and tempted to add some extra excitement by increasing your bets, or you find yourself making silly bet decisions because you're feeling fatigued, just stop. Boredom and fatigue are bankroll killers.

　　Play with the odds

　　Streaks aside, again, you are more likely to lose just after you've won, so decrease your next bet after you've won. If you keep your betting constant, you'll lose more, because on average you can expect to win twice in a row less often than not. By halving that next bet, you're still losing, but not as much. Remember, money management is about minimizing your losses.

　　Don't play catch-up

　　One of the biggest mistakes you can make is to immediately try to make up for a loss on the next couple of bets. Many players double up when they've lost to try and quickly recoup their loss. Just because you decide that the next bet is the one you're going to win your money back on, doesn't mean that the gambling gods agree. More likely, you'll just lose more. The way to recoup your losses is through patience and shrewd play, not by hoping for a lucky hit.

　　These few tips should go a long way in helping you manage your bankroll more successfully. One final thing - the virtual nature of online gambling means that it's easy to lose sight of the fact that each click represents real money - you're not even handling chips. Stay in the moment, and treat that number on the screen for what it is - your own real cash that you're playing with.

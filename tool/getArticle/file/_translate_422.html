真钱赛马也能找乐子？

作者: 博之道 来源: 未知 时间: 2011-09-24 阅读: 次 博彩译文

　　赛马是一个古老的和值得信赖的运动。真钱赛马赌博这也是其伟大的一个地方。人们为了去看比赛而盛装打扮，去享受社会生活和享受人生的乐趣。一些人发现自己被吸引到比赛的兴奋中去，并有机会把真正的钱投注在自己喜爱的马上。但是，如果你住在一个附近没有任何赛道的地方，或者如果对支付真钱赌博的比赛不感兴趣的时候，你该如何？

　　虚拟真钱赛马

　　在线赛车，当然是这些问题的答案。在线和虚拟赛马，是非常有趣的。你可以享受没有灰尘的赛马运动！当坐在自己舒适的家里，你可以在全世界进行真钱赛马赌博或着创建自己的虚拟赛马。具体方法如下：

　　网上赛马

　　在线赛马是与玩在线赌场类似。你决定你想要赌什么，然后你就可以下赌注，接着你就等着看自己是否赢了。所不同的是，你是在投注现场的行动！许多网站都提供了75至100场比赛，你可以在任何特定时间，押在来自世界各地的赌注，这是真实的马、真实的赛马师和真正的比赛。您要先自己进行比赛研究，并决定你要放置您的真钱的赌注。每次下注都会显示你的优势表和纳税表，用来分析你赢的可能性。当然，如果你输了，那么你就会失去你的赌注。如果你赢了，那么奖金很大的一部分将会取决于那场比赛的赔率和投注的那匹马。

　　在赛马比赛中，网上赛马是一个很棒的方式，我们无需穿戴整齐去参与赛马运动。您没有必要找最近的赛道，只需要到达最近的计算机！

　　虚拟赛马为取乐还是为了赚取真正的钱

　　另一个有趣的活动，其中包括赛马而且是虚拟的赛马。许多网站都提供一个虚拟赛马的途径，来免费享受或需要支付真正的金钱。虚拟赛马中你有许多选择，你可以选择你的赛马的技能，您想要使用的马和赔率。在一个虚拟的赛马网站里，都会给你提供所有的信息，包括赛马的背景和所选的马的真实历史的反映。然后，它就可以在赛道上设置您的团队并且看看他们是怎么做的！

　　虚拟赛马是可能接触到的游戏中的一个快节奏而又有趣的方式。它涉及创造性，独创性，当然，还有一些运气。并能保持你玩的兴趣和在其中的享受。

　　参与赛马要玩得开心。变化是生活的调味品，为什么不尝试改变一点不同的东西？

　　Horse Racing for Real Money or for Fun?

　　Horse racing is an old and trusted sport - and it's also a great place for real money gambling. People have been getting dressed up for ages to go to the races and to enjoy the social life and the enjoyment there. Others have found themselves drawn to the races for the excitement and the chance to place a real money bet on their favorite horse. What do you do, however, if you don't live anywhere near a race track or if you aren't interested in paying to go to the races for your real money gambling?

　　Virtual Real Money Horse Racing

　　Online racing, of course, is the answer to these questions. Online and virtual horse racing are very interesting and growing ways to enjoy the sport of horse racing without the dust! While sitting in the comfort of your own home, you can either gamble for real money on horse racing going on around the world, or create your own virtual horse race. Here's how:

　　Online Horse Racing

　　Online horse racing is similar to playing at an online casino. You decide what you want to bet on, you place your bet, and then you wait to see if you've won. The difference is that you're betting on a live action! Many websites offer 75 to 100 races that you can bet on at any given time, from around the world. These are real races with real horses and jockeys. You research into the races and decide where you want to place your real money bet. Each bet shows you the odds of winning and the payout table if you do win. Of course, if you lose, then you lose your wager. If you win, then the winnings will depend a great deal on the odds for that race and that horse.

　　Online horse racing is a great way to feel involved in the sport of horse racing, without having to get dressed for the races. You don't have to find the nearest race track - just the nearest computer!

　　Virtual Horse Racing for Fun or For Real Money

　　Another fun activity that involves horse racing is virtual horse racing. Many websites offer a way to enjoy virtual horse racing either for free or for real money. You have many decisions to make in virtual horse racing. You select your jockey's skills, the horse you want to use, and the odds you want to play with. All of the information that you are presented on a virtual horse racing site is a true reflection of the jockey's background and the horse's history. Then, it's time to set your team out on the race track and to see how they do!

　　Virtual horse racing is a fast paced and interesting way to involve yourself in a gaming opportunity. It's involves creativity, ingenuity, and, of course, some luck and will keep you on your toes as you play and enjoy.

　　Have fun getting involved with horse racing. Variety is the spice of life - why not try something a little different for a change?

詹姆斯邦德的赌场系统

作者: 博之道 来源: 未知 时间: 2011-10-19 阅读: 次 博彩译文

　　著名的詹姆斯邦德是一个热衷于玩扑克游戏的人。我们认为007已经是詹姆斯邦德的一个代表字符，这位扑克玩家高手一直热衷在摩纳哥、和拉斯维加斯和加勒比海地区玩扑克游戏。在70年代之前，他甚至是澳门有名的债券指挥官，是一个扑克专家。

　　在皇家赌场，邦德面对的是与百家乐狂赌者SMERSH的利害关系，邦德试图把他带离赌场。在“皇家赌场”中，他扮演一个解Labouchère系统，甚至赌注进展系统的人系统。他是如何发挥的呢？

　　游戏甚至金钱投注（红色或黑色，1-18或19-36）写在一张纸上的一个数字序列（比如1至5），然后再开始你的投注。请将您的第一个赌注，红色的顶部和底部的数字的总和，等于说您的进展是5 +1 = 6（类似于做数学题）。如果你赢了，重拳出击5和1的序列，然后赌未来的顶部和底部的号码的总和，再次出现4 + 2 = 6这种情况。

　　如果你输了，添加数列底部，等于弥补你的损失。你的下一个赌注，将顶部和底部没有剔除你的序列号码的总和。当所有的数字都被打掉，你的胜利将等于你原有的五个数字序列中的所有号码（在这个例子中为15）的总和。此时，您再次启动或关闭游戏。

　　当然，牢记你的运气，可能会遇到的其他方式，在这种情况下，你的进展可能会很顺利，你的赌注可能会越来越大。始终有一个止损点，当您退出时，并说：“够了，今天不是我的幸运日”。

　　一个Labouchére提示：防止你的赌注加速过快，负载低的数字序列，例如1,1,1,2,2。

　　James Bond?s Casino System. The Labouchère Progression

　　James Bond is famously keen on a flutter in the casinos. If there has been a character that has done more to glamorise casinos and Monaco we can?t think of one- 007 has always been a keen games player in Monaco, Vegas and The Carribean. He was even in Macau in the 70s before the boom

　　Commander Bond is an expert card player and gambler （think Casino Royale and Moonraker which both feature tense card scenes）。

　　In Casino Royale Bond plays high stakes baccarat against a SMERSH high roller and tries to take him to the cleaners. As well as Baccarat and Bridge, Bond is partial to Roulette: the European variant obviously. In Casino Royale he plays progression systems on the even money bets- a system know as the Labouchère system. This is the basics of how to play the system:

　　Play the even money bets （red or black, odd or even, 1-18 or 19-36） & write a sequence of numbers （say 1 to 5） on a piece of paper before you start your betting. Place your first bet, say on the red , equal to the sum of the top and bottom figures in your progression- （ 5 +1 = 6）。 If you win, strike out the 5 and the 1 on the sequence and then bet the sum of the next top and bottom numbers – so 4 + 2 = 6 again in this case.

　　If you lose, add the number that equals your loss at the bottom of your column. Your next bet would be the sum of the top and bottom numbers not struck off your sequence. When all the numbers have been struck off, your win will equal the sum of all the numbers in your original five number sequence （in this example 15）。  At this point you start again or switch games or tables. Or you head out of the casino.

　　Of course, bear in mind that your luck might run the other way, in which case your progression will get longer and longer and your bets larger and larger. Always have a stop loss- a point when you quit and say “enough is enough- today is not my lucky day”。

　　A Labouchére tip: to keep your bets from ramping up too quickly, load your sequence with low numbers. you might have 1,1,1,2,2 for example.

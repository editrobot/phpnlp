澳门新濠锋水疗温泉，游戏后的好去处

作者: 博之道 来源: 未知 时间: 2011-10-24 阅读: 次 博彩译文

　　新濠锋温泉是澳门放松身心最吸引人的地方，那里可以让你将世界上其它的休闲地方抛之脑后。你可以感觉到烦恼随之飘走，而你正在浸泡在幸福当中，同时得到放松。

　　澳门以世界级的温泉而闻名，它们是经过一天游戏之后放松的最好地方。在澳门有许多美妙的温泉，但是，位于新濠锋赌场（正式皇冠）的新濠锋温泉毫无疑问是最好的温泉之一。2009年，在亚洲水疗和健康节，新濠锋水疗赢得了最佳酒店水疗中心的称号。一旦你感受到那里的豪华气派和享受到那里的一流服务，你就会明白为什么每个人都一直在对水疗赞口不绝。

　　没有人会对其大小产生抱怨。新濠锋温泉超过6000平方米，是澳门最大的温泉之一。当你在温泉或蒸汽浴室放松，你可以同时欣赏到澳门半岛的壮丽景色。新濠锋温泉的风格是体现当代奢华之风的，可以很确切地说，他们现在已经超过了这个期望。

　　他们会提供广泛的服务范围，会迎合每一个挑剔的客户。在一个巨大的空间澳门湿活力池内有最先进的水疗设施：水晶蒸汽室，桑拿浴室，冰喷泉，拉苏尔淋浴。还有一个大的空间为个人和夫妇服务。

　　客人可以根据自己的需要来选择适合自己的治疗方式，或者他们可以选择一个“签名治疗”或“征途”。友好的新濠锋工作人员会安排好各种水疗，能够相得益彰，给你一个遍布身体的体验。

　　按摩分为两类：增添活力和补给。新濠锋温泉专家可以为你驱赶走所有疼痛，此外还有对准妈妈的轻柔按摩。身体磨砂和敷体按摩是完美的补充治疗，为你的皮肤给予混合原料成分与精油的补充。

　　你还可以尝试6个面部护理，包括抗衰老，修复，水化，甚至还有为嘉宾量身定制的服务。沙龙服务，在这里，您可以尽情享受一系列的完整服务，手部护理，足部护理，美发，染发，上蜡或化妆。这样的服范围是非常广阔的。

　　澳门水疗中心提供的服务范围远远超过大多数西方竞争对手，你会为那里的服务水平感到惊喜，同时，那里的合理价格也会让你感到惊讶。

　　新濠锋温泉开放时间是上午10时至午夜十二时，沙龙则是从上午10时至晚上八时开放。如果您真的想去，建议提前预订。

　　Relax at the Spa

　　The Altira Spa is one of Macau’s finest places to relax, unwind and forget about the rest of the world. Feel your troubles float away as you soak into blissful relaxation.

　　Macau is famous for its world class spas and they are the best place to unwind and relax after a day of gaming. There are plenty of wonderful spas in Macau and one of the best is without doubt the Altira Spa, located at the Altira Casino （formally known as Crown）。 Altira Spa won the prestigious Best Hotel Spa in Asia at the Asia Spa and Wellness Festival in 2009. Once you have treated yourself to the luxury and services on offer you’ll understand why this is the spa everyone has been talking about.

　　No one could complain about its size - the Altira Spa is generously spread over 6000 square metres making it one of the largest spas in Macau. While you relax in the spa or steam room you can take in the stunning views of the Macau Peninsula. The Altira Spa’s ethos is ‘to combine luxury in a contemporary setting’ and it is safe to say they have exceeded this expectation.

　　The range of services on offer is extensive and caters to a discerning clientèle. There is a huge range of the most sophisticated wet spa facilities in Macau - vitality pools, crystal steam rooms, saunas, an ice fountain, rasul and experience showers. There is also a huge range of services for individuals and couples.

　　Guests can choose their own treatment or they can opt for a ‘signature treatment’ or ‘journey’ that combines a package taking all the guess work out of your treatment decision. The friendly Altira staff have designed treatments to complement each other to give you an all over body experience.

　　The massages fall into two categories: invigorate and replenish. Altira Spa experts are available to take away all of those aches and pains and there is even a gentle massage for expectant mothers. Body scrubs and body wraps are the perfect complement to a massage, as your skin is replenished with custom-blended raw ingredients incorporated with essential oils.

　　There are six facial treatments to try including anti-ageing, repair, hydration and even an ‘urban’ service tailored for gentlemen. There is also an extensive range of salon services where you can indulge yourself with a range of hand care, foot care, hair styling, hair colouring, waxing or makeup services.

　　The range of services offered at Macau spas far outweighs most of their western competitors and you will be pleasantly surprised by the level of service as well as the reasonable rates.

　　Altira Spa hours are 10am to midnight and the Salon is open from 10am to 8pm. Reservations are recommended.

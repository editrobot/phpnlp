彩虹财富通过新推出的老虎机网站获得第二春

作者: 博之道 来源: 时间: 2012-08-17 阅读: 次 博彩译文

　　（新闻稿）-由英国本土老虎机开发商BG公司2006年首次发布的彩虹财富老虎机网站，涵盖了所有的标志性博彩游戏，最初是在英国的酒吧、小酒馆以及游乐园内经营的，在正式推出在线版本前就迅速上升到最受大众欢迎排行榜的首位，正式在线版本使得它获得了更显着的成功。

　　新网站的总编肖恩。威尔逊，欣喜地就网站推出表示“我们有一个四人团队专门致力于彩虹财富的特许经营开发”他说“这对我们来说是一种特权和荣誉，去开发一个配置已经触动英国上上下下数百万人们的游戏的网站”

　　威尔逊的团队在工作中对这一主题的热情是显而易见的，他们十分注重细节，威尔逊团队那些自认为是博彩游戏狂热者的成员们以批判性和创造性的眼光审视他们的老虎机特许经营，包括在传统博彩游戏市场以及衍生出的在线和移动市场。

　　“移动市场大概是最令人兴奋的”威尔逊表示“随着智能手机技术的迅速发展，在线和移动博彩游戏体验已经没有什么区别，毫无疑问此类移动博彩游戏应用将是未来的大趋势，我们现在仅仅是见识了冰山的一角”

　　随着越来越多的社交媒体页面和网页书签致力于彩虹财富老虎机博彩游戏的特许经营，毋庸置疑成千上万的游戏粉丝更加渴望看到威尔逊所描述的那座冰山的全部，这样一个狂热的粉丝群可以作为以连续版本形式进一步增加的特许经营游戏的试金石。

　　开发者的母公司BG可以轻松的证明彩虹财富所取得的惊人的成功，在经过游戏产业巨人IGT长达13年的打压后，2011年老虎机研发部门以3300万英镑的价格出售给SG公司，并且继续为博彩厂商进行在线和传统老虎机的研发。2012年BG又和GN公司就彩虹财富在新近被允许经营的几个欧洲地区上线签订了协议。

　　（PRESS RELEASE） -- The site, http://www.rainbowrichesslot.net, is a compendium of all aspects of the iconic game, which was first released by U.K.-based slots developer Barcrest Group in 2006. Originally distributed to bars, pubs and amusement parks across Britain, the game enjoyed a meteoric rise to the top of most-popular lists before being launched online, where it enjoyed further notable success.

　　Sean Wilson, Chief Editor of the new site, spoke of his delight at the launch: "We have a four-man team completely dedicated to the Rainbow Riches franchise," he said. "It is a privilege and an honor for all of us to develop a site that profiles a game that has touched the hearts and minds of many millions of Brits up and down the country."

　　Wilson's team's enthusiasm for the subject matter is apparent in their work. With keen attention to detail, Wilson's self-confessed gaming fanatics turn their critical and creative eyes to every aspect of the slot franchise, including the classic games and their transition to online and mobile markets.

　　"The mobile markets are perhaps the most exciting," Wilson adds. "With Smartphone technology advancing so rapidly there need not be any contrast between the online and mobile gaming experience. There can be no doubt that this kind of mobile gaming is the future - we have merely scaled the tip of the iceberg.”

　　With various social media pages and feeds already dedicated to the the Rainbow Riches Slot Games franchise, there are undoubtedly thousands of fans who will be eager to see the rest of the iceberg that Wilson describes. Such an eager fan-base can prove a touchstone when it comes to further additions to the franchise, as sequential releases have shown.

　　Parent developer Barcrest Gaming can lay testimony to the spectacular success the Rainbow Riches franchise has enjoyed. The slots producer was sold to Scientific Games Corporation in 2011 for ?33 million after a 13-year spell at gaming behemoth IGT, and has continued to develop online and traditional slots for the gaming sector. In 2012 Barcrest signed a deal with GameAccount Network that will see Rainbow Riches launched online in several freshly regulated European territories.

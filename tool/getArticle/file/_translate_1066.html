2011年世界老虎机展销之Aruze美国博彩公司（四）

作者: 博之道 来源: 未知 时间: 2011-10-26 阅读: 次 博彩译文

　　Aruze是一个专门开发和制造游戏设备的公司，是全球博彩市场的游戏设备供应商。该公司生产的老虎机产品涉及范围很广，包括高清晰度多媒体视频老虎机、社区游戏老虎机产品和多终端设备老虎机。 Aruze在今年的拉斯维加斯国际博彩老虎机展览会上，将目前旗帜下的产品组合起来一起展销。

　　“Aruze设计产品的重点，在于同时满足来自世界各地运营商的不同需求，还有就是注重玩家的体验。”Aruze公司游戏销售和营销执行副总裁贾马尔 - 阿扎姆说道：“我们将继续开发令人振奋的新游戏机制，运用强大的科学技术，创造出卓越的产品。”

　　Aruze公司根据传统游戏概念，运用在创新产品上。具有240多种颜色的LED灯和可变转速建立获胜组合的大卷筒设计，这样创新的游戏你呢能够捕捉玩家的注意力，有着强大的吸引力。其中展出的创新游戏系列有福禄寿、竹熊猫和龙之王游戏。

　　作为后续获奖的天堂钓鱼系列，Aruze将引进亚马逊钓鱼游戏，另一个特色主题游戏G - LINK，将会通过改进游戏技术，再次把捕鱼的行动，掌握在玩家手中。亚马逊钓鱼玩家会遇到一个全新的语言环境，有许多新的鱼种，有一些非常大，甚至三个60英寸液晶显示器也无法容纳下去。作为一个新的功能，玩家可以自己选择诱饵来吸引不同鱼的种类，他们可以根据自己的爱好来玩。

　　Aruze也将首次推出最新的G -豪华交互式机顶盒产品，包括摇滚女王III和四大天王游戏。

　　“Aruze真正表明了，我们已经拥有一切。在这样一个动态的多样化的产品线上，可满足任何运营商的需求。”营销副总裁史蒂夫·瓦尔特说：“我们从Aruze公司这些产品的势头来看，今年，我们将继续给参观者留下深刻的印象，并会获取与他们合作的殊荣。”

　　译文

　　Aruze is a rising supplier of innovative gaming equipment that develops and manufactures gaming devices for the global casino market. The company produces a wide range of slot products, including high-resolution multimedia video and stepper slot machines; communal gaming products; and multi-terminal devices. At this year’s G2E in Las Vegas, Aruze will present an extensive portfolio of products.

　　“Aruze designs products that focus on the player’s experience while balancing the varying needs for operators around the world,” said Jamal Azzam, Aruze Gaming executive vice president of sales and marketing. “We continue to release products with exciting new game mechanics, strong mathematics, and superior content.”

　　Aruze fuses traditional gaming concepts with new mechanics with its Innovator product line featuring Radiant Reels, a new spin on stepper technology. Designed with large reels dynamically illuminated by 240 multi-colored LED lights and variable spin speeds which build anticipation for winning combinations, Innovator games capture the players’ attention and provide an engaging and energetic stepper experience. Among the Innovator titles on display are Fu Lu Shou, Four Chinese Beasts, Bamboo Panda, and King of Dragons.

　　As a follow-up to the award-winning Paradise Fishing, Aruze will introduce Amazon Fishing, another G-LINK title featuring Reel Feel Gaming Technology, which again puts the action of fishing in the palm of the players’ hands. Amazon Fishing takes players to an all-new locale with all new fish, some so large they can’t even fit on just one of the three 60-inch LCD monitors. As a new feature, players can now select their own bait to attract the types of fish they would like to catch according to their own style of play.

　　Aruze will also debut their latest premium range of G-DELUXE interactive top box products including Rock You Queen III and The Four Heavenly Kings.

　　“Aruze truly shows that ‘We’ve Got It All’ in such a dynamically diverse product line that can meet the needs of any operator,” said Steve Walther, vice president of marketing. “The momentum we have gained from such engaging products has made Aruze Gaming a company to watch and attendees will continue to be impressed with our award-winning line-up this year.”

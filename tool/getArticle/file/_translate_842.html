多才多艺业余扑克女玩家―珍妮・佛提莉

作者: 博之道 来源: 未知 时间: 2011-10-19 阅读: 次 博彩译文

　　珍妮·佛提莉是加拿大的居民，是一个美国女演员，曾获得奥斯卡奖提名，她在1994年饰演了伍迪艾伦的电影——《百老汇的子弹的威力》。

　　去年12月 ，在加拿大长大，她的父母离婚后，到达不列颠哥伦比亚省农村Texada岛，然后依据到温哥华。

　　世界扑克系列

　　珍妮·佛提莉在2005年WSOP女士无限额德州扑克事件中赢得一个世界系列扑克锦标赛手镯，击败600名其他玩家获得收入$ 158 625。

　　这个手镯是佛提莉已经在WSOP获得的四个奖项之一，并且她又是在主赛事中赢得的现金。她将目光放在妇女在扑克这条路的发展上，在2008年的WSOP中取得成功。

　　其他扑克锦标赛

　　在2005年，佛提莉赢得第三次世界扑克巡回赛（WPT），这是在洛杉矶举行的自行车赌场女子邀请赛。

　　2006年，在摊牌游戏中，佛提莉成为第三位扑克名人。她也出现在GSN扑克皇家系列扑克超级明星的第三个赛季上，而她作为一个名人，在ESPN的职业之外，又以业余扑克玩家的身份（而不是一个专业扑克玩家）出现。

　　她已经在WPT获得3次奖金。

　　除了她在百老汇谜演员的表现，佛提莉她的声音在她的工作中显得非常的突出，她主要的声音角色包括《杀手娃娃蒂芙尼》的孩子角色电影，并在《格里芬的邻居邦妮斯·旺森》电视中出现，配家庭主妇盖伊的声音。

　　她还做了以下电影配音工作：怪物公司，精灵鼠小弟，和幽灵鬼屋。

　　佛提莉比较有特色的电影，包括在1997年的卖座电影《骗子、骗子》，在2000年出现的《蓝鬣蜥舞蹈脱衣舞》和《母夜叉》。她也参加了很多戏剧工作，她在《打一脱鞋》的表现赢得了一个剧院世界奖。

　　佛提莉已经指出，就目前而言，她在追求扑克游戏上比演技更感兴趣。她目前与扑克专业研究员菲尔“Unabomber”Laak是恋人关系。她在比赛中的绰号是“Unabombshell”。

　　截至2008年，珍妮·佛提莉共有超过450 000美元的比赛奖金。

　　Jennifer Tilly

　　Jennifer Tilly is a Canadian-American actress nominated for an Academy Award for her role in Woody Allen's 1994 film, Bullets Over Broadway.

　　Tilly was raised in Canada following her parents' divorce, first on the rural Texada Island in British Columbia and then in Vancouver.

　　World Series of Poker

　　Jennifer Tilly won a World Series of Poker bracelet in the 2005 WSOP Ladies' No-Limit Texas Hold'em event, earning $158 625 by beating 600 other players.

　　This bracelet is one of 4 money finishes Tilly has claimed at the WSOP, but she is yet to cash at the Main Event. She will be looking to climb up the ladder of women in poker with a successful run at the 2008 WSOP.

　　Other Poker Tournaments

　　In 2005, Tilly won the third World Poker Tour （WPT） Ladies Invitational Tournament at the Bicycle Casino in LA.

　　In 2006, Tilly finished 3rd in the Celebrity Poker Showdown. She has also appeared in the GSN Poker Royale series and the third season of Poker Superstars, while she appears as a celebrity （rather than a poker pro） in ESPN's Pro-Am Poker Equalizer.

　　She has finished in the money on 3 occasions in the WPT.

　　Acting

　　Apart from her performance in Bullets Over Broadway, Tilly is also well known for her voice-over work, with her major voice roles including killer doll Tiffany in the Child's Play movies and as the Griffin's neighbour Bonnie Swanson in the hit TV show, Family Guy.

　　She has also done voice work for the following films: Monsters Inc., Stuart Little, Home on the Range and The Haunted Mansion.

　　Tilly featured in Jim Carrey's 1997 hit film, Liar Liar, and in 2000 appeared as a stripper and dominatrix in Dancing at the Blue Iguana. She has also done a lot of theatre work, winning a Theater World Award for her performance in the play One Shoe Off.

　　Tilly has stated that for the moment, she is more interested in pursuing poker than acting. She is currently dating fellow poker professional Phil "Unabomber" Laak. Her nickname on the circuit is "The Unabombshell".

　　As of 2008, Jennifer Tilly has live tournament winnings of over $450 000.

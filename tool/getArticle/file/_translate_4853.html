艺电体育吧进驻大都会

作者: 博之道 来源: 时间: 2012-03-27 阅读: 次 博彩译文

　　艺电体育、夏普和索尼预定3月31日携带新型体育游戏进驻大都会。

　　大都会今天宣布，3月31日艺电体育吧将在大都会酒店和赌场开幕。这一休闲风格的新场地将会提供视频体育游戏以及播出传统体育赛事。新场地占地25000平方英尺，至多可同时接待125位客人，由大都会、索尼、艺电体育、夏普联合投资建设。将以Sony PlayStation 3游戏平台、艺电体育出品的疯狂橄榄球、一球成名、挑战老虎等着名游戏为主打项目，同时有六架高清电视播出当日体育赛事。体育吧设计有一个私密“客厅”，包括九座的皮椅、沙发和古董级的桌式足球桌，提供酒类零食消费服务，还设有一个服装、礼品和视频游戏的销售区。艺电体育吧周日至周四上午11时至午夜12时、周五和周六上午11时至凌晨1时营业。

　　It’s in the game: EA Sports Bar coming to the Cosmopolitan

　　EA Sports, Sharp and Sony partner to bring sports games to new lounge, scheduled to open March 31

　　The Cosmopolitan announced today that it will open an EA Sports Bar at the hotel and casino on March 31. The new lounge-style venue will offer patrons sports video games to play, as well as traditional sporting events broadcast by the bar.

　　The new 25,000-square-foot venue, which will hold as many as 125 guests, is born from a partnership among the Cosmopolitan, EA Sports, PlayStation and Sharp. It will feature Sony PlayStation 3 consoles, a selection of EA Sports games such as Madden NFL 12, FIFA 12 and golf against Tiger Woods, and six high-definition televisions to broadcast daily sporting events.

　　The sports-bar-meets-lounge is designed to have an intimate "living room" feel with nine seating vignettes featuring leather chairs and sofas, as well as an antique foosball table. It will serve bottled beers and snack food and include a retail area selling clothing, gifts and video games.

　　EA Sports Bar will be open Sundays through Thursdays from 11 a.m. to midnight and Fridays and Saturdays from 11 a.m. to 1 a.m.

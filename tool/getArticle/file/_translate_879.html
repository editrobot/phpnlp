扑克游戏界的经典名言

作者: 博之道 来源: 未知 时间: 2011-10-20 阅读: 次 博彩译文

　　他说：“在扑克的支撑点，永远要摒弃自己的脾气，当你在玩的时候，尤其是在玩扑克的时候。扑克是没有同情的。要始终保持冷静。如果你失去了你清醒的头脑，你将失去所有的筹码”。

　　- 威廉J.佛罗伦萨

　　“极限扑克是一门科学，而无限额是一门艺术。限制，意味着你是在一个目标射击。无限额，目标变得变幻莫测，需要你将其找回来。”

　　- 杰克·斯特劳斯

　　“极限扑克是一门科学，但无限额是一门艺术。限制，你是在一个目标射击。无限额，目标变得生机勃勃，拍摄你回来。”

　　- 杰克·斯特劳斯

　　“有几件事情是不容忽视的。扑克在我们国家来说，上层阶级知道它的很少。现在，你发现在玩排序游戏而拥有一般知识的大使，但是无知的人确实是可怕的。为什么，我已经知道牧师，是个好男人，善良，自由，真诚的，所有这一切，谁不知道在寻找这某种意义。这是足以让一个人感到羞耻的”

　　- 马克·吐温

　　“你不能允许这样的事情发生，当有人在比赛中让你打一张你不应该打出的牌时。当然，如果没有这类型的玩家，没有人会赢得任何数额的金钱，所以有时你必须运用一些策略。”

　　- T. J. Cloutier

　　“赌博是辛勤的工作，打扑克也是一样，不要让任何人告诉你这会有什么不同。想想是坐在一个牌桌上的人什么样子，其唯一的目标是削嘶哑的喉咙，削减你的钱，让你谈论了什么错误的话然后自食恶果。这可能听起来很苛刻。但是，这是在牌桌上的方式。如果你不相信我，那么你就是砧板上的肉，任人宰割。”

　　- 丹里德

　　“无论喜欢与否，一个人的性格是在牌桌上都必须脱离；如果其他玩家看得比他透彻，他只有承受他自己惹的祸。除非他是别人，看清所有的缺陷，然后做好准备。”

　　- 安东尼·霍尔登

　　他说：“你有你的权力击败，简单地实现这个简单的道理：你遇到更坏的牌，你是幸运的。这是一个迹象，表明你会不断承受最糟糕的状况，而且，如果你不能击败总是最坏牌，你就不能击败任何对手。”

　　- 娄克里格

　　“大部分的钱，你赢在扑克，记住，你不但是从自己的辉煌中获取，并且也是因为你的对手的无能。”

　　- 娄克里格

　　“扑克是一种计算侵略的游戏：如果你卡好，您足以称之为一个赌注，他们是足够好的机会为你提高赢钱的几率。”

　　- 阿尔弗雷德·阿尔瓦雷斯

　　“当一个人用金钱获取经验时，就会同时拥有金钱和经验，成为一个有经验的有钱人。”

　　译文

　　Educational Poker Quotes

　　“The strong point in poker is never to lose your temper, either with those you are playing with or, more particularly, with the cards. There is no sympathy in poker. Always keep cool. If you lose your head you will lose all your chips”

　　- William J. Florence

　　"Limit poker is a science, but no-limit is an art. In limit, you are shooting at a target. In no-limit, the target comes alive and shoots back at you."

　　- Jack Strauss

　　"Limit poker is a science, but no-limit is an art. In limit, you are shooting at a target. In no-limit, the target comes alive and shoots back at you."

　　- Jack Strauss

　　“There are few things that are so unpardonably neglected in our country as poker. The upper class knows very little about it. Now and then you find ambassadors who have sort of a general knowledge of the game, but the ignorance of the people is fearful. Why, I have known clergymen, good men, kind-hearted, liberal, sincere, and all that, who did not know the meaning of a "flush." It is enough to make one ashamed of the species.”

　　- Mark Twain

　　“You can't let it get you down when somebody knocks you out of a tournament playing a hand they shouldn't have played. Without these types of players, nobody would win any amount of money, so sometimes you just have to take your medicine.”

　　- T. J. Cloutier

　　“It's hard work, gambling. Playing poker. Don't let anyone tell you different. Think about what it's like sitting at a poker table with people whose only goal is to cut your throat, take your money and leave you out the back talking to yourself about what went wrong inside. That probably sounds harsh. But that's the way it is at the poker table. If you don't believe me, then you're the lamb that's going off to the slaughter.”

　　- Stu Unger

　　“You will show your poker greatness by the hands you fold, not the hands you play.”

　　- Dan Reed

　　“Whether he likes it or not, a man’s character is stripped at the poker table; if the other players read him better than he does, he has only himself to blame. Unless he is both able and prepared to see himself as others do, flaws and all, he will be a loser in cards, as in life.”

　　- Anthony Holden

　　“You have it in your power to turn a bad-beat around simply by realizing this simple truth: The more bad beats you encounter, the luckier you are. It's a sign that you are playing against opponents who continually take the worst of it, and if you can't beat someone who always takes the worst of it, you can't beat anyone.”

　　- Lou Krieger

　　“Most of the money you'll win at poker comes not from the brilliance of your own play, but from the ineptitude of your opponents.”

　　- Lou Krieger

　　“Hold'em is a game of calculated aggression: If you cards are good enough for your to call a bet, they are good enough to raise with."

　　- Alfred Alvarez

　　"When a man with money meets a man with experience, the man with experience leaves with money and the man with money leaves with experience."

　　- Anonymous

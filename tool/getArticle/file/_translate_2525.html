2010年在拉斯维加斯举行的世界扑克锦标赛

作者: 博之道 来源: 未知 时间: 2011-12-05 阅读: 次 博彩译文

　　任何玩过扑克游戏的玩家，相信都会为这一种游戏感到兴奋。当说到世界上最大的扑克比赛，世界扑克锦标赛，玩家的兴趣及积极性将会到达不可思议的地步。

　　2010世界扑克锦标赛的日期：比赛日期现在已被规定为10天，一直持续到2010年2月21日。

　　地点：哈拉斯赌场酒店。

　　世界扑克锦标赛，也可以称为WSOP，为熟练的扑克玩家提供一个在世界一流的扑克比赛中显示自己才干的机会。虽然它不是一个正式头衔的WSOP主赛事冠军，但是也被认为是扑克世界冠军。世界扑克锦标赛的冠军将会获得一个金手镯，并赢得大量现金作为奖品。

　　自2005年以来，世界扑克锦标赛一直在拉斯维加斯的比尼恩赌场举行，但本次比赛的举办由哈拉斯娱乐公司负责，世界扑克锦标赛的场地设在拉斯维加斯赌场，有火烈鸟路西全套房酒店。

　　任何支付钱的玩家都能参加世界扑克锦标赛。但是，唯有最好的玩家才可能进入到主赛事，在最终的赌桌上，谁要是成为最终的赢家，谁就有机会赚取可观的奖金。在世界扑克锦标赛中，玩家的代码都有严格的使用规定和指引。

　　World Series of Poker in Las Vegas 2010

　　Anyone who has ever played a hand of poker will understand the excitement that can be generated in this game. This interest and enthusiasm is magnified to incredible proportions when it comes to one of the world’s biggest poker tournaments, the World Series of Poker.

　　2010 Dates: Dates have now been set for the 10th through to the 21st February 2010.

　　Location: Harrah's Casino Hotel.

　　The World Series of Poker, familiarly known as the WSOP provides skilled poker players a chance to show their mettle in a world class poker competition. Although it is not an official title, the winner of the Main Event of the WSOP is considered to be the world champion in poker. The winner of the World Series of Poker gets a gold bracelet and wins large monetary prizes.

　　The WSOP was once held at Binion’s in Las Vegas, but this tournament is now organised by Harrah’s Entertainment Inc. The venue for the World Series of Poker since 2005 has been the Rio All Suite Hotel and Casino on West Flamingo Road in Las Vegas.

　　Any player can enter the WSOP as all it requires is a buy in. Only the finest players make it to the Main Event, and it is the best of these that play at the final table and get a share of the substantial winnings to be earned at the WSOP. There are strict rules and guidelines laid down for participants in the WSOP, including a code of conduct for players.

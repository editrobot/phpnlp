　　For many people, when they first enter the colourful, entertaining world of online games, the sheer number of websites and games and attractive bonuses can be overwhelming. And while the vast majority of people enjoy these games and know where to draw the line, there will be a few people who run into trouble. But with a little thought and guidance, everyone can play these games safely. In this article we’ll look at ways to play sensibly.

　　First of all, you should realise that online casino games exist for recreation, and are meant to be fun. There are very few people who make a long term living out of it （with the possible exception of poker） and you should never see using such websites as a magical way to make a living. In the long （and even short） run, you will find that the website will win. You cannot ‘game’ the complex algorithms that make these games tick.

　　Having said that, many people - often beginners - do find that they are lucky with online games and can often win large sums. The trick is to see both winning and losing as part of the fun. A good way to ensure you don’t end up losing this week’s grocery money is by setting limits. You can impose these by yourself or in some cases, casino games sites will allow you to set up an auto-limit function. In many cases, there will be a limit on your account anyway, but this may be several thousand pounds. You will have to lower it yourself, or request that it is lowered.

　　Each game site is governed by the Gambling Commission in the UK, and as such has to adhere to strict rules about how they offer their games. Setting limits is part of this and there should be a help section which tells you how to set a limit up. Otherwise there may well be a telephone number to call and receive guidance from a real person at the website.

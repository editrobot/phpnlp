韩国百家乐骗子诈骗快活大赌场100万美元

作者: 博之道 来源: 未知 时间: 2011-11-11 阅读: 次 博彩译文

　　在康涅狄格州的快活度假村赌场，两名成员被指控骗取韩国赌场高达100万美元，并且受到了严重的惩罚。在此期间，面对着类似的指控，该团伙的另外两名成员正在等待引渡到大西洋城。

　　60岁的苏杨瑞和34岁的金正日，这两个人在韩国被认为是在赌场作弊团伙的一小部分。这是全球和国内众所周知的赌场欺骗团伙。

　　为了遵循法院的诉讼，这两个韩国人使用口译员在周三的一个新的伦敦法庭出庭。因为在赌场使用电子设备作弊，使得他们在快活度假村被逮捕，他们现在面临的是一个记录着他们的阴谋和偷费的列表。

　　金正日一共偷盗83,000美元以上，而苏杨瑞被指控是本集团的组织者，并且在快活大赌场作弊获取$447,000。

　　快活度假村赌场进行了为期3个月之久的作弊调查后，这两个人被逮捕。本次行动是与国土安全部，国家警察赌场单位和新泽西州警察赌场单位一起进行的。

　　另外两名韩国人，55岁的Ingyu Park和63岁的Sun Jang被指控在大西洋城的Bally酒店的迷你百家乐比赛中作弊。

　　无论是在线赌场还是现实赌场，最新的逮捕都已经证实了这样的事实，韩国的赌博罪犯现在变得更为普遍。在网上赌场，黑客会运用各种在线扑克室的源代码进行作弊，就在几个月前，在线扑克作弊的罪犯在韩国被捕。

　　朝鲜团伙，可能会袭击美国最新的赌场，如果他们被发现有罪，则会遭到逮捕并且长期监禁。

　　Charges of defrauding the Foxwoods Resort Casino in Connecticut of up to $1 million has been framed against the two members of a Korean casino. In the meantime, on similar charges, another two members of the gang are awaiting extradition to Atlantic City.

　　Gy Young Su, 60 and Wookyung Kim, 34, the two Koreans are believed to have played just a small part in a casino cheating gang. The gang which is known for it’s worldwide and domestic dimensions.

　　In order to follow the court proceedings, the two Koreans used interpreters during Wednesday’s court appearance in a New London courtroom. For using electronic devices to cheat at the casino, they had previously been arrested at the Foxwoods Resort and they now face a whole list of conspiracy and stealing charges.

　　Kim is believed to have stolen more than $83,000, while Gy Young Su is accused of being the organizer of the group and cheating Foxwoods alone of $447,000.

　　After the Foxwoods Resort Casino conducted a three-month long cheating investigation, they were arrested. This investigation was in conjunction with the Department of Homeland Security, the state police casino unit and the New Jersey state police casino unit as well.

　　The other two Koreans, Ingyu Park, 55, and Sun Jang, 63 are accused of cheating at Atlantic City’s Bally’s Hotel’s mini-baccarat tables.

　　In both online and bricks and mortar casinos, the latest arrests would seem to confirm the fact that Korean criminals are becoming much more prevalent. Just a few months ago, for hacking the source codes of various online poker rooms, a ring of Korean online poker cheaters were arrested in South Korea.

　　For the Korean gang, the latest casino arrests to hit the US could spell lengthy jail sentences, if they are found guilty.

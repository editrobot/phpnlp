学会如何成为一个赢家

作者: 博之道 来源: 未知 时间: 2011-09-26 阅读: 次 博彩译文

　　如果用正确的态度处理，赌博可以是一种娱乐形式。而一个好的方法是，旨在赢得胜利，但也随时准备失去——失去当天大部分你计划中所要失去金额。换句话说，你必须接受这样的事实，你不能强迫一个成功的结果，你不能每一次都只赢不输。

　　有许多赌博系统或方法，都旨在克服失败的可能性，给你一个成为赢家的机会，但不要指望有系统能够帮你通过自己的方式来阻挡赌场赢得财富。除了算牌在21点或运用鞅系统没有最大赌注限制以外，所有形式的赌博是靠运气，一个精明的赌徒，应该注意该特点。

　　赌场有一个内置的数学优势，几乎在每场比赛，没有数学的投注系统是可以克服的。另一方面，玩家可以决定何时退出。你做出怎样的决策将决定你成功与否。

　　赌场让你有一个自己的钱拍，他们将不得不让你在同一时间出手。要注重有计划的投注策略。自己要有所约束，要限制你的亏损和控制您的奖金。如果你输了，坚持你的资金限制计划。如果你赢了，不贪心。要一步一步来，而不仅仅旨在赢得了金钱。而学习如何成为赢家，第一步就得由一个小幅度开始，而且是任何小幅度的上进，而这都是不容易的。

　　译文

　　Gambling can be a form of entertainment if handled with the right attitude. A good approach is to aim to win but to also be prepared to lose - and lose only the amount you planned to risk on that day. In other words, you have to accept the fact that you cannot force a winning outcome and you cannot win every time.

　　There are many devised gambling systems or methods aimed at overcoming the odds to give you an edge to make you a winner, but don't expect to systematically play your way to riches off the casinos. Apart from card counting at blackjack or applying the Martingale System with no maximum bet limit, all forms of gambling are conditioned by luck, and an astute gambler should make allowance for that.

　　The casinos have an in-built mathematical advantage on virtually every game and no mathematical betting system can overcome that. On the other hand, the player practically makes all the playing decisions including when to quit. Depending how you make those decisions will determine how successful or otherwise you are.

　　The casinos are there to let you have a shot at their money and they will have a shot at yours too at the same time. Have a planned betting strategy. Discipline yourself too. Limit your losses and control your winnings. Stick to your planned bankroll limit if you lose and don't be greedy if you win. Walk before you run. Rather than aiming to win a specific amount of money, learn how to be a winner first by a small margin - any small margin. Not easy.

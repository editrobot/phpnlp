百家乐名称的前生

作者: 博之道 来源: 未知 时间: 2011-10-09 阅读: 次 博彩译文

　　Baccarat水晶拥有的卓越历史

　　桌牌游戏，百家乐，你会注意到它的高雅和经典。这些天来，由于互联网，更多的人可以享受一次百家乐游戏是可能的，但它仍然保留其独特的光环。享有的独占性和豪华完全相同的声誉的是另一百家乐——Baccarat水晶 ，世界上最好的水晶玻璃器皿。

　　超过200年前，大约1764年，法国国王路易允许一个玻璃厂工厂在法国的洛林地区的一个镇百家乐设立。起初，该公司只生产窗口和镜子，直到1816年11月水晶头炉才开始投产。令人惊讶的是，在这个时候，有着相对数量庞大的工人，超过3000名员工，在工厂工作。百家乐的声誉是无与伦比的，它的许多产品生产使用费和其他国家的元首的相一致。它的价格与细致的制造，都是无可比拟的。但只有社会的最高层的成员，才能能买得起百家乐的奢侈玻璃器皿。1855年，在巴黎，百家乐在第一届世界博览会赢得第一枚金牌，成为许多精英公司的领头企业。

　　真正的百家乐标志

　　在其产品的底部发现百家乐的标志，这是卓越的标志。产量增加，包括酒杯，水晶吊灯和香水瓶，20世纪初，该公司每天生产超过4000个香水瓶（短短十年左右的时间同一天相比增加了约150）。多年以后，百家乐开始制作首饰，并于最近推出了自己的香水系列。其产品被认为是艺术作品，并于1964年在巴黎卢浮宫举行的展览，以庆祝公司诞辰200周年。在洛林，有Baccarat水晶博物馆，游客在这里可以看到数以百计的华丽商品。

　　我们大多数人只能梦想拥有一块巴卡拉水晶，它的的价格是这个世界上的顶尖，尽管它是可能的，伊丽莎白雅顿公司为您提供的是娇兰香水，其实，只是用百家乐瓶包装起来。这一点淡淡的优雅可以使所有的都发生变异，而不像玩赌博游戏百家乐，即使只在网上也不会发生变异。

　　译文

　　Baccarat Crystal Has a Distinguished History

　　The table game, baccarat, is noted for its elegance and class. These days, thanks to the Internet, many more people can enjoy the game of baccarat than once was possible but it still retains its unique aura. Enjoying the exact same reputation for exclusivity and luxury is another baccarat - Baccarat Crystal - the finest crystal glassware in the world.

　　Over 200 years ago - in 1764 - King Louis of France allowed a glassworks factory to open in the town of Baccarat, in the Lorraine area of France. At first, the company produced windows and mirrors until the first crystal oven went into operation in November 1816. Amazingly, over 3,000 employees worked at the factory by this time, a relatively huge number of workers for the period. Baccarat's reputation was unsurpassed and many of its products were produced for royalty and other heads of state; prices were commensurate with the painstaking work and no one but the members of the highest echelon of society could afford Baccarat's extravagant glassware. In 1855, Baccarat won the first gold medal at the first World's Fair in Paris - it would be the first of many for the elite company.

　　The Mark of Real Baccarat

　　The Baccarat mark - found at the bottom of their products - was a sign of excellence. Production increased to include stemware, chandeliers and perfume bottles; by the early 1900s the company was producing over 4,000 perfume bottles a day （as compared to about 150 a day just a decade or so earlier）。 Years later, Baccarat started making jewelry and recently it launched its own perfume line. Its products are considered works of art and in 1964 the Louvre in Paris held an exhibit to celebrate the company's 200th anniversary. In Lorraine, there is the Baccarat Crystal museum, where visitors can see hundreds of gorgeous pieces （possibly the closest most of us will come to priceless Baccarat articles）。

　　Most of us can only dream about owning a piece of Baccarat crystal - the prices are out of this world - though it is possible that the Elizabeth Arden or Guerlain perfume you use is, in fact, packaged in a Baccarat bottle. That little touch of elegance can make all the difference - not unlike playing the gambling game, baccarat, even if only online.

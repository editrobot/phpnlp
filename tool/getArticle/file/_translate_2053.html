　　Paddy Power收购Cayetano

　　这两家公司自去年6月以来就已经这项交易。

　　现在，Paddy Power公司已收购了Cayetano在线软件开发公司。

　　网上赌博业在过去几个星期的发生很多变化，兼并、收购等现象不断发生，但这一切似乎在这里停留。最后，我们很高兴看到这种变化，特别是看到各种快速的行动在帮助移动行业向前发展。

　　英国著名公司Paddy Power刚刚签署了一项协议，收购Cayetano的游戏软件。该公司在法律上被称为CT网络有限公司，但它作为Cayetano在互联网上已经广为人知。他们根据自己的名字在命名游戏名称，所以你肯定已经玩过由他们开发的赌场游戏。Paddy Power收购Cayetano的交易金额目前尚未披露。

　　几个月钱，Paddy Power公司为了能成功收购Paddy Power收购Cayetano而做出了很多努力。早在今年6月，Cayetano同意向Paddy Power公司提供在线赌博游戏。关于这个收购案，几个月以来讨论并没有就此停止。

　　截至10月31日，马恩岛公司据报道，Cayetano的软件开发团队提供一些在线赌博游戏的最大的业务。另外，网上赌场SkyVegas.com也在努力吹嘘自己的产品。

　　Cayetano的CEO尼克莫恩宣布，Paddy Power收购Cayetano是一个很好的机会，Cayetano能成为Paddy Power公司的一部分，他们感到非常兴奋。

　　Paddy Power公司最新行情证明了这一点。他们的股票市值在过去的一个季度水涨船高，网上业务也从31％上升至33％。Paddy Power公司也夸耀可以为移动用户提供更高的数值，而这些数值有上升的趋势。从Cayetano的移动设备访问Paddy Power网站的在线玩家总数在44％左右。这是惊人的，证明了移动赌博业务在未来是多么的重要。

　　总而言之，赌博业巨头公司Paddy Power，成功收购有经验的软件开发公司Cayetano以后，提高了自身在市场上的竞争力。

　　Paddy Power Acquires Cayetano

　　The two companies already had a deal since June

　　Now, Paddy Power has purchased the online software development company

　　Lots of changes in the online gambling industry over the past few weeks, and it seems like the mergers, acquisitions and coverall changes are here to stay. In the end, we’re glad that such changes are going on, especially seeing that it’s precisely these kinds of actions that help move the industry forward at the end of the day.

　　The famous British bookmaker, Paddy Power, has just signed a deal to purchase a software and games developer from the Isle of Man. The company is legally called CT Networks Limited, but it’s mostly known on the internet as Cayetano, and they have a number of games in the business under their name, so you already had the chance to play no download casino games made by them. The amount for which the transaction was made has remain undisclosed as of yet.

　　Paddy Power’s newest acquisition comes just a couple of months after a deal between themselves and Cayetano was signed. Back in June, Cayetano agreed to supply online gambling games for Paddy Power, and it seems like the discussions didn’t stop there.

　　As of the 31st of October, the Isle of Man company reportedly had assests of about ?160k. Their team of software developers provides online gambling games for some of the largest names in the business, with casinos such as SkyVegas.com boasting their products.

　　Nick Maughan, the CEO of Cayetano, who has held his position for the past two years, has declared that this is a great opportunity for the company, and they are extremely excited to be a part of Paddy Power.

　　Paddy Power aren’t doing too bad themselves – and this latest deal is a proof of that. Their numbers have gone up over the past quarter, according to their performance update. The online business went up from 31% to 33%. Paddy Power can also boast some pretty high numbers for mobile users, and these numbers are on the rise, as well. The total number of online players that have accessed the Paddy Power website from their mobile devices is around 44%, which is astounding, and goes to prove just how important the mobile gambling business is and will be in the future.

　　All in all, gambling giant Paddy Power are now securing their position on the market with the addition of Cayetano, an experienced software development company.

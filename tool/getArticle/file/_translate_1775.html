百家乐游戏中常见的误区

作者: 博之道 来源: 未知 时间: 2011-11-16 阅读: 次 博彩译文

　　百家乐是一种在赌场玩最简单和最流行的游戏之一。虽然有许多方法来增加赢钱的机会，但是要赢得任何的一次百家乐比赛并不简单。所以，为了能够避免成为失败者中的一员，玩家也有很多东西可以学习，并最终在百家乐比赛中运用。随着百家乐游戏的普及，游戏界的神话也随之而来。这里将为大家介绍一些玩家对百家乐游戏的误解。

　　误区1：只有富有的贵族才可以玩百家乐游戏

　　在过去，当百家乐刚开始在美国赌场出现，像上面所说的确实是真的。但在今天，事情已经大大的发生了改变。百家乐已经出现了许多变化，我们每个人现在都有机会在赌场和网上赌场玩这种游戏。

　　误区2：百家乐有一个特定的玩法模式

　　说到百家乐有一个特定的玩法模式，其实并没有这么一回事。百家乐是运气因素占主导地位的游戏，玩百家乐获胜的机会并不依赖于在庄家或和局中赢得了多少次。虽然把赌注押在庄家更有利可图，但这并不意味着这一决定总会让你赢得胜利。

　　误区3：百家乐的牌卡计数方式，和21点是一样的

　　玩家通常认为，百家乐纸牌游戏算牌系统的工作原理，就像在21点游戏一样，21点和百家乐都是用相同的筹码来玩，但实际上，这只能算的上是一个大神话。在百家乐游戏当中，是不可能算牌的，机会和运气才是百家乐游戏的关键。

　　误区4：百家乐系统是非常有用的。

　　如前所述，百家乐是一种机会和运气相结合的游戏。说百家乐系统有用，这只能是天方夜谭。如果只想着运用百家乐系统，只会浪费时间和金钱。以其用百家乐系统来浪费金钱，还不如依靠自己的直觉，还可能更容易赢钱。

　　Common Myths on Baccarat

　　Baccarat is one of the easiest and one of the most popular card games being played in a casino. Winning any game is not simple although there are numerous ways to increase such chance. So as to avoid being at the loser side, there are also a lot of things a player can work upon and eventually be on top of this game of baccarat. With the popularity of baccarat, myths have also cropped up. This write up will bust few myths related to Baccarat.

　　Myth #1: Only rich aristocratic people play Baccarat

　　In the past when baccarat just started its voyage to American casinos, the above statement was actually true but things have changed drastically today. Many baccarat variations have appeared and each of us now have a chance to admire this game both in casinos and online.

　　Myth #2: Baccarat has a particular pattern to play

　　Baccarat has a particular pattern to play is not actually true because your chances for winning does not depend on how many times Banker or Tie won, rather baccarat is the game of luck. Although, to bet on Banker is more profitable, it does not mean this decision will always make you win.

　　Myth #3: Baccarat can work on card counting, same as blackjack

　　It is usually believed that card counting system works for baccarat card game, just like in blackjack, as both blackjack and baccarat are played with the same deck of cards but this is a big myth. At baccarat, it is impossible to count cards. Chance and luck are crucial in Baccarat.

　　Myth #4: Baccarat systems are helpful.

　　As mentioned earlier, Baccarat is a game of chance and luck and it is a big myth that the so-called baccarat systems are helpful. They are rather just a waste of time and money. It is better to rely on your intuition than to waste money on baccarat systems.

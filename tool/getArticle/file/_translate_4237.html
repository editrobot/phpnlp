狂欢赌场-大西洋城的救星

作者: 博之道 来源: 时间: 2012-02-18 阅读: 次 博彩译文

　　三年以来，大西洋城赌业一直处于一个可怕的恶性循环之中，分析家曾表示只有新开一两家新场所才能扭转这种局面。四月份，分析家的预言将会随着狂欢赌场的开业而被验证。狂欢赌场是一个筹划多年的项目，但是2008年的全球经济危机导致开发商停止了项目建设，经济危机的几个月后项目被搁置。现在这个赌博胜地终于将开门纳客，虽然初期仅仅是部分试营业。在开业初期一个半月里，狂欢赌场仅开放部分设施，赌博和餐饮设施将在5月底的正式开业后启用。很多人相信狂欢赌场将是打开新泽西赌业复兴之门的钥匙。“过去的几年我们备受煎熬”当地一家赌场的经营者桑德拉？勒博说，“虽然我是为另一家赌场工作，但是狂欢这样豪华现代赌场的开业，一定会刺激整个地区赌业的发展，也会有助于吸引之前不会来大西洋城的顾客到我们这里来”。宾夕法尼亚的赌业兴盛发展是狂欢赌场和大西洋城其他赌场面临的最大难题，分析家预测到年底，宾夕法尼亚的赌业整体收入可能超过新泽西。狂欢赌场内设超过七家的餐厅、聚会场所、5500位的剧院、商场和赌场，在吸引眼球方面被当地赌业管理当局寄予厚望，同时也令人振奋，“我都等不及了，十多年了都是在一家赌场”罗伯特说，“这将是我下一次去大西洋城的一次全新尝试”。

　　译文

　　Atlantic City casinos have been stuck in a terrible downward spiral for the better part of three years, and analysts have been suggesting that the only way to turn the city's gaming fortunes around would be to open a new facility or two. In April, these analyst predictions will be tested when the Revel resort opens to the public. Revel is a project that has been in the works for several years. The economic recession of 2008 caused developers to stop construction of the new facility, and it has sat dormant for months after the recession. Eventually, the project was restarted, and now the resort will open, although limited at first. Over the first month and a half, Revel will only open certain parts of their establishment. Gaming and dining options will be limited until a grand opening in late May. At that time, many believe the resort could be the key to a revival of gaming in New Jersey. "We have had a tough go of it the past few years," said Sandra Lebow, a dealer at one of the AC casinos. "Although I work for another casino, it will be good to have a big modern resort open. I think it will bring excitement to the entire area, and it will help us bring in new customers that maybe before, didn't want to come to Atlantic City."

　　The biggest problem for Revel and other AC casinos is the booming gaming industry in Pennsylvania. Analysts are predicting that by the end of the year, Pennsylvania casinos may pass New Jersey casinos in terms of overall revenue. The Revel gives AC casino executives hope that with a new facility will come increased exposure. Among the attractions at Revel will be over seven restaurants, meeting space, a 5,500 capacity theatre, a shops, and a casino. The expectations are high, but they also have many people excited. "I can't wait. I've been coming to the same casinos for over ten years," said Robert Troller. "It will be great to have something new to check out next time I make my way to Atlantic City."

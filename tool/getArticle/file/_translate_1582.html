必发的新总裁：科克伦Breon

作者: 博之道 来源: 未知 时间: 2011-11-11 阅读: 次 博彩译文

　　导言：Paddy Power公司总裁科克伦Breon于昨日辞职，并将于2012年8月在必发担任首席执行官。

　　从昨天开始，Paddy Power在线赌博公司发生了一场让人意想不到的大变动，该公司的首席执行官调跳槽到对手公司。

　　这是我们在第一时间获得的最新消息：昨日，Breon科克伦辞去他作为公司Paddy Power的首席执行官职务。“爱尔兰时报”首先报道此新闻事件，而且还首次推测，科克伦将在Paddy Power公司的竞争对手之一任职。

　　自2001年以来，Paddy Power公司聘任科克伦长达10年。自2004年以来，他一直是Paddy Power公司的董事会成员。Paddy Power公司的首席执行官帕特里克肯尼迪，也是科克伦的亲密朋友，在2010年任命科克伦为Paddy Power公司的首席执行官。

　　今天，根据记者报道的最新传闻，业内专家预测，科克伦将被聘任为必发的新行政总裁。他将取代现任首席执行官大卫于，而他将在2012年8月开始上任。

　　科克伦在过去的几天也发表了声明。他说，必发公司的技术在目前的网上赌博业有很大的竞争实力和发展前景。他期待着与必发的工作团队紧密合作。

　　必发公司董事长爱德华瑞还宣称，他为科克伦出任首席执行官感到高兴，而他们计划在接来下的几个月研究世界各地的博彩市场。

　　科克伦Breon的作法是否会是一个聪明的举动，让我们在2012年拭目以待。

The Irish Times were the first to report the news, and it was also first to launch the rumor that Corcoran would be appointed in the same position at one of Paddy Power’s competitors.

　　Corcoran had been employed with Paddy Power for 10 years, since 2001, and since 2004 he has been the a member of the company’s board. A former JP Morgan employee, he was also behind a number of start-ups before making his transition into online betting. Corcoran was and is also a close friend of Patrick Kennedy, Paddy Power’s Chief Executive Officer, who named him COO in August of 2010.

　　Today, as predicted by journalists and experts in the industry, according to the latest rumors, Corcoran was confirmed as the new Chief Executive Officer at Betfair. He will be replacing current CEO David Yu, who has expressed his desire to step down from his position, and he will be instated in his new job starting in August of 2012.

　　Corcoran also made a statement that accompanied his decisions over the past few days. He said that the technology that is currently present at Betfair gives the company an unique and fantastic position in the online gambling industry, and he foresees a bright future for Betfair. He looks forward to working closely with the team over at Betfair and make sure they grow together.

　　Betfair Chairman of the Board, Edward Wray, also declared that he is happy with appointing Corcoran as CEO, and they have made this decision after months of research all over the world.

2011年世界老虎机展销之贵族科技股份有限公司（三）

　　2011年，在G2E的展销会上，贵族科技股份有限公司向我们展现了如何创建游戏新体验、游戏新技术，新的游戏类别，新的娱乐游戏，以及新的系统，它说：我们以前从未取得像现在这样的惊人进步。

　　贵族科技股份有限公司的暗碧族混合动力车系列拥有活动卷轴技术，有具有潜在功能的5卷步基地游戏，有全视频和三盘步在同一场比赛进行的新奖金游戏。该公司的LCD卷轴窗口和电子快门有阻止个别卷轴和符号的功能。

　　暗碧族混合动力车系列中的第一个产品推出取得很大的成功，有三个游戏：波斯奖，小精灵的财宝和大溪地珍宝。

　　同时还推出超旋的奖金库免费游戏，还有渐进性或信贷奖奖金游戏新系列。超旋系列将推出两个游戏：钻石的命运和红宝石轿车。这是具有最高奖金的游戏，奖金的设置可作为非渐进式或作为一个独立的渐进配置。

　　现金快车、金色玻璃是在原来流行的现金快车的基础上改造的，采用新的黄金级和乘数轮功能，是具有高频率的基础游戏。

　　佐罗系列：传奇归来和佐罗面具。在游戏中的泰山丛林扩大了，还增添了与泰山冒险返回相关的游戏情节，游戏与所有的新功能结合，用一个新的泰山作为链接。

　　娱乐式显卡老虎机，设置有25万美元的最高奖项，并与三个局域网串联，奖金设置的特点是有6个脉冲冲击。

　　表现最出色的是大白鲨老虎机系列，介绍大白鲨与赏金猎人的主题，你可以获得很好的音频体验。

　　随着越来越多印加人的加入，兆丰薪酬系列有所扩大。游戏中增加了新的理发店，还有塞捕手在野外的浪漫之旅。音频采用混音类，shuffle功能能够随机重新排列所有的符号。同时，游戏中还增加了新的让人惊奇的戴夫魔术，并可以让你有机会赢得比赛，更让你满意的是，可以让玩家自己选择游戏水平等级。

　　你会为贵族科技股份有限公司独一无二的Drag N'DROP技术感到兴奋，可以看到，在新六卷轴游戏中，玩家最爱的就是武士打败妖兽而赢得荣誉。

　　这些老虎机产品还设置有额外奖金游戏。这一系列将推出两个疯狂教授的头衔和法老的奖项。添加了不少的游戏标题种类，在游戏场景中会出现各种兔子，酷鲨，英雄的财富，还有拉斯维加斯的婴儿及红桃皇后，木头杰克等等。

　　贵族科技股份有限公司的展销可以说是很成功的，促进在区域网络的扩展，公司在G2E展销中有一些下载的游戏种类，主要是基于服务器的解决方案和网上赌场技术。

　　At G2E 2011, Aristocrat will show operators how it creates gaming experiences with new game technology, new game categories, entertaining new games, and advances in systems and technologies that, it says, are unlike anything seen before from the company.

　　Aristocrat’s Viridian Hybrid with Active Reel Technology is a new line of games with the potential to feature a 5-reel stepper base game, a full screen video and a three-reel stepper bonus, all in the same game. This is made possible with Aristocrat’s LCD with Reel Windows and electronic Shutters, providing the capability to block individual reels and symbols.

　　The first product category in the Viridian Hybrid line is Winning Wishes, launching with three game titles: Persian Prize, Pixie Riches and Tahitian Treasures.

　　Ultra Spin is a new series of bonus bank games with free games and progressive and/or credit awards bonuses. Ultra Spin will launch with two base game titles: Diamond Destiny and Ruby Saloon. Stormin’ Jackpots are games that contain bonus features and can be configured as either non-progressive or as a standalone progressive.

　　Based on the original popular Cash Express, Cash Express Gold Glass features new Gold Class and multiplier wheel features, high frequency base games, bonus and jackpot features and a five-level progressive.

　　Zorro is back in Zorro: The Legend Returns and in Zorro: The Mask & The Rose. The Tarzan Lord of the Jungle line expands with Tarzan the Adventures Return, with all-new features, and a new Tarzan link.

　　The entertainment-style video slot, Mission: Impossible features a wide area progressive with a $250,000 top award and with three local area progressive jackpots and six pulse-pounding bonus features. The top performing Jaws slot series introduces its fourth title in Jaws Bounty Hunter, with a new chair, which enhances the player’s audio experience.

　　The Mega Pay category is expanded with More Pearls and Rise of the Incas. The standalone progressives line has added new titles Barbershop, Jackpot Catcher and Wild Romance. The Remix category, with its shuffle feature where all symbols are rearranged randomly, has added new titles of Amazing Dave and Touch of Magic, and Win Your Way, where players select their own volatility level, now offers Miss Kitty.

　　Imperial House is back with the added excitement of Aristocrat’s exclusive Drag n’ Drop technology, and player-favorites Wicked Winnings III and Samurai’s Honor are back in new six-reel games.

　　In Reel Tubes, reels also act as tubes that fill to lead players to additional bonus games. This series will launch with two titles in Mad Professor and Pharaoh’s Prize. New in Aristocrat’s Reel Power product offerings are Amazon Temple and Crystal Customs, and Peeking Duck is new to the Xtra Reel Power line up. The Multi-line category has added the titles Bunny’s Rabbits, Cool Shark, Hero’s Fortune, Vegas Baby and Queen of Hearts, and the 50/100 Line category welcomes Timber Jack.

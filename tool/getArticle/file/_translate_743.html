倍受推崇的扑克女玩家―苏西・艾萨克斯

作者: 博之道 来源: 未知 时间: 2011-10-14 阅读: 次 博彩译文

　　苏西·萨克斯一直在拉斯维加斯世界打扑克赢取资本，自1986年以来，多年来在美国比赛中已成为一个强大的扑克选手。

　　萨克斯是扑克界备受推崇的妇女之一，她在一个开放的世界系列扑克中赢得比赛，在2008年世界系列扑克，她肯定还会在她的职业生涯总奖金中添上可观的一笔收入。

　　世界扑克系列

　　苏西·萨克斯在世界扑克系列锦标赛中连续赢出，在1000名妇女参加的1996年和1997年的七张牌梭哈活动中，获得的奖金分别为42 000美元和38 000美元。

　　在共有6名妇女参加的WSOP赛事中，她已经获奖，而截至2007年，萨克斯拥有现场比赛奖金总额超过310 000美元。

　　在1998年，萨克斯声称她在无限额德州扑克WSOP主赛事中有最好成绩，并获得10 000美元的奖金，赢得了第10名，而这只是因为在赌桌上输给了一个人。

　　其他扑克锦标赛

　　苏西·萨克斯已经参加过扑克皇家系列和詹姆斯伍兹VS的匿名炸弹杀手比赛。

　　她也是一个扑克玩家杂志的编者，同时还是两本扑克书籍的作者：《MsPoker》在1999年出版，《1000个最好的扑克策略和秘密》在2006年出版。

　　Susie Isaacs

　　Susie Isaacs has been based in Las Vegas, the poker capital of the world, since 1986, and over the years has become a formidable poker player on the American tournament circuit.

　　Isaacs is one of a number of well-respected women in poker circles, although she is yet to win a World Series of Poker bracelet in an open event, and she will certainly be looking to add to her career earnings at the 2008 World Series of Poker.

　　World Series of Poker

　　Susie Isaacs won consecutive ladies' championships at the World Series of Poker, in the $1 000 Women's Seven-Card Stud Events of 1996 and 1997, earning $42 000 and $38 000 respectively.

　　She has cashed in a total of 6 women's WSOP events, and as of 2007, Isaacs boasts total live tournament winnings of over $310 000.

　　In 1998, Isaacs claimed her best result at the $10 000 No-Limit Hold'em WSOP Main Event, finishing 10th and missing out on the final table by just one position.

　　Other Poker Tournaments

　　Susie Isaacs has been a regular player in the Poker Royale series, The James Woods Gang vs The Unabombers.

　　She is also a regular contributor to Card Player Magazine and is also the author of two poker books: MsPoker: Up Close & Personal, published in 1999, and 1000 Best Poker Strategies and Secrets, published in 2006.

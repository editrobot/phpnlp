普里姆谷赌场度假村推出全新的赌场布局

作者: 来源: 时间: 2011-12-19 阅读: 次 博彩译文

　　普里姆谷赌场度假村正在重新装修，已宣布在圣诞节前夕推出全新的赌场布局。

　　普里姆赌谷场度假村位于拉斯维加斯31900大道，此番装修将增加1000台赌博游戏机，以及扩大其零售面积。普里姆谷赌场度假村此次装修，完全是重新设计赌场布局的。

　　普里姆谷赌场度假村副总裁和总经理助理司徒里奇说，普里姆谷赌场度假村此次暂停营业将花费2.2万美元来重新装修赌场，保证在圣诞节前夕可重新开业。

　　“在过去的几年，我们一直努力提升我们的赌场环境，”里奇说，“我们在两年前就已经开始对赌场度假村进行一些小改造。”

　　最大的变化是什么呢？最常用的洗手间被完全改造一个新的烧烤餐厅。

　　最后里奇说，预计翻新后的普里姆谷赌场度假盈利将在8.5万美元以上。

　　“我们在过去几个星期非常忙碌，目前还没有完全结束。”里奇说。

　　普里姆谷赌场度假预计在12月24日下午4时重新开张。

　　The Primm casino resort, located at 31900 Las Vegas Blvd. south, has added 1,000 new beds throughout the property （now all beds are new）， expanded its retail area and a complete redesign of the casino layout.

　　Primm Valley Casino Resorts Vice President and Assistant General Manager Stuart Richey said Buffalo Bill’s closed down shortly after the Thanksgiving holiday to finish the $2.2 million in renovations so far.

　　“For the past several years we’ve just been trying to upgrade our product,” said Richey. “We started two years ago for small remodels.”

　　Along with 32 new slot machines, the most-used restrooms were completely remodeled and a new barbeque restaurant — Crossroads BBQ, Booze & Blues — will open as well.

　　The last restaurant the resort added, a Denny’s, was a success and one of the most popular of the franchise in the country said Richey. The renovation made in 2010 total more than $8.5 million.

　　The slower holiday season allowed for the major changes that are still underway.

　　“We’ve been busy in the past few weeks but we’re not finished,” Richey said.

　　The resort reopens at 4 p.m. Dec. 24. For more information on the resort visit them online at www.primmvalleyresorts.com.

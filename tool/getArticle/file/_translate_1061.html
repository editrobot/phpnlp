2011年世界老虎机展销之美国博彩公司产品系列（二）

作者: 博之道 来源: 未知 时间: 2011-10-26 阅读: 次 博彩译文

　　美国博彩公司（AGS）是设计者，制造商和运营商的合一，他们拥有目前运行的8000多款的游戏机。此公司在此行业的开始要追溯到1996年，AGS由LED首席执行官罗伯特Miodunski建立，在过去的一年中，成立了一个资深的管理团队，塑造了公司现有的优势，为客户提供一个新的水平服务和产品的选择。

　　这一年在G2E，AGS将引进越野车，这是一种新的游戏机，游戏和技术方面旨通过AGS来创造一个新的水平，并为将来提供一个更好的平台。新的Roadrunner平台设计功能，以改善游戏体验为目标，其中包括每秒300全帧的高速度，每屏的渲染率有多种选择，这在当今业界可以说得上速度是最快的。

　　“Roadrunner”游戏有称为“锁和凹凸”的AGS新的专有功能，这同时增加了玩家玩大基地游戏和频繁进入奖金游戏的机会。这些新游戏的推出，包括今晚在巴黎的以优雅为主题的游戏系列，描绘在巴黎的一个晚上，在一小镇上，以美丽的法国图像为背景，并在游戏中突出金熊猫。这样的游戏主要利用中国的动物象征，有一个可爱的3 - D熊猫字符活跃在游戏中，通过他的滑稽动作，为玩家提供了生活的气息；游戏中还有热带隐蔽地，它允许玩家逃脱到一个热带海滩，那里有迷人的自然美景和高能量的壁虎，在游戏中还会遇到勤奋的寄居蟹和飞舞的蝴蝶。

　　随着新的Roadrunner平台推出，AGS还将在G2E系列推出一个新的样式，包括有机顶盒的功能轮奖金和弹子式动作游戏。AGS将首次在内部开发了十几款新游戏，在AGS展台上还包括约翰尼和黑色天鹅绒AGS游戏，经典酷Catz游戏和皇家游戏和现有玩家最爱的一些游戏：如不羁的球和钻石乐透，这些游戏都具有很强的吸引力，能推动新产品例如第三方游戏的扩展。

　　通过展示一个完整的解决方案的视频游戏法的成功实施，AGS将再次展现了其在伊利诺伊州市场的强势地位。随着伊利诺伊州的奖金俱乐部的忠诚度测验计划实行，AGS也将设计在伊利诺伊州专门提供给当地人玩的多种游戏，包括基础游戏中的樱桃主品牌和各种专有的LED扑克冠军游戏。

　　译文

　　American Gaming Systems （AGS） is a designer, manufacturer and operator of gaming machines with more than 8,000 units currently in operation. With roots in the gaming industry dating back to 1996, Las Vegas-based AGS is led by CEO Robert Miodunski, who has assembled a veteran management team over the past year to build on the company’s existing strengths and to provide customers with a new level of service and product options.

　　This year at G2E, AGS will introduce Roadrunner, its new machine, game and technology initiative designed to take AGS to the next level of innovation and provide a platform for the future. The new Roadrunner platform has a wide variety of features designed to improve the game-play experience, including rendering rates of 300 full frames per second, per screen - the fastest in the industry today, according to the company.

　　Roadrunner’s first family of games all have the new AGS proprietary feature called “Lock and Bump.” This allows players the simultaneous opportunity for big base game wins and more frequent entry to bonus games. These new game releases include Evening in Paris, an elegantly themed game that portrays a night on the town in Paris with beautiful French imagery and backdrops that accentuate the game; Golden Panda, a game that utilizes Chinese symbolism coupled with a cute 3-D panda character who comes alive during game play and gives players the opportunity to live vicariously through his antics; and Tropical Hideaway, which allows players to escape to a tropical beach rich in natural beauty with a high-energy gecko, diligent hermit crabs and fluttering butterflies accentuating the game.

　　Along with the new Roadrunner platform, AGS will also introduce a new series of cabinets at G2E, including top-box games that feature wheel bonuses and pachinko-style action. AGS will debut a dozen new games developed in-house. The AGS booth will also include new third-party games such as Johnny and the Black Velvet Quartet along with attractive product extensions of AGS classics Cool Catz and Royal Reels and existing favorites such as Boogie Ball and Diamond Lotto.

　　AGS will once again emphasize its strong position in the Illinois market by showcasing a complete solution for the successful implementation of the Video Gaming Act. Along with the Illinois Bonus Club loyalty program, AGS will also highlight a variety of game titles designed specifically for locals play in Illinois, including games based on the Cherry Master brand and a variety of poker titles, led by the proprietary Pay It Again Poker game.

2011年世界老虎机产品在拉斯维加斯展销（一）

作者: 博之道 来源: 未知 时间: 2011-10-26 阅读: 次 博彩译文

　　10月4-6 日，在拉斯维加斯的会议中心，第11届G2E将会在这样一个新的场地展开一个新的历史起点。制造商将自己的职责完成得很好，他们让大家都为此感到兴奋，勾起了不少兴趣，特别是因为核心老虎机贸易秀场上出现了比任何其他类别的展会更多的参展商，据说有450多家参展。

　　新的游戏内容，独特的结构设计，生产价值和配套技术产生的诱惑，使得老虎机运营商不约而同聚在这里。而和未来的买家建立起良好的合作关系，这对于老虎机产品的销售经理来说比以往任何时候都更具竞争力。为了帮助你将目光汇聚在此次的焦点上，也可以说是为了让你节省时间而给你一些引导，在这里，我们会给你一些关于一年一度的世界老虎机产品的介绍：

　　Ainsworth对未来的憧憬主要在于A560超顶玩家的天堂白金款上面，它有自动OLED按钮面板，并设有一个万花筒般的独特的头奖品牌游戏，并将推出“世界最高奖金”限量版游戏。

　　安斯沃思宽屏超级A560的展示，有最高的奖金设置，其在行业内尚属首次。它不仅提供不同类型的游戏，同时也有许多不同的中奖主题游戏可供选择，他们会设置一种新的规则让玩家中奖。超级A560的特点是有三个大的液晶屏幕，可以纳入多个递进层次的游戏，同时还设有主题图形和游戏互动的LED照明主机。在与G2E特色主题相联系并逐步成为品牌中心的超级卖场上，世界最高奖金产品库会在一些游戏中提供七个头奖：像雷霆雄鹿队，是一种快速打击的游戏；玩家天堂，是运用灯光相机行动的游戏；格兰德河急流，是与塞区和龙有关的游戏。在2012年以后，最高奖金品牌将会在世界上推出更多。

　　安斯沃思还将推出超级款式，该公司最新的A560内阁主机设备精良，设置有一个32英寸液晶机顶盒和推动LED照明的设备。兆丰顶级超过七英尺，能够提供任何游戏核心楼层。最重要的是，还将推出两个新的超级热门品牌：财富和玩家天堂白金塔，这使得Ainsworth在天堂中奖系列中人气上升。

　　安斯沃思也成功扩大amePlus的范围，有三、四个独立的品牌，包括25个新的独立游戏：豪华双拍，三重射击，连击，超级游戏，100Lines 、50Lines等，这些都是全新的高面额游戏，成为前展厅的热销产品。

　　译文

　　The 11th annual edition of G2E is in a new venue and on a new calendar spot, October 4-6 at the Sands Expo & Convention Center, Las Vegas. Manufacturers will again do their part to generate excitement and interest, particularly in the core slot category which claims more real estate and mind share than any other on a trade show floor that numbers about 450 exhibitors.

　　New game content, cabinets, production values and supporting technologies will tempt even the most budget-minded slot operator to turn on the shopping mode switch. There will be a competitive blend of established and up-and-coming suppliers to keep things vibrant and give slot managers more interesting options than ever. To help focus your attention in the run-up to the show and perhaps save you some time navigating once you’re there, here is our annual World of Slots presentation:

　　Ainsworth’s vision for the future will be showcased beginning with a limited edition of A560 Mega Top Players Paradise Platinum, the dynamic OLED button panel, and “World of Jackpots” featuring a kaleidoscope of unique jackpot brands and games. The company invites you to see “A Galaxy of Options.”

　　Showcased on the widescreen Super A560 cabinet, Ainsworth calls World of Jackpots the first of its kind in the industry, as it not only offers players different game titles, but also many different overall jackpot themes to choose from, providing a new approach to jackpot marketing. The Super A560 cabinet features three large LCD screens to incorporate the game with multiple progressive levels, a host of themed graphics and game interactive LED lighting.

　　The extensive World of Jackpots product library provides super-store quantities of linked progressive-centric brands, with seven jackpot themes featuring at G2E; Thunder Bucks, Rapid Strike, Players Paradise, Lights Camera Action, Rio Grande Rapids, Jackpot Zone and Dragon. Many more new progressive World of Jackpots brands due for release in 2012 and beyond.

　　Ainsworth will also introduce Mega Top, the company’s newest A560 cabinet which stands above all others, with a new 32-inch LCD top box and event driven LED lighting. Standing over seven feet tall with six levels of progressives, the Mega Top range offers an imposing centerpiece for any gaming floor. Most importantly Mega Top launches with two new progressive brands, Tower of Fortune and Players Paradise Platinum, the next extension in Ainsworth’s popular Players Paradise jackpot series.

　　Ainsworth is also set to expand their successful GamePlus range with 25 new standalone games including two-, three- and four-level stand-alone progressive brands: Double Shot Deluxe, Triple Shot, Double Hit, Super Game, Play 100Lines /Play 50Lines an all-new high-denomination game range and the revolutionary ante-bet product Hot Reel.

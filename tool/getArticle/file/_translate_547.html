　　在今年十一月，澳门赌场运营商Sociedade博彩股份有限公司（澳博），赞助的中国国家芭蕾舞团在澳门文化中心演出。

　　首先是11月24日的芭蕾晚会。这是专门为纪念成立50周年的国家芭蕾舞团。二是奥涅金，在11月27日演出。奥涅金的情节的诗句基本上是19世纪俄国作家普希金的小说“叶甫盖尼奥涅金”中的诗句。在澳门演出的版本是由约翰克兰科的赞誉的，按俄罗斯作曲家乐章柴可夫斯基的音乐编排。

　　赌场运营商在一份声明中说：“澳博高兴能够通过中国国家芭蕾舞团精彩的表演，能够起到把城市打造为致力于丰富澳门市民文化生活，同时促进澳门和中国大陆之间的文化交流这样的效果。

　　美国全国广播公司成立于1959年，是中国唯一的一个国家芭蕾舞团。多年来，NBC已经推出包括古典和现代的其优秀芭蕾作品。它的剧目包括西方风格，同时也创造了独具中国特色的芭蕾作品。 NBC培养了大量的知名芭蕾人才，促进舞蹈团体交流与合作。

　　译文

　　Macau casino operator Sociedade de Jogos de Macau （SJM） is sponsoring two performances by the National Ballet of China at the Macau Cultural Centre this November.

　　The first is Ballet Gala on 24th November. It was specially created to commemorate the 50th anniversary of the National Ballet. The second is Onegin, on 27th November. Onegin’s plot is based on the verse novel ‘Eugene Onegin’ by the 19th century Russian writer Alexander Pushkin. The version to be performed in Macau is the acclaimed choreography by John Cranko using music from the Russian composer Pyotr Tchaikovsky.

　　“Once again, SJM is pleased to bring to the city brilliant performances by the National Ballet of China, dedicated to enriching the cultural life of Macau citizens while promoting cultural exchange between Macau and China,” said the casino operator in a statement.

　　The NBC was set up in 1959 and is the only national ballet company in China. Over the years, NBC has introduced its audience to outstanding ballet works, both classical and contemporary. Its repertoire includes pieces from the West, and it has also created a number of ballet works with unique Chinese characteristics. The NBC is also renowned for its success in nurturing ballet talent and in promoting exchange and cooperation with dance groups

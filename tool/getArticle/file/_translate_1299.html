以动物为主题的赌博游戏

作者: 博之道 来源: 未知 时间: 2011-11-02 阅读: 次 博彩译文

　　动物，无论喜欢与否，一直都是赌博世界复杂的组成部分。体育博彩市场的范围很广，包括对动物的投注，最明显的就是赛马，在世界各地每年产生数十亿美元经济效益。赌客下注少量资金，就有赢得巨额现金的机会，如果他们的马有用武之地。投注动物赌博的回报，取决于资金的大小。赛狗也是一个巨大的体育博彩游戏，世界各地的有数以百万计的赛狗爱好者。真是令人难以置信，动物作为人类最好的朋友，无论是马或猎犬，赌徒们喜欢在它们身上下注，创造赚钱的机会。

　　如Paddy Power或博多格等一些在线体育博彩公司都提供世界各地的动物赌博活动。你可以在世界的另一个角落投注澳大利亚或日本的赛马活动。而你需要做的是访问您最喜爱的的体育博彩网站，并享受它给您提供各种动物赌博活动。

　　但体育博彩不是唯一的动物赌博市场。如果你最近在网上赌场上玩游戏，你会发现，网上赌场有数百种以动物为主题的赌博游戏。从鱼类青蛙，狗，海豚和小松鼠，几乎涵盖所有的动物种类。而老虎机游戏大多数以动物为主题。

　　Animals, whether they like it or not, have always been an intricate part of the gambling world.   Sports betting markets include a wide range of betting options on animals, with the most obvious being horse racing, which generates literally billions of dollars around the globe each year.  Punters are able to wager small amounts of money to huge sums of cash, depending on the size of the bankroll, with the chance of great returns if their horse comes in.  In the same vein, greyhound racing is also a huge sport and has an incredible following from around the world.  Man’s best friend, whether a horse or a hound, thus gives gamblers a chance to wager on their favorite sport and make money.

　　When it comes to online gambling, sports betting at sites such as Paddy Power or Bodog are able to offer markets involving animals from all over the world. If you feel like wagering on a horse race in South Africa, Australia and Japan simultaneously, as well as a greyhound race in another corner of the world, all you have to do is access your favorite sports betting site and enjoy what it has to offer.

　　But sports betting is not the only gambling market that allows you to combine your love for animals and gambling.  If you’ve checked out the slot games at online casinos lately, you’ll discover that there are literally hundreds of animal themed games there for the taking.  From fishes to frogs, from dogs to dolphins and from snakes to squirrels, there are slot games that cover practically ever type of animal in the kingdom.

　　Microgaming, for example, has an entire range of animal themed slot games.  If you’re into apes, Monkey’s Money is the game to try out.  Gopher Gold and Track Field Mice cover the rodent population, while Alley Cats is a must for feline friends.

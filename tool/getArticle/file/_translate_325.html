常见的纸牌游戏

作者: 博之道 来源: 未知 时间: 2011-09-16 阅读: 次 博彩译文

　　纸牌游戏一直流行到未来也不会改变这一切,然而如果我们想有多少人喜欢这些游戏，则恰恰相反。

　　最流行的纸牌游戏是:扑克、21点,以及百家乐。这些都是最常见的纸牌游戏，打在每一个陆地和网上赌场。球员们和朋友在一起打这些主场。如果他们不能玩游戏,你只能在赌场像槽为例。

　　纸牌游戏是已知的最令人激动的游戏,许多都需要技能和才华。当然运气扮演重要的角色,但用很好的比赛策略，实际上也能使你自己有获得更多的钱的机会。乐趣、刺激、肾上腺素让纸牌游戏更有吸引力。

　　扑克被认为是大部分人王的纸牌游戏。有很多不同的扑克全世界都玩。这是在一个标准卡甲板上。标准的甲板包含52张牌。。一些扑克变化需要更少的卡片和其他一些额外的卡片,称为“外卡”。爱开玩笑的人被认为是在扑克为“外卡”。甲板上包含四个适合:黑桃、红心,钻石和俱乐部。每件套装包含13个卡片。卡片被排名从最低到最高的为:2、3、4、5、6、7、8、9、10,杰克,国王和王后,高手。在一些扑克变化空间可作为最高的卡片或最低,取决于具体情况。比赛的目的是要做出比对手更高的牌，并采取行动。

　　一些最流行的扑克变化是德州扑克，奥马哈高 - 低、加勒比海、奥马哈扑克,画扑克牌,7卡耳和5卡耳扑克。网上赌场提供他们的客户实践伟大的扑克锦标赛和免费游戏。扑克肯定是最有趣的纸牌游戏。

　　二十一点又称为“21”，由于它的起源在法国，所以它的游戏名叫“Vingt-un”。21点,是在一个52卡甲板上,但现在两个或两个以上的混合包都可以使用。信用卡号码是该卡的价值。法院卡值10,制胜的值会1 - 11,取决于具体情况。适合的事不在二十一点,他们没有价值，21点是在对阵一位银行家。比赛的目标就是多赢的钱比银行家画卡片多，共21分或近21越好。超过21，意味着你被打掉，你失去了锅。21点是一个简单的和好玩的游戏卡。但是，简单的算牌或类似不使用一个很好的策略，就不可以帮助您增加您获胜的机会

　　百家乐，也被称为“蓬银行”， 是迄今为止最迷人的赌场纸牌游戏之一。这是一个为大家准备的游戏。它不需要特殊的技能或复杂的策略,这是一场单纯依赖运气游戏。游戏里面是一个很大的赌注。里面的桌子是经常出现在特别安排在赌场的地方,远离人群和其他赌场的行动。借助8甲板里面卡片,芯片或金钱为赌注和处理鞋。这个游戏的目的是赢得锅的钱在两个或三张牌组合接近9。所有球员都为了有机会赢得锅，这是必须学会纸牌这个游戏是如何计算的。

　　Common card games

　　Card games have always been popular and the future won't change that, quite the opposite if we think about how many people enjoy these games.

　　The most popular card games are: poker, blackjack and baccarat. These are the most common card games played in every land based and online casino. Players have played these games at home among friends; they are not games that you can only play in casinos like slots for example.

　　Card games are known to be the most exciting games; many require both skill and talent. Of course luck plays its part, but with good game strategies you can actually increase your chances of winning more money. Fun, excitement, adrenaline it's what makes card games so attractive to people.

　　Poker is considered by the majority of people as the king of card games. Poker has many variations played all over the world. It is played with a standard card deck. Standard decks contain 52 cards. Some poker variations require less cards and others some extra cards, called "wild cards". Jokers are considered in poker as "wild cards". A deck contains four suits: spades, hearts, diamonds and clubs. Each suit contains 13 cards. The cards are ranked from the lowest to the highest as: 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 7, 8, 9, 10, Jack, Queen, King and Ace. In some poker variations Ace can be used as the highest card or the lowest one, depending on the situation. The objective of the game is to make a stronger hand than your opponents and take the pot.

　　Some of the most popular poker variations are Texas Holdem, Omaha Hi-Low, Caribbean poker, Draw Poker, 7 Card Stud and 5 Card Stud poker. Online Casinos offer their customers great poker tournaments and free games for practice. Poker is for sure the most interesting card game of all.

　　Blackjack is also known as "21" due to its origins in a French game called "Vingt-un". Blackjack is played with a 52 card deck, but nowadays two or more mixed packs can be used. The number on the card is the value of the card. Court cards value is 10, Aces value can be 1 or 11, depending on the situation. Suits do not matter in blackjack, they have no value. Blackjack is played against a banker. The goal of the game is to win more money than the banker by drawing cards and making a total of 21 points or as close to 21 as possible. Going over 21, means you busted and you lose the pot. Blackjack is a simple and fun game played with cards. But simple or not using a good strategy like card counting could help you increase your winning chances.

　　Baccarat, also called "Punto Banco", is by far one of the most glamorous casino card games. It's a game for everyone. It doesn't require special skills or complicated strategies, it's a game based purely on luck. Baccarat is a game with high stakes. The baccarat table is usually found in casinos in specially arranged places, away from the crowds and the other casino action. Baccarat is played with 8 decks of cards, chips or money for bets and a dealing shoe. The object of this game is to win the pot money by making from two or three cards a combination close to nine. All players have to do in order to have a chance to the pot is to learn how the cards are counted in this game.

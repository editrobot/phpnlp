扑克界的女性先锋领袖―Wendeen Eolis

作者: 博之道 来源: 未知 时间: 2011-10-14 阅读: 次 博彩译文

　　多年来， Wendeen Eolis已成为世界扑克和妇女在扑克比赛中的先锋领袖。 Eolis目前是世界扑克协会副主席和国际玩家的世界扑克系列咨询委员会主席。

　　她是Wendeen Eolis公司的董事长，这个公司成立于1969年，而她也就成为商界的重要成员。这包括Eolis国际集团（法律/商业/政府事务顾问）和人们的阅读策略（Eolis成立于2006年的谈判培训咨询）。

　　1974年，Eolis成为纽约（APCNY）人事顾问协会的第一位女总裁。

　　1984年，她创立和担任法律猎头顾问（NALSC）全国协会首任会长。

　　Eolis也是任务部队指挥官希望的冠军，这是在一场灾难辅导计划9 ·11事件后形成的。

　　与此同时，Eolis出版的著作包括纽约法律杂志，妇女的扑克玩家报纸和杂志崖，纽约商业的文章。她还写了一个关于2006年非法互联网赌博执行法案（UIGEA）和国会扭转UIGEA的立法建议的影响。

　　世界扑克系列

　　Wendeen Eolis应景获得了与WSOP相比多于6倍ode钱，并在1986年成为有史以来第一位女性在WSOP主赛事的。

　　Eolis也这样重复做了两次壮举，在1993年作为第一位女性在世界系列赛中连续四年赢得比赛。

　　在1996年，在WSOP发行了纪念芯片，承认她在扑克界的“里程碑”意义，2006年，Eolis被选为WSOP玩家咨询委员会的成员。

　　去年，世界扑克锦标赛专员杰弗里波拉克与Eolis组成“扑克大圣母院”。

　　其他扑克锦标赛

　　Wendeen Eolis在1990年欧洲公开赛无限额德州扑克比赛中获得成功，并已在世界扑克巡回赛和美国扑克冠军赛中再次获胜。

　　在2004年5月，Eolis当选为专业扑克巡回赛的评委。

　　译文

　　Wendeen Eolis

　　For many years, Wendeen Eolis has been a leader in the world of poker and a pioneer for women in poker competitions. Eolis is currently Vice-Chairperson of the World Poker Association and Chair of the International Players Advisory Council of the World Series of Poker.

　　She is a prominent member of the business community in her position as Chairwoman of Wendeen Eolis Enterprises, which she founded in 1969. This includes Eolis International Group （a legal/business/government affairs consultancy） and People Reading Strategies （a negotiation training consultancy founded by Eolis in 2006）。

　　In 1974 Eolis became the first woman president of the Association of Personnel Consultants of New York （APCNY）。

　　In 1984, she founded and served as the first president of the National Association of Legal Search Consultants （NALSC）。

　　Eolis is also Task Force Commander of Hope's Champion, a disaster counselling programme formed following the events of 9/11.

　　Eolis' published writings include articles for the New York Law Journal, Women's Business of New York, Poker Player Newspaper and Bluff Magazine. She has also written a wealth of articles about the impact of the Unlawful Internet Gambling Enforcement Act of 2006 （UIGEA） and subsequent congressional proposals to reverse the UIGEA legislation.

　　World Series of Poker

　　Wendeen Eolis has finished in the money 6 times at the WSOP and in 1986 became the first woman ever to cash in the WSOP Main Event.

　　Eolis was also the first woman to do this twice, repeating the feat in 1993, and has cashed four times in the last 5 years at the World Series.

　　In 1996, the WSOP issued a commemorative chip recognising her "milestone" for women in poker and in 2006, Eolis was named on the WSOP Players Advisory Council.

　　Last year, WSOP Commissioner Jeffrey Pollack dubbed Eolis the "Grand Dame of Poker".

　　Other Poker Tournaments

　　Wendeen Eolis won the 1990 European Open No-Limit Hold'em competition, and has cashed in both the World Poker Tour and the US Poker Championship.

　　In 2004/5 Eolis was elected to the Professional Poker Tour.

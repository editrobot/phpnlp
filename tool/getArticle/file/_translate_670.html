在真钱百家乐中运用的PostePay卡

　　PostePay卡与Visa和Visa Electron卡，都是意大利发明的。这是一个预付费的增值卡，可以购买任何意大利邮政（意大利邮政局），可用于支付，并在意大利和欧洲的其他地方或者网上的任何地方都可以购买。

　　要购买该卡，你不需要是当前银行帐户的所有者。如果你提供一个有效的身份证明文件，如护照或驾驶执照，这就已经足够，你可以在任何一间邮政局购买。

　　PostePay卡持有人可在PostePay网站注册，然后可以查看所有的信用卡交易，用来支付账单，在网上赌场玩与用于手机等充值外，PostePay卡还可在ATM显示机器VISA和Visa Electron上运用。

　　用PostePay玩真钱百家乐

　　虽然百家乐使用被认为是贵族游戏，但是其实网上真钱百家乐是给所有的人提供的，你可以发现其最低投注只要十元。

　　这并不奇怪，因此，意大利自己本身来说就有许多喜爱网上赌场百家乐的爱好者，他们都会在当地邮局购买一个PostePay卡。

　　百家乐和二十一点游戏类似，但它更快，更容易。游戏的目的是让一手牌的点数到达九个点的值，或尽可能接近九个点。A、C、E在百家乐牌面数为“0”，而所有其他的牌数则与其面值一致。加入点时，如果手上牌点的值是两个数字，第一个数字是下降的。例如：一个8和6是4值，而不是14。因此很容易它比在21游戏中更容易获得9的数值。

　　这真钱百家乐是快速和令人兴奋的的比赛，同时是意大利人的最爱，而对欧洲其他地方的人来说则一般。运用PostePay卡来支付时，玩真钱百家乐是安全的。

　　The PostePay Card

　　The PostePay Card is an Italian invention together with VISA and Visa Electron. It is a pre-paid reloadable card purchased at any Poste Italiane （Italian postal office） and can be used to pay and buy anywhere in Italy and Europe and online.

　　To purchase the card you do not need to be a current bank account owner. It is enough if you produce a valid identification document, i.e. passport or driving license, and you may purchase the card at any post office.

　　PostePay card holders may register with the PostePay web site and can then view all card transactions, reload the card, pay bills, play at online casinos, recharge your mobile phone, etc. Furthermore, the PostePay card may be used at ATM machines displaying the VISA and Visa Electron logo.

　　Playing Real Money Baccarat with PostePay

　　Although Baccarat used to be considered the game of the "elite" and of "high rollers", online real money Baccarat is available to all and minimum bets can be found as low as ten dollars.

　　It is not surprising, therefore, to find Italian Baccarat lovers playing at their favorite online casinos and depositing and collecting with a PostePay Card which they purchased at their local post office.

　　Baccarat is similar to Blackjack, but it is faster and easier. The aim of the game is to make a hand of two or three cards with a points value of nine, or as near to nine as possible. In Baccarat face cards count as "0", Ace is one and all other cards count at their face value. When adding up points, if the hand value is two digits, the first digit is dropped. For example: an 8 and 6 has a value of 4, not 14. Thus it is easier to make a nine than it is to make a 21 in Blackjack.

　　This fast and exciting game of real money Baccarat is a favorite with Italians, and Europeans in general, and it is safe and securely played when powered by a PostePay Card.

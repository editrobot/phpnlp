了解百家乐

作者: 博之道 来源: 未知 时间: 2011-11-17 阅读: 次 博彩译文

　　玩百家乐游戏的目的是让赌徒在两个或三个牌卡中的牌点总数尽可能接近9点。

　　两个牌卡的总数为9点是完美的牌卡。第二个最好的手牌，是点数总计为8点。如果前两张牌为八，九，则被认为是一个“例牌”。

　　如果处理例牌，则双方都需要站立起来，荷官宣告例牌获胜，除非出现了平局。

　　击败八点例牌唯一的办法是拥有一个9点例牌。

　　所有的赌注在和局上都会被关闭，除非有人下注平局。

　　牌卡的点值：

　　王牌：1点

　　2：2点

　　3：3点

　　4：4点

　　5：5点

　　6：6点

　　7：7点

　　8：8点

　　9：9点

　　10：点

　　杰克：0点

　　女王：0点

　　王：0点

　　通常情况下，当在手上的两张牌的点数总数为两位数，那么第一个数字是需要舍弃的，而在第二位的数字则成为实际的点值。例如，如果一手处理的牌含有9和5，数字是14，但点值则为4。

　　参与百家乐比赛的玩家分为“庄家”和“玩家”，庄家不一定是一个赌场的雇员，但是通常由下注金额最多的那一位玩家来充当。

　　了解百家乐

　　庄家和玩家同时处理三手牌，每个人获得两张牌，正面朝下。这三手牌，一手牌是发给庄家左边的玩家，一手牌是发给位于庄家右边的玩家，还有一手牌是留给庄家自己的。

　　如果一个玩家投注“庄家”，这意味着他投注的是整个庄家的资金价值，那么他其他的所有赌注都会被自动撤回。

　　百家乐在赌场通常是打8副完整的纸牌。每一次下新的赌注都会重新洗牌。

　　在百家乐游戏，玩家在处理牌卡之前投注，可投注庄家、玩家或和局。

　　牌点值达到或者最接近9点获胜。

　　Understanding Baccarat

　　The purpose to the game of baccarat is to bet on the hand that comes the closest to nine points as possible, in a hand of either two or three cards.

　　The perfect hand would total nine in the first two cards that are dealt. Second best will be the hand that totals eight. Both eight and nine in the first two cards are considered to be a “natural” hand.

　　If a natural hand has been dealt, both hands automatically stand, and the natural hand automatically wins unless there is a tie.

　　He only way to beat a natural eight is a natural nine hand.

　　All bets are called off in the case of a tie, unless someone has bet on a tie.

　　Card Point value

　　Ace 1

　　Two 2

　　Three 3

　　Four 4

　　Five 5

　　Six 6

　　Seven 7

　　Eight 8

　　Nine 9

　　Ten 0

　　Jack 0

　　Queen 0

　　King 0

　　In the case that the numeric total of a hand runs into double digits, the first digit is dropped, and the second digit becomes the hand’s score. For example, if a hand is dealt, containing a 9 and a 5, the numeric total would be 14, but the point value would be 4.

　　The participants are “the banker” and “the player.” The banker is not necessarily a casino employee, but is usually defined as the person with the most money.

　　The banker and the player are each dealt three hands of two cards each, face down. The hands are designated for two players, one to the right and one the left of the banker, plus the banker’s own hand.

　　When a player is betting “banco,” this means that they are betting the entire value of the bank’s funds, and it means that all other bets are automatically withdrawn.

　　If one of the players has a count of less than a natural hand, s/he may stand by saying “non” or take another card, face-up, by saying “carte.” Basically, the player must stand on a score of six or seven, and must draw another card at a score of four or less.

　　Baccarat is usually played with eight complete decks of cards, when played at a casino. Before each new hand is dealt, the bets are placed.

　　In baccarat, before the hands are dealt, the bets are made. Bets can be made on the banker’s hand, the player’s hand, or on a tie.

　　When the bets have been made, the hands are dealt, each receiving two cards. According to the rules, another card is sometimes also drawn.

　　It is the hand, which achieves the score closest to 9 that wins.

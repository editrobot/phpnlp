二十一点培训学校

作者: 博之道 来源: 未知 时间: 2011-11-28 阅读: 次 博彩译文

　　俄亥俄州希望全面建设二十一点培训学校，培养高素质的二十一点荷官，以满足新开赌场的需求。佩里斯堡正在紧锣密鼓地开展二十一点培训课程，为那些希望以赌场荷官为职业生涯的学生提供科学的培训。在当前的形势下，能够成为荷官，将有一个非常有利可图的职业生涯。

　　佩里斯堡的欧文斯社区学院甚至开了一个特殊培训班，上周约四十名学员学习了如何处理纸牌，计数筹码以及如何吸引潜在客户。有些赌场计划在明天春天从这批培训学员中聘请荷官。

　　佩里斯堡一家新开的赌场的桌牌游戏部主任说，当一名二十一点荷官是很有趣的。荷官的职业生涯是令人兴奋。他还表示，赌场招聘的所有的荷官都将来自二十一点培训学校。

　　学生完成他们的培训课程后，该赌场将举办一次面试过程。得到聘任的荷官工薪预计将达16美元至22美元一个小时，还包括一些小费。此薪酬是吸引人们选择荷官为职业的重要原因之一。在当前严峻的就业形势下，这个薪酬标准是比较令人满意的。所以大部分二十一点培训学校的学生都希望能尽被赌场录用。

　　二十一点学校的培训成本只需 200美元，如果学员们能被赌场录用，他们一定会得到比较高的回报价值。

　　译文

　　Ohio students hoping blackjack school pays off The Hollywood Casino in Toledo is in full construction and students are now taking part in special blackjack classes in Perrysburg in the hopes of becoming dealers at the new casino. Classes are in full swing and those who are able to become dealers will have a very lucrative career.

　　The Owens Community College in Perrysburg are holding the special classes and last week around forty people were learning how to deal cards as well as count chips and handle payouts to potential customers. The casino is set to open this coming spring and the classes last five weeks and dealers will be hired from the student list.

　　Neal Perry the director of table games at the new casino commented that being a blackjack dealer can be very exciting. The dealer of the games makes the event exciting. Perry went on to say the most if not all the dealers will come from the dealer school.

　　After the students have finished their training the company will host an audition process. The dealers who are hired are expected to earn from $16 to $22 an hour and that is including tips. The pay is the main draw for many attending the school. Many are unemployed and the pay is good so most are hoping to be hired as soon as the casino opens.

　　The classes cost only $200 to attend so students will definitely get their money’s worth if they are hired.

丽都赌场度假村在地区经济低迷的时期开放

作者: 博之道 来源: 未知 时间: 2011-11-14 阅读: 次 博彩译文

　　在拉斯维加斯最新的度假胜地将在周三晚上开放。这间渡假村开幕期间是处于地方经济低迷的时刻。

　　晚上八时，上百万美元打造的大都会正式开放。它有一个10万平方英尺的赌场，2000个酒店客房和12间餐厅。现在酒店所有的一切都设计得非常漂亮，虽然他们还在完善酒店的最后工作。

　　该度假胜地肯定有着巨额的财产，有着14个餐厅在内，是众多度假胜地的佼佼者之一。这一大都会坐落在8.7亩的土地上。酒店很有看点，那里有一座三层的水晶吊灯，这是由价值两百万的晶体，一幅数字动画和一个巨大的赌场组成。

　　现场娱乐表演是酒店的重点特色，那里有能够展示DJ和供乐队现场表演的舞台。

　　管理跑马灯夜总会的合伙人，贾森斯特劳斯说：“赌场也有一个全世界都会谈论的40英尺，价值300万美元的DJ展台。”他还表示：“他们负责提供技术，内容和经验方面的支持，创造这个镇上从来没有见过的东西。”

　　丽都的许多房间都配有厨房，而百分之七十的房间都有一个令人叹为观止的阳台。同时，你还会发现，那里有三个独立的游泳池。度假村还设有零售店，这是在拉斯维加斯的新品牌，在拉斯维加斯的其他任何地方你都不会找到它们。

　　国际大都会的开设将产生5000就业机会，这将有利于地区经济的发展，因为它使该地区的失业率下降为14.2％，这是全国最低的。分析师Mary琳Palenik说：“该地区的经济复苏可能会落后于全国各地12至18个月。”

　　内华达大学赌博和商业博彩研究学会主任威廉R Eadington说：新的大型赌场时代已经结束了。博彩业的急转直下，很多的投资者都会受到不利影响。

　　Cosmopolitan Opens During Tough Local Economy

　　The newest resort on the Las Vegas strip will open on Wednesday night. This resort is opening at a time when the period is pretty tough for the local economy.

　　At 8:00 p.m., opens the multi-million dollar Cosmopolitan. It has a 100,000 square foot casino, 2,000 hotel rooms and 12 restaurants. Everything about the hotel is pretty much done, although they are working on the final touches on the hotel.

　　The resort definitely has a fun, playful side to the property, with 14 restaurants inside as one of the many standouts. On 8.7 acres of land, sits the property. The hotel has a lot to look at, which includes a three-story chandelier made up of two million crystals, digitally animated columns and a huge casino.

　　Live entertainment is one of the big priorities at the Cosmopolitan, with each bar capable of showcasing DJ’s or live bands.

　　Managing partner of Marquee Nightclub, Jason Strauss said that the casino also have a 40 foot, $3 million DJ booth stage that the whole world will talk about. He also said that they are about to deliver technology, content and experience-wise, something that the town has never seen.

　　Many of the rooms have kitchenettes, while seventy percent of the rooms have balconies, with a breath-taking view. On this property, one will find three separate pools. The resort also has retail stores, which are brand new for Las Vegas, as one will not find them in any other location in Las Vegas.

　　5000 jobs will be generated by the Cosmopolitan, which will help the state as it leads in the nation-leading jobless rate of 14.2%. Analyst Mary Lynn Palenik said that the state’s recovery would likely lag 12 to 18 months behind the rest of the nation.

　　Director of the Institute for the Study of Gambling and Commercial Gaming at the University of Nevada, William R. Eadington said that era of new mega-casinos is over. By the gaming industry’s nosedive, too many investors were adversely affected.

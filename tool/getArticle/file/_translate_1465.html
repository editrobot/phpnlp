亚洲的赌博地标 - 澳门威尼斯人

作者: 博之道 来源: 未知 时间: 2011-11-08 阅读: 次 博彩译文

　　如果您计划前往澳门澳门，你绝对不能错过澳门威尼斯人酒店，一个在亚洲赌博具有里程碑意义的赌博度假村酒店 。此度假酒店属于拉斯维加斯金沙集团，它不仅是一个超级赌场，还是一个超级娱乐场，不仅是赌徒的最好去处，也是家庭集体旅游的最好去处。该酒店的赌场空间有546,000平方尺，大到足以容纳九十架747大型喷气式客机，还有数量多达3000的赌博游戏机。澳门威尼斯人酒店还有3000间酒店客房，能容纳大量赌客。如果钱不是一个问题，为什么不在威尼斯人酒店预定客房？澳门威尼斯人酒店客房是绝对豪华的CIELO套房，包括水疗室，餐厅设施，按摩浴缸，宝格丽设施和等离子电视。此外，您还能享受从机场到酒店免费的豪华轿车接送。

　　澳门威尼斯人度假村的赌场是绝对值得一游的，该赌场有四个不同的赌博领域，被称为帝国大厦，红龙，凤凰和金鱼。如果你是一个贵宾客户，你可以享受贵宾的专用游戏俱乐部。

　　在澳门威尼斯人酒店，从未有一个沉闷的时刻，因为该酒店提供很多丰富有趣的表演，而且它还有30个不同的餐厅和酒吧供您选择，包含所有类型的菜肴，从日本到中国，从东部到西部。

　　家庭旅游在澳门威尼斯人是很受欢迎的，它提供大量儿童活动，还有众多的泳池，高尔夫球场及其他游乐设施。

　　译文：

　　If you’ve ever had the honor of visiting Macau, you definitely could not have missed one of Asia’s gambling landmark’s – the Venetian Macau. This resort hotel is controlled by the Las Vegas Sands and was the first uber-casino to grace the Cotoi Strip in the enclave. The casino itself is a massive 546,000 square feet – large enough to hold NINETY 747 jumbo jets, and boasts a whopping 3,000 gambling machines.

　　Gamblers travel from near and far to enjoy the amenities of the Venetian Macau, and there are 3,000 hotel rooms to accommodate the masses. All guests are accommodated on floors 7 to 38.  If money isn’t an issue, why not book the Cielo Suite at the Venetian Hotel, which affords you 3,830 feet of absolute luxury? This royal retreat includes a spa room, dining room facilities, a jacuzzi, Bvlgari amenities and plasma TVs.  In addition, you’ll be pampered with a limousine transfer to the hotel on arriving at the airport, exclusive check in, complimentary floral arrangements and an espresso coffee maker.

　　The casino itself is definitely worth a visit – even if you can’t afford to actually stay at this resort. There are four distinct gambling areas, known as the Imperial House, the Red Dragon, the Phoenix and the Golden Fish.  If you’re a premium guest, you will be upgraded to the Praiza Club, an exclusive gaming club for VIP’s, which offers members their own lobby and reception, as well as private rooms.

　　There is never a dull moment at the Venetian, thanks to the huge array of international shops, plenty of street performers to keep you enthralled and even the opportunity to take a ride in a real gondola to fit in the with the Italian theme. There are a staggering 30 different restaurants and bars to choose from, which serve all types of dishes, ranging from Japanese to Chinese, international and western.

　　Families are welcome at the Venetian, and there are plenty of activities for children. From a multitude of swimming pools, to mini golf courses and other rides.

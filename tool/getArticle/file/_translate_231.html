　　赌场游戏指南 - 扑克类型

　　扑克游戏在美国和欧洲是很受欢迎的。 “扑克”一词是指一系列的纸牌游戏，涉及玩家们最好的牌手可能出有许多类型的扑克游戏，其中最流行的是：

　　德州Hold'Em

　　奥马哈

　　奥马哈高 - 低

　　加勒比海扑克

　　和七张牌梭哈。

　　其他常见的扑克正在播放世界各地的赌场今天的版本，包括直扑克，这是最古老的形式被称为扑克，扑克，绘制扑克和社区扑克，这是一个扑克的变化。

　　虽然这些游戏彼此各不相同，大多数扑克游戏的基本原则和背后的投注规则保持不变。他们也遵循相同的手排名。

　　每场比赛本身区别于其他的方式，包括如何处理各有关游戏卡，在一只手就可以形成的，手高或低是否决定谁胜的方式不同。每场比赛也有它自己的规则，每场比赛允许多少轮投注。

　　最初的金额，在大多数扑克游戏的今天，是预先固定。游戏向前以顺时针运动，每个球员必须尽可能多的钱作为预固定数额或以前的赌注下注，或折叠轮拉。

　　球员有另一种选择，是提高赌注，而不是只把更多的钱匹配前面的金额。当所有的球员都轮到他们来匹配，提高倍投注，投注结束。根据正在播放的是哪个版本的扑克，高或低手的玩家能够胜出。但有一个例外，在某些情况下，如奥马哈高低，奖金，两者之间的分裂。这样玩家就能获胜。

　　Casino Games guides - Type of POKER

　　Poker is very popular game in America and Europe. The term “Poker” refers to a range of card games that involve players making the best hand possible out of the cards they are dealt. There are many types of Poker games, and the most popular among them are:

　　Texas Hold ‘Em

　　Omaha

　　Omaha High-Low

　　Caribbean Poker

　　and Seven Card Stud.

　　Other common versions of Poker being played in casinos all over the world today include Straight Poker, which is the oldest known form of Poker, Stud Poker, Draw Poker and Community Poker, which is a variation of Stud Poker.

　　Although these games vary from each other, the basic principles and betting rules behind most poker games remains the same. They also follow the same hand rankings.

　　The ways each game distinguishes itself from the other include how the cards are dealt for each respective game, the different ways in which a hand can be formed and whether the high hand or the low one determines who wins. Each game also has its own rules as to how many rounds of betting are allowed per game.

　　The initial amount, in most poker games today, is pre-fixed. The game moves forward in a clockwise motion, and each player must either wager as much money as the pre-fixed amount or the previous bet made or pull out of the round by folding.

　　Another option players have, is to raise the stakes and not only match the previous amount but put in even more money. When all the players have had their turn to match, raise of fold their bets, the betting ends. Depending on which version of Poker is being played, the player with the high or low hand can win. In some cases, such as with Omaha High-Low, the winnings are split between the two. If all the players fold, with one exception, that player wins.

西佛吉尼亚州赌场或面临更多赌博竞争

作者: 艳艳 来源: 时间: 2012-08-16 阅读: 次 博彩译文

　　西佛吉尼亚州的赌场星期三在马里兰州签署了一份清淡，其可能面临更多的赌博竞争。

　　马里兰州州长画没有浪费时间在签署立法妥协，自2008年以来，在他的国家这是最大的赌博扩张。

　　法律建立了一个程序，让选民决定在马里兰州国家指定的赌博地点，是否可能将其变成赌场赌博。

　　其中的一个指定地点是岩石峡度假村，是在摩根沿68号州际公路80英里以东的地方。

　　2008年马里兰州的选民赞成视频老虎机，但那时候西佛吉尼亚州，宾夕法尼亚和特拉华都却倾向于支持桌上游戏。

　　州长奥马利说，他会支持在国家海港附近的华盛顿哥伦比亚特区弄一个赌场的计划。这样一个赌场，对于在西佛吉尼亚州查尔斯镇表现最好的赌场来说，可能会产生不小的竞争。

　　西佛吉尼亚州赌博场所，特别是在北部狭长，被宾夕法尼亚和俄亥俄州的竞争沉重打击了。在匹兹堡和克利夫兰地区，有新的赌场。

　　译文：

　　West Virginia's casinos could face even more gambling competition in a bill signed into law Wednesday in Maryland.

　　Maryland Gov. Martin O'Malley wasted little time in signing a legislative compromise that would represent the largest gambling expansion in his state since 2008.

　　The law sets up a process that will allow voters in Maryland to decide whether the state's designated gambling locations could turn into casinos.

　　One of those designated locations is Rocky Gap Resort, which is 80 miles east of Morgantown along Interstate 68.

　　Maryland voters approved video slot machines in 2008 but since then West Virginia, Pennsylvania and Delaware have all gone to table games.

　　Gov. O'Malley says he'll be supporting plans for a casino at National Harbor waterfront near Washington, D.C. Such a casino would likely provide competition for West Virginia's best-performing casino in Charles Town.

　　West Virginia gambling locations, particularly in the northern panhandle, have been hit hard by competition from Pennsylvania and Ohio. There are new casinos in the Pittsburgh and Cleveland areas.

加拿大扑克玩家集体诉讼行动

作者: 博之道 来源: 未知 时间: 2011-09-27 阅读: 次 博彩资讯

    加拿大已成为扑克玩家集体诉讼最频繁的国家。

　　杰夫Orenstein，身为消费者律师集团的律师，在魁北克省高等法院提出诉讼。

　　根据联络小组网站的一份声明，扑克玩家集体起诉对方非法冻结他们的网上帐户。

　　Orenstein估计，可能有高达10万美元的加拿大资金仍然被加拿大司法部冻结。

　　目前在加拿大在线扑克本身没有任何限制，因为加拿大这起集体诉讼行为，来自世界各地的玩家也一样陷入了混乱。

　　Alderney赌博控制委员会（海湾合作委员会）的下达命令，该网站在六月下旬暂停运营。

　　听证会这个星期正在进行关于恢复或永久吊销其许可证的讨论，被非法冻结的资金要估计要过一段时间后才能被解冻。

　　有报告显示，该网站已经处在严峻的财务状况之中。

　　Orenstein列出下列名单，并提起诉讼：

　　Tiltware有限责任公司，掌上国王有限公司，FILCO有限公司，兰森有限公司，邮件传媒有限公司，业主雷Bitar和尼尔森Burtnick和一些Full Tilt团队，其中包括霍华德莱德勒，克里斯弗格森，菲尔艾维，安迪布洛赫，埃里克成员林格伦，珍妮哈曼，艾伦坎宁安，埃里克赛德尔和迈克马图索。

　　请记住，为了避免未来风险，请选择安全的网站。

　　Full Tilt Cops Canadian Class Action

　　by ryan | Published: Tuesday, September 13th, 2011

　　Canadian poker players have become the latest group to launch an attack on Full Tilt Poker with a class action lawsuit filed earlier this week.

　　Jeff Orenstein, attorney for the Consumer Law Group, submitted the suit in the Superior Court of Quebec.

　　He represents an unspecified number of Canadian players who funds remain inaccessible following the websites closure on lack Friday

　　According to the CLG website a statement reads, he class action lawsuit involves Full Tilt Poker illegal freezing of customers money that is kept in their internet poker player accounts.

　　In estimates made by Orenstein there could be as much as $10 million worth of Canadian funds still tied up by the U.S. Department of Justice freezing of Full Tilt accounts.

　　In Canada itself there are currently no restrictions on online poker however because Full Tilt was based in the USA they, like players from all over the world, were also caught up in the fiasco.

　　The website was licensed under the Alderney Gambling Control Commission （AGCC） with that approval being suspended in late June.

　　A hearing will get underway this week in regards to reinstating or permanently revoking their license however even if Full Tilt were to re-surface it still highly unlikely funds would be immediately available.

　　Reports have been flooding in about the dire financial position the website was in even before black Friday and after paying out hundreds of thousands of dollars in overdue fees to the AGCC that has only got worse.

　　The following names are listed on the lawsuit filed  by Orenstein:

　　Tiltware LLC, Pocket Kings Ltd., Filco Ltd., Ranson Ltd., Mail Media Ltd., owners Ray Bitar and Nelson Burtnick and some members of Team Full Tilt, which includes Howard Lederer, Chris Ferguson, Phil Ivey, Andy Bloch, Erick Lindgren, Jennifer Harman, Allen Cunningham, Erik Seidel and Mike Matusow.

　　This isn the first time a class action has been fired towards Full Tilt with the Southern District of New York fielding a case on behalf of American players.

　　That being said the attorneys of record for that case, Jeff Ifrah and David, later withdrew it on the basis that, ontinued representation of [the] defendants would create unreasonable financial burden to our law firm.

　　Latest estimates of Full Tilt total bill to players is that the number could be as high as $150 million with very little capacity to pay any of it back.

　　Fellow website Pokerstars which was caught up in black Friday as well had similar debts but was able to honour players cash out requests.

　　Remember that to avoid all this into the future it best to stick with a website you know is safe and secure.
